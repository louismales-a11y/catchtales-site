Fishing in Northern Delaware

Brandywine Creek, White Clay Creek, Lums Pond, Newark area, and the freshwater fishing of northern Delaware.

1. Lums Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lums Pond State Park, New Castle County

Lums Pond is the largest freshwater pond in Delaware at 200 acres, offering excellent largemouth bass, crappie, and bluegill fishing in a state park setting.

2. Brandywine Creek (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Rainbow Trout, Sunfish)

Brandywine Creek, New Castle County

Brandywine Creek offers good smallmouth bass and stocked trout fishing through the Brandywine Valley.

3. White Clay Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

White Clay Creek, New Castle County

White Clay Creek offers excellent trout fishing in the White Clay Creek Preserve.

4. Delaware River (Northern Reach) (Striped Bass, White Perch, Channel Catfish, Smallmouth Bass)

Delaware River, New Castle County

The Delaware River along northern Delaware offers excellent striped bass and white perch fishing.

5. Hoopes Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Chain Pickerel)

Hoopes Reservoir, New Castle County

Hoopes Reservoir offers good bass and panfish fishing near Wilmington.

6. Brandywine River (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Sunfish)

Brandywine River, Wilmington area

The Brandywine River offers good warm-water fishing through Wilmington.

7. Alapocas Run (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Alapocas Run, Wilmington

Alapocas Run offers stocked trout fishing in Wilmington's Alapocas State Park.

8. Bellevue State Park Pond (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Sunfish)

Bellevue State Park, Wilmington

Bellevue Park pond offers good family fishing near Wilmington.

9. Carousel Park Pond (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Carousel Park, New Castle County

Carousel Park pond offers stocked trout and bass fishing.

10. Glasgow Park Pond (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Bluegill)

Glasgow Park, New Castle County

Glasgow Park pond offers good family fishing.

11. Killens Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Killens Pond State Park, Kent County

Killens Pond offers excellent bass and panfish fishing in central Delaware.

12. Silver Lake (Dover) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Silver Lake, Dover

Silver Lake offers good warm-water fishing in the capital city.

13. Wyoming Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Chain Pickerel)

Wyoming Lake, Kent County

Wyoming Lake offers good bass and panfish fishing near Dover.

14. Courtland Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Sunfish)

Courtland Lake, New Castle County

Courtland Lake offers good fishing near Newark.

15. Pike Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Pike Creek, New Castle County

Pike Creek offers stocked trout fishing in northern Delaware.

16. Brandywine Creek State Park (Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Brandywine Creek, New Castle County

Brandywine Creek offers good fishing in a state park.

17. White Clay Creek Preserve (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

White Clay Creek, New Castle County

White Clay Creek offers excellent trout fishing.

18. Lum's Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lum's Pond, New Castle County

Lum's Pond offers excellent fishing.

19. C&D Canal (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish)

C&D Canal, New Castle County

The C&D Canal offers good fishing.

20. Augustine Creek (Largemouth Bass, White Perch)

Augustine Creek, New Castle County

Augustine Creek offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnrec.delaware.gov. Non-resident options available.
  • Fish Lums Pond -- Lums Pond is Delaware's premier freshwater fishing lake with good shore access.
  • Try Brandywine Creek -- Brandywine Creek offers year-round fishing for smallmouth bass and stocked trout.
  • Spring trout stocking -- Delaware stocks trout in northern streams each spring. April is peak season.
  • Urban fishing -- Wilmington parks offer convenient family fishing opportunities.
