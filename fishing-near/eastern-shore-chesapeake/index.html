Fishing on the Eastern Shore & Chesapeake Bay

Chesapeake Bay, Choptank River, Nanticoke River, Pocomoke River, and the world-class fishing of Maryland's Eastern Shore.

1. Chesapeake Bay (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Speckled Trout, White Perch, Croaker, Spot)

Chesapeake Bay, Eastern Shore

The Chesapeake Bay is the largest estuary in the US, offering world-class striped bass (rockfish) fishing and diverse saltwater species.

2. Choptank River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Blue Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Choptank River, Talbot/Caroline counties

The Choptank River offers excellent striped bass and blue catfish fishing on the Eastern Shore.

3. Nanticoke River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Blue Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Nanticoke River, Dorchester/Wicomico counties

The Nanticoke River offers good striped bass and catfish fishing on the lower Eastern Shore.

4. Pocomoke River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch)

Pocomoke River, Worcester County

The Pocomoke River is a unique blackwater river offering excellent bass and crappie fishing in a cypress swamp setting.

5. Wicomico River (Eastern Shore) (Striped Bass, White Perch, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Wicomico River, Wicomico County

The Wicomico River offers good striped bass and white perch fishing near Salisbury.

6. Chester River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Blue Catfish, Largemouth Bass)

Chester River, Queen Anne's/Kent counties

The Chester River offers excellent striped bass fishing on the upper Eastern Shore.

7. Sassafras River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Blue Catfish, Largemouth Bass)

Sassafras River, Kent/Cecil counties

The Sassafras River offers good fishing on the upper Eastern Shore.

8. Elk River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish, Largemouth Bass)

Elk River, Cecil County

The Elk River offers good fishing at the head of the Chesapeake Bay.

9. Fishing Bay (Striped Bass, Speckled Trout, White Perch, Bluefish, Croaker)

Fishing Bay, Dorchester County

Fishing Bay offers excellent fishing on the lower Eastern Shore.

10. Hooper Strait (Striped Bass, Speckled Trout, Bluefish, Croaker)

Hooper Strait, Dorchester County

Hooper Strait offers good fishing between the Chesapeake Bay and Tangier Sound.

11. Tangier Sound (Striped Bass, Speckled Trout, Bluefish, Croaker, Spot)

Tangier Sound, Somerset/Dorchester counties

Tangier Sound offers excellent fishing on the Eastern Shore side of the Chesapeake.

12. Pocomoke Sound (Striped Bass, Speckled Trout, White Perch, Bluefish)

Pocomoke Sound, Worcester County

Pocomoke Sound offers good fishing on the Virginia border.

13. Assawoman Bay (Speckled Trout, Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder)

Assawoman Bay, Worcester County

Assawoman Bay offers good fishing near Ocean City.

14. Isle of Wight Bay (Speckled Trout, Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder)

Isle of Wight Bay, Worcester County

Isle of Wight Bay offers good fishing near Ocean City.

15. Sinepuxent Bay (Speckled Trout, Striped Bass, Flounder, Blue Crab)

Sinepuxent Bay, Worcester County

Sinepuxent Bay offers good fishing near Assateague Island.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnrec.maryland.gov. Chesapeake Bay and coastal licences required.
  • Fish the Chesapeake Bay -- The Chesapeake offers world-class striped bass fishing. Spring and fall are peak.
  • Try the Choptank River -- The Choptank offers excellent fishing for striped bass and blue catfish.
  • Eastern Shore bass -- The Pocomoke River offers excellent largemouth bass fishing in a unique blackwater setting.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-November offer the best fishing.
