Fishing in Central Georgia

Lake Sinclair, Lake Oconee, Ocmulgee River, and the heart of Georgia's reservoir fishing.

1. Lake Sinclair (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Lake Sinclair, Baldwin/Putnam counties

Lake Sinclair is a 15,330-acre reservoir on the Oconee River offering excellent largemouth bass and crappie fishing near Milledgeville.

2. Lake Oconee (Largemouth Bass, Striped Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Lake Oconee, Greene/Putnam counties

Lake Oconee is a 19,000-acre reservoir on the Oconee River offering excellent bass, striped bass, and crappie fishing in a popular retirement and resort area.

3. Oconee River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Oconee River, central Georgia

The Oconee River offers good fishing between Lake Sinclair and Lake Oconee.

4. Ocmulgee River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Ocmulgee River, central Georgia

The Ocmulgee River offers good fishing below Jackson Lake through Macon.

5. Lake Tobesofkee (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Lake Tobesofkee, Bibb County

Lake Tobesofkee is a 2,500-acre reservoir offering good fishing near Macon.

6. Juliette Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Juliette Lake, Monroe County

Juliette Lake offers good fishing near the town of Juliette, known for the movie "Fried Green Tomatoes."

7. Lake Blackshear (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Lake Blackshear, Crisp/Dooly counties

Lake Blackshear is a 8,700-acre reservoir on the Flint River offering excellent bass and crappie fishing.

8. Flint River (Upper) (Largemouth Bass, Shoal Bass, Catfish)

Upper Flint River, central Georgia

The Flint River offers good fishing for shoal bass and largemouth bass.

9. Dooly County Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bream, Crappie)

Dooly County, central Georgia

Dooly County Lake offers good family fishing.

10. Houston County Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bream, Crappie)

Houston County, central Georgia

Houston County Lake offers good fishing near Perry.

11. Echeconnee Creek (Largemouth Bass, Bream, Catfish)

Echeconnee Creek, Bibb County

Echeconnee Creek offers good fishing near Macon.

12. Big Indian Creek (Largemouth Bass, Bream)

Big Indian Creek, Monroe County

Big Indian Creek offers good fishing.

13. Commissioner Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bream, Crappie)

Commissioner Lake, Macon area

Commissioner Lake offers good fishing near Macon.

14. Lake Russell (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Russell, Hart County

Lake Russell offers good fishing on the Savannah River arm of Lake Hartwell.

15. Clarks Hill Lake (Largemouth Bass, Striped Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Clarks Hill Lake (J. Strom Thurmond), Columbia County

Clarks Hill Lake is a 71,000-acre reservoir on the Savannah River offering world-class striped bass and largemouth bass fishing.

16. Lake Varner (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream)

Lake Varner, Newton County

Lake Varner offers good fishing.

17. Lake Juliette (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Juliette, Monroe County

Lake Juliette offers good fishing.

18. Rum Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Rum Lake, Bibb County

Rum Lake offers good fishing near Macon.

19. Lake Tobesofkee (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream)

Lake Tobesofkee, Bibb County

Lake Tobesofkee offers good fishing.

20. Echeconnee Creek (Largemouth Bass, Bream)

Echeconnee Creek, Bibb County

Echeconnee Creek offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at georgiawildlife.com.
  • Fish Lake Oconee for bass -- Lake Oconee offers excellent bass fishing year-round. Spring and fall are peak.
  • Try Lake Sinclair for crappie -- Lake Sinclair is known for excellent crappie fishing, particularly in spring.
  • Explore the Ocmulgee -- The Ocmulgee River offers good fishing through Macon with several public access points.
  • Striped bass on Clarks Hill -- Clarks Hill Lake is one of the best striped bass lakes in the country.
