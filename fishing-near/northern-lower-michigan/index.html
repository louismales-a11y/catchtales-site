Fishing in the Northern Lower Peninsula

Au Sable River, Manistee River, Torch Lake, Grand Traverse Bay, and the world-class fishing of northern Michigan.

1. Au Sable River (Brown Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye)

Au Sable River, Crawford/Oscoda counties

The Au Sable River is one of the most famous trout streams in America, offering world-class fly fishing for brown and rainbow trout.

2. Manistee River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Smallmouth Bass)

Manistee River, Wexford/Manistee counties

The Manistee River offers excellent trout, steelhead, and salmon fishing in a beautiful river valley.

3. Torch Lake (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Torch Lake, Antrim County

Torch Lake is the largest natural inland lake in the Lower Peninsula at 18,770 acres, offering excellent lake trout and smallmouth bass fishing.

4. Grand Traverse Bay (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Whitefish)

Grand Traverse Bay, Grand Traverse/Leelanau counties

Grand Traverse Bay offers world-class salmon and trout fishing on Lake Michigan, with excellent charter fishing from Traverse City.

5. Burt Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Burt Lake, Cheboygan County

Burt Lake offers excellent walleye and pike fishing in the northern Lower Peninsula.

6. Mullett Lake (Lake Trout, Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Mullett Lake, Cheboygan County

Mullett Lake offers excellent lake trout and walleye fishing near the Straits of Mackinac.

7. Higgins Lake (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Higgins Lake, Roscommon County

Higgins Lake is a 9,700-acre lake offering excellent lake trout and smallmouth bass fishing in north-central Michigan.

8. Lake Charlevoix (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye)

Lake Charlevoix, Charlevoix County

Lake Charlevoix is a 17,200-acre lake offering excellent lake trout and salmon fishing connected to Lake Michigan.

9. Elk River Chain of Lakes (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Elk River Chain, Antrim County

The Elk River Chain of Lakes offers excellent warm-water fishing in a connected lake system.

10. Lake Bellaire (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Largemouth Bass)

Lake Bellaire, Antrim County

Lake Bellaire offers good fishing in the Elk River Chain of Lakes.

11. Clam Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Clam Lake, Antrim County

Clam Lake offers good fishing in the Elk River Chain.

12. Intermediate Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Intermediate Lake, Antrim County

Intermediate Lake offers good fishing in the Elk River Chain.

13. Big Twin Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Big Twin Lake, Antrim County

Big Twin Lake offers good fishing near Bellaire.

14. Lake Margrethe (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Lake Margrethe, Crawford County

Lake Margrethe offers good fishing near Grayling.

15. Fletcher Pond (Northern Pike, Walleye, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Fletcher Pond, Alpena/Montmorency counties

Fletcher Pond is a large impoundment offering excellent pike and walleye fishing.

16. Long Lake (Grand Traverse) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Long Lake, Grand Traverse County

Long Lake offers good fishing near Traverse City.

17. Boardman River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Steelhead)

Boardman River, Grand Traverse County

The Boardman River offers excellent trout and steelhead fishing flowing into Grand Traverse Bay.

18. Betsie River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon)

Betsie River, Benzie County

The Betsie River offers excellent steelhead and salmon fishing near Frankfort.

19. Platte River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon)

Platte River, Benzie County

The Platte River offers excellent salmon and steelhead fishing at the Sleeping Bear Dunes area.

20. Crystal Lake (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Crystal Lake, Benzie County

Crystal Lake offers excellent lake trout and smallmouth bass fishing near Frankfort.

21. Glen Lake (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Glen Lake, Leelanau County

Glen Lake offers good fishing near Sleeping Bear Dunes.

22. Lake Leelanau (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Lake Leelanau, Leelanau County

Lake Leelanau offers good fishing in the Leelanau Peninsula.

23. North Lake Leelanau (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

North Lake Leelanau, Leelanau County

North Lake Leelanau offers good fishing.

24. Little Traverse Bay (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon)

Little Traverse Bay, Emmet County

Little Traverse Bay offers excellent salmon and trout fishing near Petoskey and Harbor Springs.

25. Crooked Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Crooked Lake, Emmet County

Crooked Lake offers good fishing near Petoskey.

26. Pickerel Lake (Emmet) (Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Pickerel Lake, Emmet County

Pickerel Lake offers good fishing near Petoskey.

27. Walloon Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Largemouth Bass)

Walloon Lake, Emmet County

Walloon Lake offers excellent fishing near Petoskey.

28. Cheboygan River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Muskie, Lake Trout)

Cheboygan River, Cheboygan County

The Cheboygan River connects Mullett Lake to Lake Huron and offers excellent walleye and muskie fishing.

29. Black River (Cheboygan) (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon)

Black River, Cheboygan County

The Black River offers excellent steelhead and salmon fishing near the Straits of Mackinac.

30. Ocqueoc River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon)

Ocqueoc River, Presque Isle County

The Ocqueoc River offers excellent trout and steelhead fishing in the northern Lower Peninsula.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at Michigan.gov/DNR.
  • Fish the Au Sable -- The Au Sable is one of America's premier trout streams with world-class fly fishing.
  • Try Grand Traverse Bay -- Grand Traverse Bay offers excellent charter fishing for salmon and trout.
  • Torch Lake -- Torch Lake is Michigan's largest inland lake in the Lower Peninsula with excellent lake trout fishing.
  • Spring and fall -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in northern Michigan.
