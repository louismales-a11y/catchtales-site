Fishing in Central Kansas

Wilson Lake, Kanopolis Lake, Marion Reservoir, McPherson State Fishing Lakes, and the reservoirs of central Kansas.

1. Wilson Lake (Walleye, Wiper, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass)

Wilson Lake, Russell/Lincoln counties

Wilson Lake is a 9,000-acre reservoir offering excellent walleye and wiper fishing in the Smoky Hills region.

2. Kanopolis Lake (Wiper, Walleye, Crappie, Channel Catfish, Largemouth Bass, White Bass)

Kanopolis Lake, Ellsworth County

Kanopolis Lake is a 3,500-acre reservoir offering good wiper and walleye fishing.

3. Marion Reservoir (Wiper, Walleye, Crappie, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish, White Bass)

Marion Reservoir, Marion County

Marion Reservoir is a 6,200-acre reservoir offering excellent wiper and walleye fishing.

4. McPherson State Fishing Lakes (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Walleye)

McPherson SFLs, McPherson County

The McPherson State Fishing Lakes offer good fishing in central Kansas.

5. Lake Afton (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Lake Afton, Sedgwick County

Lake Afton offers stocked trout fishing in winter and bass fishing year-round near Wichita.

6. Harvey County East Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Harvey County East Lake, Harvey County

Harvey County East Lake offers good fishing near Newton.

7. Harvey County West Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Harvey County West Lake, Harvey County

Harvey County West Lake offers good fishing.

8. Lake Roberts (KS) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Roberts, Harvey County

Lake Roberts offers good fishing near Hesston.

9. Sand Hills State Park Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Sand Hills SP, Reno County

Sand Hills State Park offers good fishing near Hutchinson.

10. Rice County State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Rice County SFL, Rice County

Rice County SFL offers good fishing near Lyons.

11. Barton State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Barton SFL, Barton County

12. Stafford State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Stafford SFL, Stafford County

Stafford SFL offers good fishing near St. John.

13. Pawnee State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Pawnee SFL, Pawnee County

Pawnee SFL offers good fishing near Larned.

14. Edwards State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Edwards SFL, Edwards County

Edwards SFL offers good fishing near Kinsley.

15. Pratt County Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Pratt County Lake, Pratt County

Pratt County Lake offers good fishing near Pratt.

16. Kingman State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Kingman SFL, Kingman County

Kingman SFL offers good fishing near Kingman.

Cheney Reservoir, Reno/Kingman counties

Cheney Reservoir is a 9,500-acre lake offering excellent wiper and walleye fishing near Wichita.

23. Mined Land Lakes (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at ksoutdoors.com.
  • Fish Wilson Lake -- Wilson Lake is one of Kansas' top walleye and wiper lakes.
  • Try Kanopolis -- Kanopolis Lake offers excellent wiper fishing in the Smoky Hills.
  • Winter trout -- Several central Kansas lakes are stocked with rainbow trout in winter.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing.
