Fishing in Central Arkansas

Greers Ferry Lake, Lake Conway, Arkansas River, Little Rock area, and the heart of Arkansas fishing.

1. Greers Ferry Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Crappie, Catfish)

Greers Ferry Lake, Cleburne County

Greers Ferry Lake is a 40,000-acre reservoir on the Little Red River offering excellent largemouth bass, walleye, and crappie fishing.

2. Little Red River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Little Red River, below Greers Ferry Dam

The Little Red River offers excellent rainbow and brown trout fishing in its cold tailwaters below Greers Ferry Dam.

3. Lake Conway (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream, Catfish)

Lake Conway, Faulkner County

Lake Conway is a 6,700-acre lake created by the Arkansas Game and Fish Commission offering excellent crappie, bass, and bream fishing.

4. Arkansas River (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, White Bass, Catfish, Crappie)

Arkansas River, central Arkansas

The Arkansas River through central Arkansas offers excellent white bass, largemouth bass, and catfish fishing in its series of locks and dams.

5. Lake Maumelle (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Lake Maumelle, Pulaski County

Lake Maumelle is a 8,900-acre reservoir providing drinking water for Little Rock, offering excellent largemouth bass and crappie fishing.

6. Lake Hamilton (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Striped Bass)

Lake Hamilton, Hot Springs area

Lake Hamilton is a 7,200-acre reservoir on the Ouachita River offering good bass and crappie fishing near Hot Springs.

7. Lake Catherine (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Lake Catherine, Hot Springs area

Lake Catherine offers good bass and crappie fishing below Lake Hamilton.

8. Lake Norrell (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Norrell, near Benton

Lake Norrell offers good bass and crappie fishing near Benton in central Arkansas.

9. Lake Winona (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Winona, Saline County

Lake Winona offers good bass and crappie fishing in central Arkansas.

10. Lake Hickory Hills (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Hickory Hills, near Benton

Lake Hickory Hills offers good bass and crappie fishing near Benton.

11. Lake Diamond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Diamond, Saline County

Lake Diamond offers good bass and crappie fishing in central Arkansas.

12. Lake Bailey (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Bailey, near Little Rock

Lake Bailey offers good fishing near Little Rock.

13. Lake Sylvia (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream)

Lake Sylvia, Perry County

Lake Sylvia offers good bass and bream fishing in the Ouachita National Forest.

14. Gillham Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Gillham Lake, Sevier County

Gillham Lake offers good bass and crappie fishing on the Cossatot River.

15. Fourche LaFave River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie)

Fourche LaFave River, central Arkansas

The Fourche LaFave River offers good bass and catfish fishing in central Arkansas.

16. Lake Barnett (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream)

Lake Barnett, Conway County

Lake Barnett offers good fishing.

17. Lake Overcup (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream)

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at agfc.com. Trout permit required for trout waters.
  • Fish Greers Ferry for walleye -- Greers Ferry Lake is one of the best walleye lakes in Arkansas. Spring and fall are peak.
  • Try the Little Red River -- The Little Red River offers excellent trout fishing below Greers Ferry Dam.
  • Urban fishing in Little Rock -- The Arkansas River through Little Rock offers good fishing from banks and boats.
  • Spring crappie -- Lake Conway is famous for its crappie fishing. March through May offers the best action.
