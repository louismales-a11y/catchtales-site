Fishing in Central Illinois

Lake Springfield, Clinton Lake, Shelbyville Lake, Decatur Lake, and the heart of Illinois fishing.

1. Lake Springfield (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Walleye)

Lake Springfield, Sangamon County

Lake Springfield is a 4,200-acre city reservoir offering excellent largemouth bass and crappie fishing in the state capital.

2. Clinton Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, Catfish, Bluegill)

Clinton Lake, DeWitt County

Clinton Lake is a 4,900-acre cooling lake offering excellent bass and crappie fishing with warm-water influence.

3. Shelbyville Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, Catfish, White Bass)

Shelbyville Lake, Shelby/Moultrie counties

Shelbyville Lake is an 11,100-acre reservoir offering excellent walleye, crappie, and white bass fishing.

4. Lake Decatur (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Walleye)

Lake Decatur, Macon County

Lake Decatur is a 2,800-acre reservoir offering good bass and crappie fishing in central Illinois.

5. Sangchris Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Sangchris Lake, Christian/Sangamon counties

Sangchris Lake is a 3,100-acre cooling lake offering good fishing.

6. Lake Taylorville (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Lake Taylorville, Christian County

Lake Taylorville offers good fishing near Taylorville.

7. Lake Mattoon (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Lake Mattoon, Coles County

Lake Mattoon offers good bass and crappie fishing.

8. Lake Charleston (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Lake Charleston, Coles County

Lake Charleston offers good fishing near Eastern Illinois University.

9. Paradise Lake (IL) (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Bluegill)

Paradise Lake, Champaign County

Paradise Lake offers stocked trout and bass fishing near Champaign.

10. Homer Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

11. Lake of the Woods (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

12. Kaufman Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Bluegill)

13. Crystal Lake Park Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

14. Lake Vermilion (Central) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

16. Middle Fork River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

17. Salt Fork River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

The Salt Fork River offers good smallmouth bass fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnr.illinois.gov.
  • Fish Lake Springfield -- Lake Springfield offers excellent bass and crappie fishing in the capital city.
  • Try Clinton Lake -- Clinton Lake's warm-water discharge creates unique fishing opportunities.
  • Shelbyville for walleye -- Shelbyville Lake is one of the best walleye lakes in central Illinois.
  • Spring crappie -- April and May offer excellent crappie fishing on most central Illinois lakes.
