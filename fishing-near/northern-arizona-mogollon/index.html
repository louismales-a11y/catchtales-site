Fishing in Northern Arizona & Mogollon Rim

Lake Powell, Lees Ferry, Willow Springs Lake, Woods Canyon Lake, and the trout and bass waters of Arizona's high country.

1. Lake Powell (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Striped Bass, Walleye, Crappie)

Lake Powell, Glen Canyon National Recreation Area

Lake Powell is one of the premier fishing destinations in the Southwest. The massive reservoir offers excellent smallmouth bass, striped bass, walleye, and crappie fishing in stunning red rock canyon scenery.

2. Lees Ferry (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Lees Ferry, Colorado River below Glen Canyon Dam

Lees Ferry offers world-class rainbow trout fishing in the Colorado River below Glen Canyon Dam. The cold, clear water produces trophy rainbow trout in the 16-22 inch range in a stunning canyon setting.

3. Willow Springs Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout)

Willow Springs Lake, Mogollon Rim

Willow Springs Lake is a high-elevation lake on the Mogollon Rim offering excellent rainbow and brook trout fishing. The lake's cold, clear waters produce good-sized trout.

4. Woods Canyon Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout)

Woods Canyon Lake, Mogollon Rim

Woods Canyon Lake is one of the most popular fishing lakes on the Mogollon Rim, offering excellent rainbow trout and brook trout fishing in a scenic pine forest setting.

5. Chevelon Canyon Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Apache Trout)

Chevelon Canyon Lake, Mogollon Rim

Chevelon Canyon Lake offers rainbow trout and the unique Apache trout fishing in a remote canyon setting on the Mogollon Rim.

6. Bear Canyon Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Bear Canyon Lake, Mogollon Rim

Bear Canyon Lake offers rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing in a scenic pine-forested canyon on the Mogollon Rim.

7. Knoll Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout, Brown Trout)

Knoll Lake, Mogollon Rim

Knoll Lake offers good rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing on the Mogollon Rim near the Blue Range.

8. Blue Ridge Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout, Brown Trout)

Blue Ridge Reservoir, Mogollon Rim

Blue Ridge Reservoir offers excellent rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing in a scenic canyon on the Mogollon Rim.

9. Black Canyon Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Black Canyon Lake, Mogollon Rim

Black Canyon Lake offers rainbow and brook trout fishing in a secluded mountain lake setting.

10. Lee Valley Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Lee Valley Lake, White Mountains

Lee Valley Lake offers rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing in the White Mountains near the Mogollon Rim.

11. Lake Powell (Colorado Arm) (Smallmouth Bass, Striped Bass, Walleye)

Lake Powell, Colorado River arm

The Colorado River arm of Lake Powell offers excellent smallmouth bass and striped bass fishing in the flooded canyon system.

12. Lake Powell (San Juan Arm) (Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Powell, San Juan River arm

The San Juan arm of Lake Powell offers excellent crappie and smallmouth bass fishing in the extensive flooded canyon system.

13. Paria River (Catfish, Sunfish, Carp)

Paria River, near Lees Ferry

The Paria River offers catfish and sunfish fishing near its confluence with the Colorado River at Lees Ferry.

14. Colorado River (Grand Canyon Reach) (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Colorado River, Grand Canyon

The Colorado River through the Grand Canyon offers excellent rainbow trout fishing below Phantom Ranch. Access is by multi-day raft trips only.

15. Little Colorado River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Little Colorado River, near Grand Canyon

The Little Colorado River offers good trout fishing near its confluence with the Colorado River in the Grand Canyon.

16. Jacob Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Jacob Lake, Kaibab Plateau

Jacob Lake offers rainbow and brook trout fishing on the Kaibab Plateau north of the Grand Canyon.

17. Kaibab Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Kaibab Lake, Williams area

Kaibab Lake offers rainbow and brown trout fishing near Williams on the south rim of the Grand Canyon.

18. Dogtown Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Dogtown Lake, Williams area

Dogtown Lake offers rainbow trout and largemouth bass fishing in the Kaibab National Forest near Williams.

19. Cataract Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Cataract Lake, Williams area

Cataract Lake offers rainbow trout and bass fishing near Williams, Arizona.

20. Whitehorse Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Whitehorse Lake, Kaibab National Forest

Whitehorse Lake offers rainbow and brook trout fishing in the Kaibab National Forest south of Williams.

21. Santa Fe Lake (Rainbow Trout)

Santa Fe Lake, near Flagstaff

Santa Fe Lake offers rainbow trout fishing near Flagstaff in the Coconino National Forest.

22. Francis Short Pond (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Francis Short Pond, Flagstaff

Francis Short Pond offers stocked rainbow trout and bass fishing in Flagstaff.

23. Ashurst Lake (Rainbow Trout, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Ashurst Lake, near Flagstaff

Ashurst Lake offers rainbow trout, northern pike, and yellow perch fishing south of Flagstaff.

24. Kinnikinick Lake (Rainbow Trout, Northern Pike)

Kinnikinick Lake, near Flagstaff

Kinnikinick Lake offers rainbow trout and northern pike fishing near Ashurst Lake.

25. Mormon Lake (Northern Pike, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Mormon Lake, near Flagstaff

Mormon Lake is Arizona's largest natural lake, offering northern pike, crappie, and yellow perch fishing south of Flagstaff.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at azgfd.gov.
  • Fish Lees Ferry year-round -- Lees Ferry offers excellent rainbow trout fishing every month of the year, with the best fishing in spring and fall.
  • Try Lake Powell in spring -- April through June offers the best smallmouth and striped bass fishing on Lake Powell before the summer heat.
  • Mogollon Rim lakes in summer -- The high-elevation lakes on the Mogollon Rim offer excellent trout fishing and escape from the desert heat.
  • Bring layers -- Northern Arizona's high country can be cold even in summer. Temperatures can drop below freezing at night.
