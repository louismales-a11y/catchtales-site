Fishing in Southeast Michigan & Detroit Area

Lake Erie, Detroit River, Huron River, inland lakes, and the walleye, bass, muskie, and perch fishing of metro Detroit.

1. Lake Erie (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Freshwater Drum)

Lake Erie, Monroe/Wayne counties

Lake Erie's western basin offers world-class walleye and yellow perch fishing, making Michigan's Lake Erie coast a premier Great Lakes fishery.

2. Detroit River (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Sturgeon)

Detroit River, Wayne County

The Detroit River connects Lake St. Clair to Lake Erie and is one of the busiest walleye fisheries in the Great Lakes.

3. Huron River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Huron River, Washtenaw/Wayne counties

The Huron River offers excellent smallmouth bass and walleye fishing from Dexter through Ann Arbor to Lake Erie.

4. River Rouge (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish, Carp)

River Rouge, Oakland/Wayne counties

The River Rouge offers urban fishing opportunities in metro Detroit.

5. Clinton River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Clinton River, Macomb County

The Clinton River offers good fishing through Macomb County into Lake St. Clair.

6. Belle Isle (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Largemouth Bass)

Belle Isle, Detroit

Belle Isle Park in the Detroit River offers excellent urban fishing opportunities in the heart of Detroit.

7. Lake Erie Metro Park (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Lake Erie Metro Park, Wayne County

Lake Erie Metro Park offers excellent shore fishing on Lake Erie with good access and facilities.

8. Sterling State Park (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish)

Sterling SP, Monroe County

Sterling State Park is Michigan's only Lake Erie state park, offering excellent walleye and perch fishing.

9. Pointe Mouillee (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Carp)

Pointe Mouillee SGA, Monroe County

Pointe Mouillee State Game Area offers excellent fishing in the Lake Erie marshlands.

10. Ford Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Ford Lake, Washtenaw County

Ford Lake offers excellent bass and panfish fishing near Ypsilanti.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at Michigan.gov/DNR.
  • Fish Lake Erie -- Michigan's Lake Erie coast offers world-class walleye and perch fishing.
  • Try the Detroit River -- The Detroit River is one of the best urban fisheries in America for walleye.
  • Metro Detroit lakes -- Oakland and Livingston counties offer hundreds of inland lakes with excellent bass and panfish fishing.
  • Spring and fall -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in southeast Michigan.
