Fishing in Central Rockies & Summit County

Dillon Reservoir, Blue River, Arkansas River, Leadville, Twin Lakes, and the heart of Colorado's gold medal trout waters.

1. Dillon Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee Salmon, Lake Trout)

Dillon Reservoir, Summit County

Dillon Reservoir is a 3,300-acre reservoir offering excellent trout, kokanee, and lake trout fishing with stunning mountain views.

2. Blue River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Blue River, Summit County

The Blue River is a gold medal trout stream offering excellent rainbow and brown trout fishing from Dillon Dam downstream.

3. Arkansas River (Brown Trout, Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Arkansas River, Chaffee/Lake counties

The Arkansas River is one of Colorado's premier gold medal trout fisheries, offering excellent brown and rainbow trout fishing through the Arkansas River Valley.

4. Twin Lakes (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee)

Twin Lakes, Lake County

Twin Lakes offers excellent lake trout and kokanee salmon fishing near Leadville at 9,200 feet.

5. Turquoise Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Lake Trout, Kokanee)

Turquoise Lake, Lake County

Turquoise Lake offers excellent trout and kokanee fishing near Leadville.

6. Leadville Area Lakes (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Leadville, Lake County

The Leadville area has numerous high-elevation lakes offering excellent trout fishing, including Evans Lake and Cascade Lake.

7. Green Mountain Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee, Lake Trout)

Green Mountain Reservoir, Summit County

Green Mountain Reservoir offers excellent trout and kokanee fishing on the Blue River.

8. South Platte River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

South Platte River, Park/Jefferson counties

The South Platte River offers gold medal trout fishing, particularly in Cheesman Canyon and Dream Stream sections.

9. Eleven Mile Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee, Northern Pike)

Eleven Mile Reservoir, Park County

Eleven Mile Reservoir offers excellent trout and pike fishing in South Park.

10. Spinney Mountain Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Northern Pike)

Spinney Mountain Reservoir, Park County

Spinney Mountain offers excellent trout and pike fishing with gold medal status.

11. Antero Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Antero Reservoir, Park County

Antero Reservoir offers excellent stillwater trout fishing in South Park.

12. Tarryall Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Northern Pike)

Tarryall Reservoir, Park County

Tarryall Reservoir offers good trout and pike fishing.

13. Eagle River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Eagle River, Eagle County

The Eagle River offers excellent gold medal trout fishing near Vail.

14. Gore Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Gore Creek, Eagle County

Gore Creek offers good trout fishing through Vail.

15. Colorado River (Upper) (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat)

Upper Colorado River, Grand County

The upper Colorado River offers gold medal trout fishing through the Gore Canyon and Kremmling areas.

16. Dillion Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee)

Dillon Reservoir, Summit County

Dillon Reservoir offers excellent trout and kokanee fishing.

17. Green Mountain Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee)

Green Mountain Reservoir, Summit County

Green Mountain offers good fishing on the Blue River.

18. Williams Fork Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Pike)

Williams Fork Reservoir, Grand County

Williams Fork offers good fishing.

19. Wolford Mountain Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Pike)

Wolford Mountain Reservoir, Grand County

Wolford Mountain offers good fishing.

20. Heeney Marina (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Heeney, Summit County

Heeney offers good fishing on Green Mountain Reservoir.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at cpw.state.co.us.
  • Fish Dillon for kokanee -- Dillon Reservoir offers excellent kokanee salmon fishing in summer.
  • Try the Arkansas River -- The Arkansas River is one of Colorado's best gold medal trout streams. Focus on the Browns Canyon section.
  • High-elevation fishing -- The Leadville area (10,000+) offers excellent trout fishing with breathtaking views.
  • Check flow rates -- River flows vary dramatically with snowmelt and dam releases. Check flows before heading out.
