Fishing in Mississippi Gulf Coast

Mississippi Sound, Gulf Islands National Seashore, Biloxi, Gulfport, Ocean Springs, and the speckled trout, redfish, flounder, and Spanish mackerel fishing of the Coast.

1. Mississippi Sound (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano)

Mississippi Sound, Hancock/Harrison/Jackson counties

The Mississippi Sound offers world-class inshore fishing for speckled trout and redfish along Mississippi's entire 62-mile Gulf Coast.

2. Gulf Islands National Seashore (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Pompano, Spanish Mackerel, Sheepshead)

Gulf Islands NS, Jackson County

The Gulf Islands National Seashore — including Ship Island, Horn Island, and Petit Bois Island — offers pristine surf and wade fishing for speckled trout, redfish, and pompano.

3. Ship Island (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano)

Ship Island, Harrison County

Ship Island is a popular barrier island offering excellent fishing and accessible by ferry from Gulfport.

4. Horn Island (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Spanish Mackerel)

Horn Island, Jackson County

Horn Island offers pristine remote fishing in the Gulf Islands National Seashore.

5. Petit Bois Island (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Pompano, Spanish Mackerel)

Petit Bois Island, Jackson County

Petit Bois Island is the easternmost barrier island in Mississippi, offering excellent remote fishing.

6. Cat Island (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

Cat Island, Harrison County

Cat Island offers excellent fishing near the western end of the Mississippi Sound.

7. Round Island (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano)

Round Island, Jackson County

Round Island near Pascagoula offers good fishing accessible by boat.

8. Biloxi Bay (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum, Blue Crab)

Biloxi Bay, Harrison County

Biloxi Bay offers excellent inshore fishing for speckled trout and redfish, with the Biloxi-Ocean Springs bridge providing popular fishing access.

9. Back Bay of Biloxi (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

Back Bay of Biloxi, Harrison County

The Back Bay of Biloxi offers good fishing in a sheltered estuary.

10. Gulfport Harbor (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Spanish Mackerel, Sheepshead)

Gulfport Harbor, Harrison County

Gulfport Harbor offers good fishing at the harbor walls and piers.

11. Gulfport Pier (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Gulfport Pier, Gulfport

The Gulfport Pier is a popular fishing pier offering excellent access for all skill levels.

12. Biloxi Pier (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano, Flounder)

Biloxi Pier, Biloxi

The Biloxi Pier offers good fishing in Biloxi.

13. Ocean Springs Pier (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel, Flounder, Pompano)

Ocean Springs Pier, Ocean Springs

The Ocean Springs fishing pier offers good fishing on Biloxi Bay.

14. Pascagoula River (Tidal) (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum, Largemouth Bass)

Tidal Pascagoula River, Jackson County

The tidal portion of the Pascagoula River offers excellent redfish and speckled trout fishing.

15. Pascagoula Beach (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano, Flounder)

Pascagoula Beach, Jackson County

Pascagoula Beach offers good surf fishing near the eastern end of the coast.

16. Bayou Caddy (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

Bayou Caddy, Hancock County

Bayou Caddy offers good fishing near the Louisiana border.

17. Heron Bay (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Heron Bay, Hancock County

Heron Bay offers good fishing near Waveland.

18. Bay of Saint Louis (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum, Blue Crab)

Bay of Saint Louis, Hancock County

The Bay of Saint Louis offers excellent fishing near the historic town of Bay St. Louis.

19. Waveland Pier (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano, Flounder)

Waveland Pier, Waveland

The Waveland Pier offers good fishing on the western end of the coast.

20. Lakeshore (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Lakeshore, Hancock County

Lakeshore on the Jourdan River offers good fishing.

21. Point A (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Point A, Hancock County

Point A on Bay St. Louis offers good fishing access.

22. Graveline Bayou (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

Graveline Bayou, Jackson County

Graveline Bayou offers good fishing near Gautier.

23. Gautier Beach (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Pompano)

Gautier, Jackson County

Gautier Beach offers good fishing on the eastern end of the coast.

24. Ocean Springs Beach (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Pompano)

Ocean Springs Beach, Jackson County

Ocean Springs Beach offers good fishing on Biloxi Bay.

25. Pass Christian Harbor (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

Pass Christian, Harrison County

Pass Christian Harbor offers good fishing in a historic coastal town.

26. Long Beach Harbor (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Long Beach, Harrison County

Long Beach Harbor offers good fishing.

27. D'Iberville Pier (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead)

D'Iberville, Harrison County

The D'Iberville Pier offers good fishing on the Back Bay of Biloxi.

28. St. Louis Bay (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum, Blue Crab)

St. Louis Bay, Hancock County

St. Louis Bay offers excellent inshore fishing.

29. Mississippi Offshore (King Mackerel, Spanish Mackerel, Cobia, Red Snapper, Grouper, Amberjack, Mahi-Mahi, Tuna)

Offshore MS, Gulf of Mexico

The offshore waters of Mississippi offer excellent deep-sea fishing for king mackerel, cobia, red snapper, grouper, and amberjack at the artificial reefs and oil platforms.

30. Gulfport Offshore Reefs (Red Snapper, Grouper, Amberjack, King Mackerel, Cobia)

Gulfport Offshore Reefs, Harrison County

The artificial reefs off Gulfport offer excellent offshore fishing for snapper, grouper, and amberjack.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at mdwfp.com. Saltwater licence required.
  • Fish the Mississippi Sound -- The Sound offers world-class speckled trout and redfish fishing.
  • Try the barrier islands -- Gulf Islands National Seashore offers pristine fishing accessible by ferry or boat.
  • Book a charter -- Biloxi and Gulfport have excellent charter fleets for inshore and offshore fishing.
  • Spring through fall -- March through November offers the best fishing on the Gulf Coast.
