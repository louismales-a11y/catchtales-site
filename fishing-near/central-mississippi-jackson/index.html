Fishing in Central Mississippi & Jackson Area

Ross Barnett Reservoir, Pearl River, Jackson area lakes, and the bass, crappie, and striped bass fishing of central Mississippi.

1. Ross Barnett Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Striped Bass, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Bluegill)

Ross Barnett Reservoir, Rankin/Madison counties

Ross Barnett Reservoir, known as "The Rez," is a 33,000-acre reservoir serving Jackson with excellent striped bass, crappie, and largemouth bass fishing.

2. Pearl River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Striped Bass, Blue Catfish)

Pearl River, Rankin/Hinds counties

The Pearl River flows through the Jackson area and offers excellent urban fishing for catfish, bass, and crappie.

3. Big Black River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Blue Catfish)

Big Black River, Hinds/Warren counties

The Big Black River offers good catfish and bass fishing in west central Mississippi.

4. Homochitto River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Rainbow Trout)

Homochitto River, Lincoln/Franklin counties

The Homochitto River offers good fishing and is stocked with rainbow trout in late fall.

5. Okhissa Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish, Rainbow Trout)

Okhissa Lake, Franklin County

Okhissa Lake in the Homochitto National Forest offers excellent fishing and is stocked with rainbow trout in winter.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at mdwfp.com.
  • Fish Ross Barnett Reservoir -- The Rez offers excellent striped bass and crappie fishing near Jackson.
  • Try the Pearl River -- The Pearl River offers good urban fishing through the Jackson area.
  • Winter trout stocking -- Several lakes and rivers in central Mississippi are stocked with rainbow trout in fall/winter.
  • Spring and fall are best -- March-May and September-November offer the best fishing.
