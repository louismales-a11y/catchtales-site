Fishing in Delaware Bay & Atlantic Coast

Cape Henlopen, Lewes, Delaware Bay, Atlantic beaches, and the world-class saltwater fishing of Delaware's coast.

1. Cape Henlopen State Park (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Tautog, Surf Perch)

Cape Henlopen State Park, Lewes

Cape Henlopen offers excellent surf and pier fishing at the mouth of Delaware Bay. The fishing pier and beaches provide access to striped bass, bluefish, and flounder.

2. Lewes Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Weakfish, Flounder, Black Drum)

Lewes Harbor, Sussex County

Lewes Harbor offers excellent saltwater fishing with charter boat access to Delaware Bay and the Atlantic.

3. Delaware Bay (Striped Bass, Weakfish, Bluefish, Black Drum, Croaker)

Delaware Bay, entire coast

Delaware Bay offers world-class striped bass fishing and excellent weakfish, black drum, and croaker fishing.

4. Broadkill Beach (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Kingfish, Spot)

Broadkill Beach, Sussex County

Broadkill Beach offers good surf fishing along the Delaware Bay coast.

5. Slaughter Beach (Black Drum, Striped Bass, Croaker, Spot)

Slaughter Beach, Sussex County

Slaughter Beach is famous for horseshoe crab spawning and the fishing it attracts, particularly for black drum and striped bass.

6. Port Mahon Road (Striped Bass, Weakfish, Croaker, White Perch)

Port Mahon, Kent County

Port Mahon offers excellent shoreline fishing in Delaware Bay, known for big striped bass.

7. Bowers Beach (Striped Bass, Weakfish, Croaker, Bluefish)

Bowers Beach, Kent County

Bowers Beach offers good Delaware Bay fishing with boat ramp access.

8. Mispillion River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Blue Crabs)

Mispillion River, Kent County

The Mispillion River offers good tidal fishing for striped bass and white perch.

9. St. Jones River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish)

St. Jones River, Kent County

The St. Jones River offers good tidal fishing near Dover.

10. Murderkill River (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish, Blue Crabs)

Murderkill River, Kent County

The Murderkill River offers good fishing and crabbing in central Delaware.

11. Milford Neck Wildlife Area (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Milford Neck, Sussex County

Milford Neck offers good freshwater fishing in its ponds and impoundments.

12. Prime Hook National Wildlife Refuge (Striped Bass, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Prime Hook NWR, Sussex County

Prime Hook offers both freshwater and saltwater fishing in its diverse wetland habitats.

13. Lewes Beach (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Kingfish, Spot)

Lewes Beach, Lewes

Lewes Beach offers good surf fishing within walking distance of historic Lewes.

14. Delaware Bay Reef Sites (Black Sea Bass, Tautog, Flounder)

Offshore Delaware Bay

Artificial reef sites in Delaware Bay offer excellent bottom fishing for sea bass, tautog, and flounder.

15. Atlantic Ocean (Offshore) (Bluefin Tuna, Mahi-Mahi, Shark, Mackerel)

Offshore Atlantic, Lewes/Indian River

The Atlantic Ocean off Delaware offers excellent offshore fishing for tuna, mahi-mahi, and sharks from charter boats.

16. Cape Henlopen Pier (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder)

Cape Henlopen, Sussex County

The fishing pier at Cape Henlopen offers excellent fishing.

17. Lewes Canal (Striped Bass, White Perch)

Lewes, Sussex County

The Lewes Canal offers good fishing.

18. Rehoboth Beach Pier (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Kingfish)

Rehoboth, Sussex County

The Rehoboth Beach Pier offers good fishing.

19. Dewey Beach (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Surf Perch)

Dewey, Sussex County

Dewey Beach offers good surf fishing.

20. Bethany Beach Pier (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Kingfish)

Bethany, Sussex County

Bethany Beach Pier offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnrec.delaware.gov. Saltwater registry required.
  • Fish Cape Henlopen -- Cape Henlopen offers excellent pier and surf fishing with beautiful scenery.
  • Spring striper run -- May and June offer the best striped bass fishing in Delaware Bay.
  • Book a Lewes charter -- Lewes has a strong charter fleet targeting offshore species and Delaware Bay fish.
  • Fall fishing -- September through November offers excellent fishing for bluefish and striped bass along the coast.
