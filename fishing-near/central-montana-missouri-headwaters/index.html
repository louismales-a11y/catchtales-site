Fishing in Central Montana & Missouri Headwaters

Missouri River below Holter, Canyon Ferry Reservoir, Hauser Reservoir, Helena area, and the rainbow trout, brown trout, walleye, and perch fishing of central Montana.

1. Missouri River - Holter Dam Tailwaters (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Mountain Whitefish, Walleye, Sauger)

Missouri River, Lewis & Clark County

The Missouri River tailwaters below Holter Dam is a world-famous blue-ribbon trout fishery with exceptional rainbow trout, brown trout, and prolific insect hatches.

2. Canyon Ferry Reservoir (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Northern Pike, Sauger)

Canyon Ferry Reservoir, Broadwater/Lewis & Clark counties

Canyon Ferry Reservoir is a 35,000-acre impoundment offering excellent walleye, yellow perch, and trout fishing with good public access.

3. Hauser Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Walleye, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike, Sauger)

Hauser Reservoir, Lewis & Clark County

Hauser Reservoir is a 3,700-acre impoundment offering excellent fishing near Helena with good shore access.

4. Holter Reservoir (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike, Sauger, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Holter Reservoir, Lewis & Clark County

Holter Reservoir is a 4,800-acre impoundment offering excellent walleye and perch fishing below Holter Dam.

5. Helena Valley Regulating Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee, Walleye)

Helena Valley Reservoir, Lewis & Clark County

Helena Valley Reservoir offers good fishing near Helena with a fishing dock.

6. Lake Helena (Northern Pike, Walleye, Yellow Perch, Rainbow Trout)

Lake Helena, Lewis & Clark County

Lake Helena offers good pike and walleye fishing near Helena.

7. Missouri River - Craig Area (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Mountain Whitefish)

Missouri River, Craig area, Lewis & Clark County

The Missouri River near Craig is one of the most famous trout fishing destinations in Montana with excellent wade and float access.

8. Missouri River - Cascade to Ulm (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Walleye, Sauger)

Missouri River, Cascade area, Cascade County

The Missouri River from Cascade to Ulm offers excellent trout and walleye fishing.

9. Dearborn River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Dearborn River, Lewis & Clark County

The Dearborn River offers excellent brown trout fishing in a scenic canyon setting.

10. Smith River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Brook Trout)

Smith River, Meagher/Cascade counties

The Smith River is a premier float fishing destination offering excellent trout fishing through a scenic limestone canyon.

11. Sun River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Walleye)

Sun River, Cascade/Teton counties

The Sun River offers good trout and walleye fishing west of Great Falls.

12. Gibson Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Lake Trout, Northern Pike)

Gibson Reservoir, Teton County

Gibson Reservoir on the Sun River offers excellent kokanee and lake trout fishing.

13. Pishkun Reservoir (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike, Rainbow Trout)

Pishkun Reservoir, Teton County

Pishkun Reservoir offers excellent walleye and perch fishing west of Great Falls.

14. Bynum Reservoir (Walleye, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Bynum Reservoir, Teton County

Bynum Reservoir offers good walleye and pike fishing.

15. Freezeout Lake (Rainbow Trout, Yellow Perch, Walleye, Northern Pike)

Freezeout Lake, Teton County

Freezeout Lake offers good fishing and is also a major waterfowl migration stopover.

16. Greenfields Lake (Rainbow Trout, Yellow Perch, Walleye)

Greenfields Lake, Teton County

Greenfields Lake offers good fishing.

17. Teton River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Walleye, Northern Pike)

Teton River, Teton/Pondera counties

The Teton River offers good trout and warmwater fishing.

18. Marias River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Sauger, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish)

Marias River, Toole/Liberty counties

The Marias River offers excellent warmwater fishing for walleye, pike, and smallmouth bass.

19. Tiber Reservoir (Walleye, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch, Sauger, Smallmouth Bass)

Tiber Reservoir, Toole County

Tiber Reservoir is a 16,000-acre reservoir offering excellent walleye and pike fishing.

20. Lake Elwell (Walleye, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch, Rainbow Trout)

Lake Elwell, Liberty County

Lake Elwell (Tiber Dam) offers excellent walleye and northern pike fishing.

21. Little Belt Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Little Belt Creek, Cascade County

Little Belt Creek offers good trout fishing in the Little Belt Mountains.

22. Belt Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Belt Creek, Cascade County

Belt Creek offers good trout fishing near the town of Belt.

23. Scratchgravel Hills Ponds (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Scratchgravel Hills Ponds, Lewis & Clark County

Scratchgravel Hills Ponds near Helena offer good put-and-take trout fishing.

24. Hauser Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Walleye, Yellow Perch)

Hauser Lake, Lewis & Clark County

Hauser Lake offers good fishing with convenient access from Helena.

25. Broadwater Pond (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Broadwater Pond, Broadwater County

Broadwater Pond offers good fishing near Townsend.

26. Townsend Area Ponds (Rainbow Trout, Walleye)

Townsend ponds, Broadwater County

Various ponds near Townsend offer good fishing opportunities.

27. Deep Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Deep Creek, Lewis & Clark County

Deep Creek offers good trout fishing near Wolf Creek.

28. Wolf Creek (MT) (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Wolf Creek, Lewis & Clark County

Wolf Creek offers good trout fishing near the Missouri River.

29. Little Prickly Pear Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Little Prickly Pear Creek, Lewis & Clark County

Little Prickly Pear Creek offers good trout fishing near Helena.

30. Prickly Pear Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Prickly Pear Creek, Lewis & Clark County

Prickly Pear Creek offers good trout fishing in the Helena valley.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at fwp.mt.gov. Conservation license required.
  • Fish the Missouri below Holter -- One of America's most famous tailwater trout fisheries with consistent hatches.
  • Try Canyon Ferry -- Canyon Ferry Reservoir is the premier walleye fishery in Montana.
  • Float the Smith River -- The Smith River offers a world-class multi-day float fishing experience.
  • Summer and fall are best -- June through October offer the best trout fishing. Walleye fishing peaks in spring and fall.
