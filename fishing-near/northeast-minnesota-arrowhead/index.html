Fishing in Northeast Minnesota & Arrowhead

Lake Superior, Boundary Waters Canoe Area, North Shore streams, and the lake trout, walleye, steelhead, and brook trout fishing of the Arrowhead region.

1. Lake Superior (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Steelhead)

Lake Superior, North Shore

Lake Superior offers world-class lake trout, salmon, and steelhead fishing along Minnesota's spectacular North Shore from Duluth to Grand Portage.

2. Boundary Waters Canoe Area (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout, Yellow Perch)

BWCAW, St. Louis/Lake/Cook counties

The Boundary Waters Canoe Area Wilderness offers pristine canoe-access fishing in over 1,000 interconnected lakes with excellent walleye, pike, and lake trout fishing.

3. Lake Vermilion (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Muskellunge, Walleye, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Vermilion, St. Louis County

Lake Vermilion is a 40,000-acre lake in the Arrowhead region, famous for its excellent walleye, muskie, and smallmouth bass fishing.

4. Rainy Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Rainy Lake, Koochiching County

Rainy Lake on the Canadian border offers excellent walleye and northern pike fishing in a scenic lake dotted with hundreds of islands.

5. Kabetogama Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Kabetogama Lake, St. Louis County

Kabetogama Lake in Voyageurs National Park offers excellent walleye and pike fishing.

6. Namakan Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Lake Trout)

Namakan Lake, St. Louis County

Namakan Lake offers excellent fishing in Voyageurs National Park.

7. Sand Point Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Sand Point Lake, St. Louis County

Sand Point Lake offers good fishing in Voyageurs National Park.

8. Crane Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Crane Lake, St. Louis County

Crane Lake offers good fishing on the Kabetogama Peninsula.

9. Burntside Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout, Crappie)

Burntside Lake, St. Louis County

Burntside Lake near Ely offers excellent lake trout and smallmouth bass fishing.

10. Fall Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Fall Lake, Lake County

Fall Lake at the edge of the BWCAW offers good fishing near Ely.

11. Basswood Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout, Crappie)

Basswood Lake, Lake/St. Louis counties

Basswood Lake in the BWCAW offers excellent wilderness fishing for walleye and lake trout.

12. Snowbank Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout)

Snowbank Lake, Lake County

Snowbank Lake offers excellent lake trout fishing near Ely.

13. Knife Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout)

Knife Lake, Lake County

Knife Lake in the BWCAW offers excellent wilderness fishing.

14. Saganaga Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout, Crappie)

Saganaga Lake, Cook County

Saganaga Lake on the Canadian border offers excellent walleye and lake trout fishing.

15. Gunflint Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout)

Gunflint Lake, Cook County

Gunflint Lake offers excellent lake trout and walleye fishing on the Gunflint Trail.

16. Hungry Jack Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Lake Trout)

Hungry Jack Lake, Cook County

Hungry Jack Lake offers good fishing on the Gunflint Trail.

17. Poplar Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Poplar Lake, Cook County

Poplar Lake offers good fishing on the Gunflint Trail.

18. North Shore Streams (Steelhead, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon)

North Shore streams, Lake County to Cook County

The rivers along Lake Superior's North Shore — including the Knife, Baptism, Temperance, Cascade, and Pigeon rivers — offer world-class steelhead and salmon fishing during spring and fall runs.

19. Baptism River (Steelhead, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Chinook Salmon)

Baptism River, Lake County

The Baptism River at Tettegouche State Park offers excellent steelhead and salmon fishing.

20. Temperance River (Steelhead, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Coho Salmon)

Temperance River, Cook County

The Temperance River offers good steelhead and salmon fishing.

21. Cascade River (Steelhead, Rainbow Trout, Coho Salmon, Chinook Salmon)

Cascade River, Cook County

The Cascade River offers good steelhead and salmon fishing at Cascade River State Park.

22. Pigeon River (Steelhead, Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon)

Pigeon River, Cook County

The Pigeon River forms the Canadian border and offers excellent steelhead and salmon fishing at Grand Portage State Park.

23. French River (Steelhead, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Coho Salmon)

French River, St. Louis County

The French River offers good steelhead fishing near Duluth.

24. Lester River (Steelhead, Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Coho Salmon)

Lester River, Duluth

The Lester River offers good steelhead and trout fishing in Duluth.

25. Amity Creek (Brook Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Amity Creek, Duluth

Amity Creek offers good trout fishing in Duluth.

26. St. Louis River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

St. Louis River, St. Louis/Carlton counties

The St. Louis River is the largest river flowing into Lake Superior from Minnesota, offering excellent walleye and smallmouth bass fishing from Jay Cooke State Park to Duluth harbor.

27. Island Lake Reservoir (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Island Lake Reservoir, St. Louis County

Island Lake Reservoir offers good walleye fishing near Duluth.

28. Fish Lake (St. Louis) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Fish Lake, St. Louis County

Fish Lake offers good warm-water fishing.

29. Whiteface Reservoir (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Whiteface Reservoir, St. Louis County

Whiteface Reservoir offers good walleye fishing.

30. Boulder Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Boulder Lake, St. Louis County

Boulder Lake offers good fishing near Duluth.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnr.state.mn.us. Non-resident licences available.
  • Fish the BWCAW -- The Boundary Waters offer pristine wilderness fishing accessible only by canoe.
  • Try Lake Vermilion -- Lake Vermilion offers excellent walleye and muskie fishing in a beautiful setting.
  • North Shore steelhead -- Spring steelhead runs on North Shore streams are world-class.
  • Summer and fall -- June-September offers the best fishing in northeast Minnesota.
