Fishing in Northwestern Illinois

Rock River, Pecatonica River, Apple River, Shabbona Lake, and the rivers and lakes of northwestern Illinois.

1. Rock River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Channel Catfish, Northern Pike)

Rock River, Winnebago/Ogle counties

The Rock River is one of Illinois' premier fishing rivers, offering excellent smallmouth bass, walleye, and catfish fishing from Rockford to the Mississippi.

2. Pecatonica River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Catfish)

Pecatonica River, Stephenson/Winnebago counties

The Pecatonica River offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing in northwest Illinois.

3. Apple River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Apple River, Jo Daviess County

The Apple River offers good smallmouth bass fishing in the scenic Driftless Area.

4. Shabbona Lake (Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Shabbona Lake, DeKalb County

Shabbona Lake is a 318-acre state park lake offering excellent bass, walleye, and crappie fishing.

5. Kishwaukee River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Catfish)

Kishwaukee River, Winnebago/Boone counties

The Kishwaukee River offers good smallmouth bass fishing near Rockford.

6. Lake Carroll (Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Crappie, Northern Pike, Bluegill)

Lake Carroll, Carroll County

Lake Carroll is a 260-acre private lake offering excellent fishing near Savanna.

7. Lake Le-Aqua-Na (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Lake Le-Aqua-Na, Stephenson County

Lake Le-Aqua-Na State Park offers good fishing in northwest Illinois.

8. Yellow Creek (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Yellow Creek, Jo Daviess County

Yellow Creek offers good smallmouth fishing in the Driftless Area.

9. Pecatonica River (Upper) (Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Catfish)

Upper Pecatonica River, Stephenson County

The upper Pecatonica offers good smallmouth bass fishing.

10. Rock River (Rockford Reach) (Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Catfish, Crappie)

The Rock River through Rockford offers excellent urban fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnr.illinois.gov.
  • Fish the Rock River -- The Rock River offers excellent smallmouth bass and walleye fishing through northwest Illinois.
  • Try Shabbona Lake -- Shabbona Lake is one of the best walleye fisheries in northern Illinois.
  • Mississippi River pools -- Pools 12-14 along northwestern Illinois offer excellent walleye and sauger fishing.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in northwestern Illinois.
