Fishing in Southeast Missouri & the Bootheel

Mississippi River, Wappapello Lake, Duck Creek, Otter Creek, and the bass, crappie, blue catfish, and sunfish fishing of the Missouri Bootheel.

1. Mississippi River (SE MO) (Blue Catfish, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, White Bass, Sauger, Crappie)

Mississippi River, Cape Girardeau/Scott/New Madrid counties

The Mississippi River along southeast Missouri offers world-class blue catfish and flathead catfish fishing with excellent access at multiple ramps and wing dams.

2. Wappapello Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Bluegill, Redear Sunfish)

Wappapello Lake, Wayne County

Wappapello Lake is an 8,400-acre reservoir in the Ozark foothills offering excellent bass, crappie, and catfish fishing.

3. Duck Creek Conservation Area (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish)

Duck Creek CA, Bollinger/Wayne counties

Duck Creek Conservation Area offers excellent fishing in its many managed lakes and wetlands with good shore access.

4. Otter Creek Conservation Area (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish)

Otter Creek CA, Wayne County

Otter Creek Conservation Area offers excellent fishing with multiple managed lakes and fishing docks.

5. Lake Girardeau (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Girardeau, Cape Girardeau County

Lake Girardeau is a 90-acre lake offering good fishing near Cape Girardeau with a fishing dock.

6. Cape LaCroix Bluff Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Cape LaCroix Bluff Lake, Cape Girardeau County

Cape LaCroix Bluff Lake offers good urban fishing.

7. Millers Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Millers Pond, Cape Girardeau County

Millers Pond offers good fishing.

8. Lake Sainte Louise (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Sainte Louise, St. Charles County

Lake Sainte Louise offers good fishing.

9. St. Johns Bayou (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Blue Catfish, Channel Catfish)

St. Johns Bayou, New Madrid County

St. Johns Bayou offers good fishing in the Bootheel.

10. Little River Ditches (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Little River ditches, Dunklin/Stoddard counties

The Little River drainage ditches in the Bootheel offer good fishing with convenient access.

11. Ten Mile Pond CA (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Ten Mile Pond CA, Mississippi County

Ten Mile Pond Conservation Area offers good fishing in the Bootheel.

12. Eagle Bluffs CA (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Eagle Bluffs CA, Boone County

Eagle Bluffs Conservation Area offers good fishing on the Missouri River floodplain.

13. Schell-Osage CA (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish)

Schell-Osage CA, St. Clair/Vernon counties

Schell-Osage Conservation Area offers excellent fishing with multiple managed lakes.

14. Lake Ozark (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Ozark, St. Clair County

Lake Ozark offers good fishing.

15. Harry S. Truman Reservoir (East) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Walleye, Channel Catfish)

Truman Reservoir (east), Benton/Hickory counties

The eastern arm of Truman Reservoir offers excellent fishing with numerous access points.

16. Big River (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Big River, St. Francois/Washington counties

The Big River offers good fishing in the Ozark foothills.

17. St. Francis River (MO) (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

St. Francis River, Wayne/Madison counties

The St. Francis River offers good smallmouth bass fishing.

18. Black River (SE MO) (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Black River, Butler/Reynolds counties

The Black River offers good smallmouth bass fishing with scenic Ozark float access.

19. Current River (Smallmouth Bass, Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish)

Current River, Shannon/Carter counties

The Current River is a premier Ozark float stream offering excellent smallmouth bass fishing and stocked rainbow trout.

20. Jacks Fork River (Smallmouth Bass, Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Jacks Fork River, Shannon County

The Jacks Fork River offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing in the Ozark National Scenic Riverways.

21. Eleven Point River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Eleven Point River, Oregon County

The Eleven Point River is a designated National Wild and Scenic River offering excellent trout fishing.

22. Castor River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Castor River, Bollinger/Cape Girardeau counties

The Castor River offers good fishing.

23. Whitewater River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Whitewater River, Cape Girardeau/Bollinger counties

The Whitewater River offers good fishing.

24. Saline Creek (Ste. Genevieve) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Saline Creek, Ste. Genevieve County

Saline Creek offers good fishing.

25. Apple Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Apple Creek, Perry/Cape Girardeau counties

Apple Creek offers good fishing.

26. Cinque Hommes Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Cinque Hommes Creek, Perry County

Cinque Hommes Creek offers good fishing.

27. Brazeau Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Brazeau Creek, Perry County

Brazeau Creek offers good fishing.

28. Mississippi River - Tailwaters (Sauger, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Blue Catfish)

Mississippi River tailwaters, multiple counties

The tailwaters below Mississippi River dams offer excellent sauger, white bass, and catfish fishing.

29. Mingo National Wildlife Refuge (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Mingo NWR, Wayne/Stoddard counties

Mingo National Wildlife Refuge offers excellent fishing in its many oxbows and waterways.

30. Lake Wappapello State Park (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Wappapello SP, Wayne County

Lake Wappapello State Park offers excellent shore fishing access with a fishing dock.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at mdc.mo.gov.
  • Fish the Mississippi River -- The Mississippi River offers world-class blue catfish fishing with many access points.
  • Try Wappapello Lake -- Offers excellent bass and crappie fishing with good fishing docks.
  • Explore the Conservation Areas -- Duck Creek and Otter Creek offer excellent managed lake fishing.
  • Spring and fall are best -- March-May and September-November offer the best fishing.
