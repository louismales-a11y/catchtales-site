Fishing in Southeastern Arizona

San Carlos Lake, Patagonia Lake, Parker Canyon Lake, Roper Lake, and the hidden fishing gems of southeastern Arizona.

1. San Carlos Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Sunfish)

San Carlos Lake, Gila County

San Carlos Lake is a 19,000-acre reservoir on the Gila River offering excellent largemouth bass and crappie fishing. The lake is operated by the San Carlos Apache Tribe and requires a tribal fishing permit.

2. Patagonia Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Sunfish, Catfish)

Patagonia Lake State Park, Santa Cruz County

Patagonia Lake is a 265-acre lake in the Patagonia Mountains offering excellent bass, crappie, and sunfish fishing in a scenic state park setting.

3. Parker Canyon Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Parker Canyon Lake, Cochise County

Parker Canyon Lake is a 145-acre lake in the Huachuca Mountains offering rainbow trout, largemouth bass, and crappie fishing in a high-elevation setting.

4. Roper Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Roper Lake State Park, Safford area

Roper Lake offers rainbow trout, largemouth bass, and catfish fishing in a state park setting near Safford, with a unique natural warm-water pond.

5. Dankworth Pond (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Dankworth Pond, Safford area

Dankworth Pond offers rainbow trout and largemouth bass fishing near Roper Lake in the Safford area.

6. Cluff Ranch Ponds (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Cluff Ranch, Safford area

Cluff Ranch's ponds offer stocked rainbow trout and catfish fishing in a scenic ranch setting.

7. Riggs Flat Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Riggs Flat Lake, Pinaleno Mountains

Riggs Flat Lake is a high-elevation lake in the Pinaleno Mountains offering rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing in a mountain setting.

8. Arcadia Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Arcadia Lake, Safford area

Arcadia Lake offers rainbow trout and bass fishing in the Safford area of southeastern Arizona.

9. Sonoita Creek State Natural Area (Largemouth Bass, Sunfish, Catfish)

Sonoita Creek, Patagonia area

Sonoita Creek offers good bass and sunfish fishing in a scenic riparian area near Patagonia.

10. Peña Blanca Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Sunfish, Catfish)

Peña Blanca Lake, Nogales area

Peña Blanca Lake is a 50-acre lake in the Coronado National Forest offering good largemouth bass, crappie, and sunfish fishing near Nogales.

11. Lyman Lake (Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Crappie, Catfish)

Lyman Lake, St. Johns area

Lyman Lake is a 1,500-acre reservoir on the Little Colorado River offering largemouth bass, walleye, and crappie fishing.

12. Nelson Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Walleye, Catfish)

Nelson Reservoir, Springerville area

Nelson Reservoir offers rainbow trout, walleye, and catfish fishing in the Springerville area of eastern Arizona.

13. Fool Hollow Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie)

Fool Hollow Lake, Show Low area

Fool Hollow Lake offers rainbow trout, largemouth bass, and catfish fishing in a state park near Show Low.

14. Show Low Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Show Low Lake, Show Low area

Show Low Lake offers rainbow trout and largemouth bass fishing in the White Mountains near Show Low.

15. Rainbow Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout)

Rainbow Lake, Greer area

Rainbow Lake offers rainbow, brook, and brown trout fishing in the Greer area of the White Mountains.

16. Bunch Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Bunch Reservoir, Alpine area

Bunch Reservoir offers rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing in the Alpine area of eastern Arizona.

17. Carnero Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Carnero Lake, Alpine area

Carnero Lake offers rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing near Alpine.

18. Becker Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout, Brown Trout)

Becker Lake, Springerville area

Becker Lake is a catch-and-release, artificial-fly-only lake offering trophy rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing.

19. River Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

River Reservoir, Alpine area

River Reservoir offers rainbow and brown trout fishing in the Alpine area.

20. Luna Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout)

Luna Lake, Alpine area

Luna Lake offers excellent rainbow, brook, and brown trout fishing in a scenic lake near the New Mexico border.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at azgfd.gov. San Carlos Lake requires a separate tribal permit.
  • Fish San Carlos for bass -- San Carlos Lake offers excellent largemouth bass fishing with less pressure than the Salt River chain.
  • Try Patagonia Lake in spring -- Spring offers the best bass fishing at Patagonia Lake in the scenic Patagonia Mountains.
  • High-elevation trout -- The lakes in the White Mountains and Pinaleno Mountains offer excellent trout fishing and escape from summer heat.
  • Check tribal regulations -- San Carlos Lake is on tribal land and has separate regulations and permit requirements.
