Fishing in Southern Maryland & Potomac River

Potomac River, St. Mary's Lake, Mattawoman Creek, and the fishing of southern Maryland.

1. Potomac River (Blue Catfish, Channel Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Striped Bass, Crappie)

Potomac River, Charles/St. Mary's counties

The Potomac River offers world-class blue catfish fishing, with fish exceeding 80 pounds in the tidal Potomac.

2. St. Mary's Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Chain Pickerel)

St. Mary's Lake, St. Mary's County

St. Mary's Lake is a 250-acre man-made lake offering good bass and crappie fishing at St. Mary's Lake State Park.

3. Mattawoman Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Northern Pike)

Mattawoman Creek, Charles County

Mattawoman Creek is one of the most productive tidal creeks on the Potomac, offering excellent bass and crappie fishing.

4. Gilbert Run Park Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Gilbert Run Park, Charles County

Gilbert Run Park's lake offers good family fishing near La Plata.

5. Zekiah Swamp (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Crappie, Bluegill)

Zekiah Swamp, Charles County

Zekiah Swamp offers unique fishing in a large forested wetland.

6. Port Tobacco River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Striped Bass)

Port Tobacco River, Charles County

The Port Tobacco River offers good tidal fishing on the Potomac.

7. Wicomico River (MD South) (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Striped Bass)

Wicomico River, Charles County

The Wicomico River offers good tidal fishing in southern Maryland.

8. Potomac River (Tidal) (Striped Bass, Blue Catfish, White Perch, Crappie)

Tidal Potomac, Charles/St. Mary's counties

The tidal Potomac offers world-class striped bass (rockfish) and blue catfish fishing.

9. Breton Bay (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish, Crappie)

Breton Bay, St. Mary's County

Breton Bay offers good fishing on the Potomac River.

10. St. Clement's Bay (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish)

St. Clement's Bay, St. Mary's County

St. Clement's Bay offers good tidal fishing.

11. Patuxent River (Tidal) (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish, Largemouth Bass)

Tidal Patuxent, Calvert/St. Mary's counties

The tidal Patuxent River offers excellent striped bass and white perch fishing.

12. Calvert Cliffs (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Spanish Mackerel)

Calvert Cliffs SP, Calvert County

Calvert Cliffs State Park offers good shore fishing on the Chesapeake Bay.

13. Solomons Island (Striped Bass, White Perch, Croaker, Spot)

Solomons Island, Calvert County

Solomons Island offers excellent fishing at the mouth of the Patuxent River.

14. Point Lookout (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Speckled Trout, Croaker)

Point Lookout SP, St. Mary's County

Point Lookout State Park offers excellent fishing at the tip of southern Maryland.

15. Piney Point (Striped Bass, White Perch, Catfish, Croaker)

Piney Point, St. Mary's County

Piney Point offers good fishing on the Potomac River.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnrec.maryland.gov. Free fishing days available.
  • Fish the Potomac for blue catfish -- The Potomac River offers world-class blue catfish fishing.
  • Try Mattawoman Creek -- Mattawoman Creek is one of the most productive tidal creeks on the East Coast.
  • Free fishing areas -- Maryland has designated free fishing areas where no licence is required.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-November offer the best fishing in southern Maryland.
