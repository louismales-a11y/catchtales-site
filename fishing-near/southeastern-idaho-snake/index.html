Fishing in Southeastern Idaho & Snake Plain

Snake River, Henrys Fork, Portneuf River, American Falls Reservoir, and the fishing of the Snake River Plain.

1. South Fork Snake River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Mountain Whitefish)

South Fork Snake, Bonneville/Jefferson counties

The South Fork Snake River is a world-class trout fishery, known for large rainbow and cutthroat trout in a scenic river below Palisades Dam.

2. Henrys Fork of the Snake River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Henrys Fork, Fremont/Madison counties

The Henrys Fork is one of the most famous trout streams in the world, known for its technical fly fishing and large rainbow trout.

3. American Falls Reservoir (Walleye, Rainbow Trout, Crappie, Yellow Perch, Catfish)

American Falls Reservoir, Power County

American Falls is a 57,000-acre reservoir offering excellent walleye, perch, and trout fishing.

4. Portneuf River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Portneuf River, Bannock County

The Portneuf River offers good trout and bass fishing near Pocatello.

5. Blackfoot River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Blackfoot River, Caribou County

The Blackfoot River offers good trout fishing in southeastern Idaho.

6. Palisades Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Lake Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Palisades Reservoir, Bonneville County

Palisades Reservoir offers excellent trout and kokanee fishing on the South Fork Snake River.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at idfg.idaho.gov.
  • Fish the Henrys Fork -- The Henrys Fork is one of the world's great trout streams. The Ranch section is world-famous.
  • Try American Falls for walleye -- American Falls Reservoir is Idaho's best walleye fishery.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in southeastern Idaho.
  • Check Bear Lake regulations -- Bear Lake has special regulations. The Bonneville cutthroat trout is protected.
