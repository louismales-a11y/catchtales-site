Fishing in Southern Delaware & Inland Bays

Indian River Inlet, Rehoboth Bay, Indian River Bay, and the rich coastal waters of southern Delaware.

1. Indian River Inlet (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Weakfish, Flounder, Tautog)

Indian River Inlet, Sussex County

Indian River Inlet is one of the premier fishing spots in Delaware, offering excellent striped bass, bluefish, weakfish, and tautog fishing from the jetty and shore.

2. Rehoboth Bay (Striped Bass, Weakfish, Bluefish, Flounder, Crabbing)

Rehoboth Bay, Sussex County

Rehoboth Bay offers excellent inshore fishing for striped bass and weakfish in a protected bay setting.

3. Indian River Bay (Striped Bass, Weakfish, Flounder, Blue Crab)

Indian River Bay, Sussex County

Indian River Bay offers good inshore fishing and crabbing in southern Delaware.

4. Delaware Seashore State Park (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Surf Perch, Kingfish)

Delaware Seashore State Park, Sussex County

The park offers excellent surf fishing along 6 miles of Atlantic coastline.

5. Bethany Beach (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Kingfish, Spot)

Bethany Beach, Sussex County

Bethany Beach offers good surf fishing from the beach and pier.

6. Fenwick Island (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Tautog)

Fenwick Island, Sussex County

Fenwick Island offers excellent fishing from the state park and surf.

7. Assawoman Wildlife Area (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Assawoman Wildlife Area, Sussex County

The Assawoman ponds offer good freshwater fishing in a wildlife refuge setting.

8. Trussum Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Chain Pickerel)

Trussum Pond, Sussex County

Trussum Pond offers good warm-water fishing near Laurel.

9. Trap Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Chain Pickerel)

Trap Pond State Park, Sussex County

Trap Pond offers excellent bass and panfish fishing in a scenic cypress swamp setting.

10. Nanticoke River (Largemouth Bass, White Perch, Catfish, Crappie)

Nanticoke River, Sussex County

The Nanticoke River offers good freshwater and tidal fishing in western Sussex County.

11. Broad Creek (Largemouth Bass, White Perch, Catfish)

Broad Creek, Sussex County

Broad Creek offers good fishing in the Nanticoke River watershed.

12. Ingram Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Ingram Pond, Sussex County

Ingram Pond offers good warm-water fishing near Millsboro.

13. Millsboro Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Millsboro Pond, Sussex County

Millsboro Pond offers good family fishing in southern Delaware.

14. Records Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Records Pond, Sussex County

Records Pond offers good warm-water fishing near Laurel.

15. Lake Gerar (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Gerar, Sussex County

Lake Gerar offers good fishing near Rehoboth Beach.

16. Millsboro Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Millsboro, Sussex County

Millsboro Pond offers good freshwater fishing.

17. Records Pond (Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Records Pond, Sussex County

Records Pond offers good freshwater fishing.

18. Wagamon Pond (Largemouth Bass, Sunfish)

Wagamon Pond, Sussex County

Wagamon Pond offers good fishing.

19. Griffith Lake (Largemouth Bass, Sunfish)

Griffith Lake, Sussex County

Griffith Lake offers good fishing.

20. Concord Pond (Largemouth Bass, Sunfish)

Concord Pond, Sussex County

Concord Pond offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnrec.delaware.gov. Saltwater fishing requires a registry.
  • Fish Indian River Inlet -- The inlet is one of the best fishing spots on the East Coast for striped bass and tautog.
  • Try the inland bays -- Rehoboth and Indian River bays offer excellent kayak fishing.
  • Summer weakfish -- June through August offers the best weakfish fishing in the inland bays.
  • Crab while you fish -- The inland bays offer excellent blue crabbing alongside fishing.
