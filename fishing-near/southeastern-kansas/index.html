Fishing in Southeastern Kansas

Elk City Lake, Toronto Lake, Fall River Lake, John Redmond Reservoir, and the timbered reservoirs of southeast Kansas.

1. Elk City Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Bluegill, Walleye)

Elk City Lake, Montgomery County

Elk City Lake is a 4,500-acre reservoir offering excellent largemouth bass and crappie fishing in the timbered hills of southeast Kansas.

2. Toronto Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Bluegill, Walleye)

Toronto Lake, Woodson County

Toronto Lake is a 2,800-acre reservoir offering good bass and crappie fishing.

3. Fall River Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Bluegill)

Fall River Lake, Greenwood County

Fall River Lake is a 3,000-acre reservoir offering good bass and crappie fishing.

4. John Redmond Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Wiper)

John Redmond Reservoir, Coffey County

John Redmond Reservoir is a 9,400-acre reservoir offering good wiper and bass fishing.

5. El Dorado Lake (Wiper, Walleye, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass)

El Dorado Lake, Butler County

El Dorado Lake is an 8,000-acre reservoir offering excellent wiper and walleye fishing.

6. Big Hill Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Bluegill)

Big Hill Lake, Labette County

Big Hill Lake is a 1,200-acre reservoir offering good bass and crappie fishing.

7. Osage State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Osage SFL, Osage County

Osage SFL offers good fishing near Lyndon.

8. Chautauqua State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Chautauqua SFL, Chautauqua County

Chautauqua SFL offers good fishing near Sedan.

9. Elk County State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Elk County SFL, Elk County

Elk County SFL offers good fishing near Howard.

10. Neosho State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Neosho SFL, Neosho County

Neosho SFL offers good fishing near Erie.

11. Labette State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Labette SFL, Labette County

Labette SFL offers good fishing near Parsons.

12. Montgomery State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Montgomery SFL, Montgomery County

Montgomery SFL offers good fishing near Coffeyville.

13. Wilson State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Wilson SFL, Wilson County

Wilson SFL offers good fishing near Fredonia.

14. Allen State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Allen SFL, Allen County

Allen SFL offers good fishing near Iola.

15. Bourbon State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Bourbon SFL, Bourbon County

Bourbon SFL offers good fishing near Fort Scott.

16. Linn State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Linn SFL, Linn County

Linn SFL offers good fishing near Mound City.

17. Anderson State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Anderson SFL, Anderson County

Anderson SFL offers good fishing near Garnett.

18. Franklin State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Franklin SFL, Franklin County

Franklin SFL offers good fishing near Ottawa.

19. Miami State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Miami SFL, Miami County

Miami SFL offers good fishing near Paola.

20. Cross Timbers State Park (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Cross Timbers SP, Woodson County

Cross Timbers State Park offers good fishing on Toronto Lake.

21. Verdigris River (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish)

Verdigris River, Montgomery/Wilson counties

The Verdigris River offers good catfish and bass fishing in southeast Kansas.

22. Neosho River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Neosho River, Neosho/Labette counties

The Neosho River offers excellent catfish fishing.

23. Cottonwood River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Cottonwood River, Chase/Marion counties

The Cottonwood River offers good smallmouth fishing.

24. Marmaton River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie)

Marmaton River, Bourbon County

The Marmaton River offers good fishing near Fort Scott.

25. Mined Land Lakes (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Mined Land lakes, Crawford/Cherokee counties

The strip-pit lakes of southeast Kansas offer excellent fishing in reclaimed mining areas.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at ksoutdoors.com.
  • Fish Elk City Lake -- Elk City offers excellent bass and crappie fishing in the timbered hills.
  • Try the mined-land lakes -- Southeast Kansas' strip-pit lakes offer unique fishing opportunities.
  • Spring crappie -- April and May offer excellent crappie fishing on southeast Kansas reservoirs.
  • Fall bass fishing -- September and October offer excellent bass fishing in cooler weather.
