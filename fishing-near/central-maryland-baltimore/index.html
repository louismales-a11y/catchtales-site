Fishing in Central Maryland & Baltimore Area

Liberty Reservoir, Loch Raven, Gunpowder River, Patapsco River, and the reservoirs and rivers near Baltimore.

1. Liberty Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, Catfish, Bluegill)

Liberty Reservoir, Carroll/Baltimore counties

Liberty Reservoir is a 3,800-acre reservoir offering excellent bass, crappie, and walleye fishing near Baltimore.

2. Loch Raven Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, Chain Pickerel)

Loch Raven Reservoir, Baltimore County

Loch Raven Reservoir is a 2,400-acre reservoir offering good bass and walleye fishing.

3. Prettyboy Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, Northern Pike)

Prettyboy Reservoir, Baltimore County

Prettyboy Reservoir offers good fishing in a protected watershed.

4. Gunpowder River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Gunpowder River, Baltimore County

The Gunpowder River offers excellent trout fishing below Prettyboy Dam and good smallmouth bass fishing.

5. Patapsco River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Rainbow Trout)

Patapsco River, Howard/Baltimore counties

The Patapsco River offers good fishing through Patapsco Valley State Park.

6. Patuxent River (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bluegill)

Patuxent River, Howard/Montgomery counties

The Patuxent River offers good fishing in central Maryland.

7. Triadelphia Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Walleye)

Triadelphia Reservoir, Howard/Montgomery counties

Triadelphia Reservoir offers good fishing near Washington DC.

8. Rocky Gorge Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Rocky Gorge Reservoir, Howard/Montgomery counties

Rocky Gorge Reservoir offers good fishing near Triadelphia.

9. Little Pipe Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Little Pipe Creek, Carroll County

Little Pipe Creek offers good trout fishing.

10. Morgan Run (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Morgan Run, Carroll County

Morgan Run offers good trout fishing.

11. North Branch Patapsco (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

North Branch Patapsco, Carroll County

The North Branch of the Patapsco offers good trout fishing.

12. Piney Run Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Piney Run Lake, Carroll County

Piney Run Lake offers good fishing in Carroll County.

13. Lake Elkhorn (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Elkhorn, Howard County

Lake Elkhorn offers good urban fishing in Columbia.

14. Centennial Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Centennial Lake, Howard County

Centennial Lake offers good fishing in Ellicott City.

15. Lake Kittamaqundi (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Lake Kittamaqundi, Howard County

Lake Kittamaqundi offers good urban fishing in Columbia.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnrec.maryland.gov. Free fishing days available.
  • Fish Liberty Reservoir -- Liberty Reservoir offers excellent bass and walleye fishing near Baltimore.
  • Try the Gunpowder River -- The Gunpowder is one of Maryland's best trout streams.
  • Urban fishing -- Columbia's lakes offer good urban fishing opportunities.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing.
