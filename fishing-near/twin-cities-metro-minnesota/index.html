Fishing in Twin Cities Metro Area

Minneapolis, St. Paul, Lake Minnetonka, Mississippi River, and the urban and suburban fishing of the Twin Cities.

1. Mississippi River (Minneapolis) (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Northern Pike, Crappie, Largemouth Bass)

Mississippi River, Minneapolis/St. Paul

The Mississippi River through the Twin Cities offers excellent urban fishing with a surprising diversity of species including walleye, smallmouth bass, and channel catfish.

2. Lake Minnetonka (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Muskellunge)

Lake Minnetonka, Hennepin County

Lake Minnetonka is a 14,000-acre lake with 175 miles of shoreline, offering excellent walleye, bass, and muskie fishing in a scenic setting.

3. Minneapolis Chain of Lakes (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye, Sunfish)

Chain of Lakes, Minneapolis

The Minneapolis Chain of Lakes — including Lake Harriet, Lake Calhoun (Bde Maka Ska), Lake of the Isles, and Cedar Lake — offers excellent urban fishing in the heart of the city.

4. Bde Maka Ska (Lake Calhoun) (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Bde Maka Ska, Minneapolis

Bde Maka Ska offers excellent urban fishing in south Minneapolis with a healthy panfish and bass population.

5. Lake Harriet (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Lake Harriet, Minneapolis

Lake Harriet offers good fishing and a popular walking path in south Minneapolis.

6. Lake of the Isles (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake of the Isles, Minneapolis

Lake of the Isles offers good fishing in a beautiful Minneapolis neighborhood.

7. Cedar Lake (Minneapolis) (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Cedar Lake, Minneapolis

Cedar Lake offers good fishing near downtown Minneapolis.

8. Lake Nokomis (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Lake Nokomis, Minneapolis

Lake Nokomis offers good fishing in south Minneapolis.

9. Lake Hiawatha (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Hiawatha, Minneapolis

Lake Hiawatha offers good fishing in Minneapolis.

10. Como Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Como Lake, St. Paul

Como Lake offers good urban fishing in St. Paul.

11. Lake Phalen (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Lake Phalen, St. Paul

Lake Phalen offers good fishing in St. Paul.

12. Lake Johanna (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Lake Johanna, Ramsey County

Lake Johanna offers good fishing in Arden Hills.

13. Bald Eagle Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Bald Eagle Lake, Ramsey County

Bald Eagle Lake offers good fishing in White Bear Lake area.

14. White Bear Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Crappie, Bluegill)

White Bear Lake, Ramsey/Washington counties

White Bear Lake offers excellent fishing in the northeast metro.

15. Tanners Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Tanners Lake, Washington County

Tanners Lake offers good fishing in Oakdale.

16. Lake Elmo (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Lake Elmo, Washington County

Lake Elmo offers good fishing in Lake Elmo Park Reserve.

17. Big Marine Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Big Marine Lake, Washington County

Big Marine Lake offers excellent walleye and bass fishing.

18. Forest Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Forest Lake, Washington County

Forest Lake offers good fishing in the northern suburbs.

19. Peltier Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Peltier Lake, Anoka County

Peltier Lake offers good fishing.

20. Centerville Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Centerville Lake, Anoka County

Centerville Lake offers good fishing.

21. Ham Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Ham Lake, Anoka County

Ham Lake offers good fishing.

22. Coon Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Coon Lake, Anoka County

Coon Lake offers good walleye and crappie fishing.

23. Martin Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Martin Lake, Anoka County

Martin Lake offers good fishing.

24. Linwood Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Linwood Lake, Anoka County

Linwood Lake offers good fishing.

25. Minnehaha Creek (Brown Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Minnehaha Creek, Hennepin County

Minnehaha Creek offers urban trout fishing from Lake Minnetonka to Minnehaha Falls in Minneapolis.

26. Mississippi River (St. Paul) (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Crappie, Northern Pike, Sauger)

Mississippi River, St. Paul

The Mississippi River through St. Paul offers excellent walleye, sauger, and smallmouth bass fishing below the Ford Dam.

27. Minnesota River (Channel Catfish, Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass)

Minnesota River, Hennepin/Carver counties

The Minnesota River offers good fishing from the southwestern suburbs to its confluence with the Mississippi in St. Paul.

28. Prior Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Prior Lake, Scott County

Prior Lake offers excellent walleye and bass fishing in the southern metro.

29. Spring Lake (Scott) (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Spring Lake, Scott County

Spring Lake offers good fishing.

30. Lake Rebecca (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Rebecca, Hennepin County

Lake Rebecca offers good fishing in Lake Rebecca Park Reserve.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnr.state.mn.us.
  • Fish the Minneapolis Chain of Lakes -- The Chain of Lakes offers excellent urban fishing with easy access.
  • Try Lake Minnetonka -- Lake Minnetonka is one of the best walleye and muskie lakes in the metro.
  • Mississippi River -- The river through the Twin Cities offers surprisingly good walleye and smallmouth bass fishing.
  • Year-round fishing -- Open water from April-November and ice fishing in winter on metro lakes.
