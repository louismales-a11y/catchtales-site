Fishing in Western Colorado & Grand Mesa

Grand Mesa's 300+ alpine lakes, Colorado River, Roaring Fork, Fryingpan, and the best of western Colorado fishing.

1. Grand Mesa (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Kokanee)

Grand Mesa, Mesa/Delta counties

The Grand Mesa has over 300 alpine lakes offering exceptional trout fishing. Key lakes include Island Lake, Vega Reservoir, Alexander Lake, and Baron Lake.

2. Roaring Fork River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Roaring Fork River, Pitkin County

The Roaring Fork River is a gold medal trout stream offering excellent fishing from Aspen to Glenwood Springs.

3. Fryingpan River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Fryingpan River, Pitkin County

The Fryingpan River is a world-class gold medal trout stream known for large rainbow and brown trout below Ruedi Dam.

4. Colorado River (Glenwood Reach) (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Colorado River, Garfield County

The Colorado River through Glenwood Springs offers excellent gold medal trout fishing.

5. Rifle Gap Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass)

Rifle Gap Reservoir, Garfield County

Rifle Gap offers excellent trout, pike, and bass fishing near Rifle.

6. Harvey Gap Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Harvey Gap Reservoir, Garfield County

Harvey Gap offers good trout and bass fishing near Rifle.

7. Vega Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee)

Vega Reservoir, Mesa County

Vega Reservoir offers excellent trout and kokanee fishing on the Grand Mesa.

8. Island Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Island Lake, Grand Mesa

Island Lake offers excellent trout fishing on the Grand Mesa, accessible by road.

9. Alexander Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Alexander Lake, Grand Mesa

Alexander Lake offers good trout fishing on the Grand Mesa.

10. Baron Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Cutthroat)

Baron Lake, Grand Mesa

Baron Lake offers good trout fishing.

11. Eggleston Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Eggleston Lake, Grand Mesa

Eggleston Lake offers good trout fishing on the Grand Mesa.

12. Kannah Creek Basin Lakes (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Kannah Creek, Grand Mesa

The Kannah Creek basin on the Grand Mesa has numerous small lakes with excellent trout fishing.

13. Crystal River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat)

Crystal River, Pitkin County

The Crystal River offers good trout fishing near Carbondale.

14. Plateau Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Plateau Creek, Mesa County

Plateau Creek offers good trout fishing on the Grand Mesa.

15. Elk River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Elk River, Routt County

The Elk River offers good trout fishing near Steamboat Springs.

16. Vega Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee)

Vega Reservoir, Mesa County

Vega Reservoir offers excellent trout and kokanee fishing on the Grand Mesa.

17. Carson Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Carson Lake, Grand Mesa

Carson Lake offers good trout fishing on the Grand Mesa.

18. Cottonwood Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Cottonwood Lake, Grand Mesa

Cottonwood Lake offers good trout fishing.

19. Ward Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Ward Lake, Grand Mesa

Ward Lake offers good trout fishing.

20. Kiser Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Kiser Lake, Grand Mesa

Kiser Lake offers good trout fishing on the Grand Mesa.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at cpw.state.co.us.
  • Fish the Grand Mesa -- The Grand Mesa's 300+ alpine lakes offer incredible trout fishing. Drive to many of them or hike to remote lakes.
  • Try the Fryingpan -- The Fryingpan River is one of Colorado's premier gold medal trout streams. Focus on the catch-and-release section below Ruedi Dam.
  • Explore the Roaring Fork -- The Roaring Fork Valley offers excellent fishing from Aspen to Glenwood Springs with numerous access points.
  • Summer on Grand Mesa -- The Grand Mesa offers cool fishing (70s) when Grand Junction is over 100F. Perfect summer escape.
