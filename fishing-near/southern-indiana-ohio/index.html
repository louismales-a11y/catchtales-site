Fishing in Southern Indiana & Ohio River

Ohio River, Blue River, Hoosier National Forest, and the rivers and lakes of southern Indiana.

1. Ohio River (Sauger, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Ohio River, Indiana border

The Ohio River forms Indiana's southern border and offers world-class sauger and catfish fishing.

2. Blue River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie)

Blue River, Crawford/Harrison counties

The Blue River is one of Indiana's premier canoeing and smallmouth bass fishing streams.

3. Hoosier National Forest Ponds (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

Hoosier National Forest, Monroe/Brown counties

The Hoosier National Forest has numerous small ponds offering good fishing in a forest setting.

4. Lake Monroe (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Striped Bass, Catfish, Bluegill)

Lake Monroe, Monroe County

Lake Monroe is Indiana's largest lake at 10,750 acres, offering excellent striped bass and crappie fishing.

5. Patoka Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Muskie, Catfish, Walleye)

Patoka Lake, Dubois/Crawford counties

Patoka Lake is an 8,800-acre reservoir offering excellent muskie and bass fishing.

6. Scales Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Scales Lake, Warrick County

Scales Lake offers good fishing near Boonville.

7. Bluegrass Fish and Wildlife Area (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Bluegrass FWA, Warrick County

Bluegrass offers good fishing in its reclaimed surface-mine lakes.

8. Glendale Fish and Wildlife Area (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Glendale FWA, Daviess/Martin counties

Glendale offers good fishing in its strip-mine lakes.

9. Hasmer Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Hasmer Lake, Daviess County

Hasmer Lake offers good fishing.

10. Boonville Pond (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Boonville, Warrick County

Boonville Pond offers stocked trout fishing in winter.

11. Ohio River (Evansville) (Sauger, Catfish, White Bass, Crappie)

Ohio River, Evansville/Vanderburgh County

The Ohio River at Evansville offers excellent sauger and catfish fishing from the riverfront.

12. Ohio River (New Albany) (Sauger, Catfish, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Ohio River, New Albany/Floyd County

The Ohio River at New Albany and Louisville offers good fishing.

13. Ohio River (Madison) (Sauger, Catfish, White Bass)

Ohio River, Madison/Jefferson County

The Ohio River at Madison offers good fishing.

14. Ohio River (Lawrenceburg) (Sauger, Catfish, Crappie)

Ohio River, Lawrenceburg/Dearborn County

The Ohio River at Lawrenceburg offers good fishing near the Indiana-Ohio border.

15. Hovey Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Hovey Lake, Posey County

Hovey Lake offers good fishing in a backwater lake on the Ohio River.

16. Horseshoe Lake (IN) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Horseshoe Lake, Posey County

Horseshoe Lake offers good fishing.

17. Goose Pond FWA (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Goose Pond FWA, Greene County

Goose Pond offers good fishing in restored wetlands.

18. White River (Lower) (Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Saugeye)

Lower White River, Gibson/Posey counties

The lower White River offers excellent catfish fishing near its confluence with the Wabash.

19. Wabash River (Lower) (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Sauger, Crappie)

Lower Wabash River, Gibson/Posey counties

The lower Wabash River offers world-class flathead and channel catfish fishing.

20. Pigeon Creek (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Bluegill)

Pigeon Creek, Warrick/Spencer counties

Pigeon Creek offers good fishing near Evansville.

21. Anderson River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Anderson River, Perry County

The Anderson River offers good fishing in southern Indiana.

22. Little Blue River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Little Blue River, Crawford County

The Little Blue River offers good smallmouth fishing.

23. Indian Creek (IN) (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Indian Creek, Perry County

Indian Creek offers good fishing.

24. Big Creek (IN) (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Big Creek, Perry County

Big Creek offers good fishing.

25. Buck Creek (IN) (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Buck Creek, Crawford County

Buck Creek offers good fishing.

26. Lost River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Lost River, Orange County

The Lost River is a unique stream that goes underground and offers good fishing.

27. Lick Creek (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Lick Creek, Orange County

Lick Creek offers good fishing.

28. French Lick Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

French Lick Creek, Orange County

French Lick Creek offers stocked trout fishing near the French Lick resort.

29. Tipsaw Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Tipsaw Lake, Perry County

Tipsaw Lake offers good fishing in the Hoosier National Forest.

30. Saddle Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Saddle Lake, Perry County

Saddle Lake offers good fishing in the Hoosier National Forest.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at in.gov/dnr/fish.
  • Fish the Ohio River -- The Ohio River offers world-class sauger and catfish fishing. Spring sauger runs are legendary.
  • Try the Blue River -- The Blue River offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing in a scenic stream.
  • Hoosier National Forest -- The forest has numerous small ponds and streams offering quiet fishing.
  • Spring and fall are best -- March-May and September-November offer the best fishing in southern Indiana.
