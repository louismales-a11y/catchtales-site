Fishing in Southwestern Louisiana & Atchafalaya

Atchafalaya Basin, White Lake, Vermilion Bay, Marsh Island, and the heart of Cajun fishing country.

1. Atchafalaya Basin (Blue Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Sac-a-lait, Redfish)

Atchafalaya Basin, St. Martin/Iberville parishes

The Atchafalaya Basin is the nation's largest river swamp at 1.4 million acres, offering world-class catfish and bass fishing.

2. White Lake (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

White Lake, Vermilion Parish

White Lake offers excellent saltwater fishing in a large coastal lake near Gueydan.

3. Vermilion Bay (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead, Blue Crab)

Vermilion Bay, Iberia/Vermilion parishes

Vermilion Bay offers excellent inshore fishing on the Louisiana Gulf Coast.

4. Marsh Island (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

Marsh Island, Iberia Parish

Marsh Island offers excellent fishing in a remote coastal marsh.

5. Rockefeller Wildlife Refuge (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Drum)

Rockefeller Refuge, Cameron/Vermilion parishes

Rockefeller Wildlife Refuge offers excellent fishing in managed coastal marshes.

6. Freshwater Bayou (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Crabs)

Freshwater Bayou, Vermilion/Cameron parishes

Freshwater Bayou offers good inshore fishing.

7. Vermilion River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Redfish, Speckled Trout)

Vermilion River, Lafayette/Vermilion parishes

The Vermilion River offers diverse fishing from freshwater to saltwater through Lafayette.

8. Bayou Vermilion (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Sac-a-lait)

Bayou Vermilion, Lafayette Parish

Bayou Vermilion offers good urban fishing in Lafayette.

9. Mermentau River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Redfish)

Mermentau River, Acadia/Jefferson Davis parishes

The Mermentau River offers good fishing in southwestern Louisiana.

10. Calcasieu River (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Calcasieu River, Calcasieu/Cameron parishes

The Calcasieu River offers excellent fishing from freshwater to the Gulf.

11. Sabine Lake (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead, Largemouth Bass)

Sabine Lake, Cameron Parish

Sabine Lake offers excellent fishing on the Louisiana-Texas border.

12. Calcasieu Lake (Big Lake) (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Calcasieu Lake, Cameron Parish

Calcasieu Lake, known as "Big Lake," offers world-class speckled trout and redfish fishing.

13. Lacassine National Wildlife Refuge (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Lacassine NWR, Cameron Parish

Lacassine NWR offers good freshwater fishing in its managed wetlands.

14. Lac Blanc (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Lac Blanc, Vermilion Parish

Lac Blanc offers good fishing in a coastal marsh lake.

15. Bayou Queue de Tortue (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Sac-a-lait, Bream)

Bayou Queue de Tortue, Acadia/Lafayette parishes

Bayou Queue de Tortue offers good freshwater fishing.

16. Additional Bayou (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Additional Bayou, Louisiana

Additional Bayou offers good fishing in the region.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at wlf.louisiana.gov. Saltwater licence required for coastal areas.
  • Explore the Atchafalaya -- The Atchafalaya Basin offers unmatched fishing in America's largest river swamp.
  • Fish Calcasieu Lake -- Calcasieu Lake ("Big Lake") offers world-class speckled trout fishing.
  • Try Vermilion Bay -- Vermilion Bay offers excellent redfish and trout fishing in a beautiful coastal setting.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-November offer the best fishing.
