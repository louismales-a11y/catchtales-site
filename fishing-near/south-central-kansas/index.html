Fishing in South-Central Kansas & Wichita Area

Cheney Reservoir, El Dorado Lake, Kingman State Fishing Lake, Wichita area lakes, and the best fishing near Kansas' largest city.

1. Cheney Reservoir (Wiper, Walleye, Crappie, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish, White Bass)

Cheney Reservoir, Reno/Kingman counties

Cheney Reservoir is a 9,500-acre lake offering excellent wiper and walleye fishing, the primary water supply for Wichita.

2. El Dorado Lake (Wiper, Walleye, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass)

El Dorado Lake, Butler County

El Dorado Lake is an 8,000-acre reservoir offering excellent wiper and walleye fishing near Wichita.

3. Kingman State Fishing Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Walleye)

Kingman SFL, Kingman County

Kingman SFL offers good fishing near Kingman.

4. Lake Afton (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Lake Afton, Sedgwick County

Lake Afton is a popular Wichita-area lake offering winter trout stocking and warm-water fishing.

5. Sedgwick County Park Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Catfish)

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at ksoutdoors.com.
  • Fish Cheney Reservoir -- Cheney offers excellent wiper and walleye fishing near Wichita.
  • Try El Dorado Lake -- El Dorado offers excellent wiper fishing in the Flint Hills.
  • Urban fishing -- Wichita has numerous park lakes stocked with rainbow trout in winter.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in south-central Kansas.
