Fishing in North Louisiana

Toledo Bend, Lake Bistineau, Caney Lake, Black Bayou, and the premier bass and crappie waters of north Louisiana.

1. Toledo Bend Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, White Bass, Bream, Striped Bass)

Toledo Bend, Sabine/DeSoto parishes

Toledo Bend is the largest man-made lake in the South at 185,000 acres, offering world-class bass and crappie fishing on the Louisiana-Texas border.

2. Lake Bistineau (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, White Perch)

Lake Bistineau, Bossier/Webster parishes

Lake Bistineau is a 17,000-acre lake offering excellent bass and crappie fishing in a scenic cypress swamp setting.

3. Caney Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, Sac-a-lait)

Caney Lake, Jackson Parish

Caney Lake is a 5,000-acre reservoir known for excellent bass fishing and hosting major tournaments.

4. Black Bayou Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Black Bayou Lake, Bossier/Caddo parishes

Black Bayou Lake offers good fishing near Bossier City.

5. Cross Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, White Bass)

Cross Lake, Caddo Parish

Cross Lake is a 8,575-acre lake offering good fishing near Shreveport.

6. Caddo Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, White Bass)

Caddo Lake, Caddo County

Caddo Lake is a 25,400-acre lake and bayou system offering excellent fishing in a unique cypress swamp.

7. Lake Claiborne (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, White Bass)

Lake Claiborne, Claiborne Parish

Lake Claiborne is a 6,400-acre lake offering good bass and crappie fishing.

8. Lake D'Arbonne (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, Sac-a-lait)

Lake D'Arbonne, Union Parish

Lake D'Arbonne is a 15,250-acre lake offering excellent crappie and bass fishing.

9. Bussey Brake Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Bussey Brake, Morehouse Parish

Bussey Brake offers good fishing near Bastrop.

10. Cheniere Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream, Catfish)

Cheniere Lake, Ouachita Parish

Cheniere Lake offers good fishing near West Monroe.

11. Lafourche Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bream)

Lafourche Lake, Lafourche Parish

Lafourche Lake offers good fishing.

12. Red River (Upper) (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Upper Red River, Bossier/Caddo parishes

The upper Red River offers excellent catfish fishing through north Louisiana.

13. Sabine River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bream)

Sabine River, Sabine/Vernon parishes

The Sabine River offers good fishing along the Louisiana-Texas border.

14. Ouachita River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bream, Striped Bass)

Ouachita River, Ouachita/Morehouse parishes

The Ouachita River offers excellent catfish and bass fishing.

15. Bayou D'Arbonne (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bream)

Bayou D'Arbonne, Union Parish

Bayou D'Arbonne offers good fishing in north Louisiana.

16. Additional Bayou (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Additional Bayou, Louisiana

Additional Bayou offers good fishing in the region.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at wlf.louisiana.gov.
  • Fish Toledo Bend -- Toledo Bend is one of the best bass lakes in the country. Spring and fall are peak.
  • Try Lake Bistineau -- Lake Bistineau offers excellent crappie fishing in a beautiful cypress swamp.
  • Sac-a-lait (crappie) -- Louisiana's term for crappie. Spring is the best time for sac-a-lait fishing.
  • Check Toledo Bend guides -- Numerous guides operate on Toledo Bend for bass and crappie.
