Fishing in North Central Mississippi

Pickwick Lake, Bay Springs Lake, Tenn-Tom Waterway, Aberdeen Lake, and the bass, crappie, and striped bass fishing of northeast Mississippi.

1. Pickwick Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Striped Bass, White Bass, Catfish)

Pickwick Lake, Tishomingo County

Pickwick Lake on the Tennessee River border offers excellent fishing for smallmouth bass, striped bass, and crappie in Mississippi's northeast corner.

2. Bay Springs Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Striped Bass, White Bass, Catfish)

Bay Springs Lake, Tishomingo/Prentiss counties

Bay Springs Lake is a 6,600-acre lake on the Tenn-Tom Waterway offering excellent bass and crappie fishing.

3. Aberdeen Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Bluegill, Striped Bass)

Aberdeen Lake, Monroe County

Aberdeen Lake is a 5,800-acre lake on the Tenn-Tom Waterway offering excellent fishing.

4. Columbus Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Bluegill, Striped Bass)

Columbus Lake, Lowndes County

Columbus Lake offers good fishing on the Tenn-Tom Waterway near Columbus.

5. Aliceville Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Bluegill, Striped Bass)

Aliceville Lake, Pickens County (AL/MS border)

Aliceville Lake on the Mississippi-Alabama border offers good fishing.

6. Tombigbee River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Blue Catfish, Striped Bass)

Tombigbee River, Itawamba/Monroe counties

The Tombigbee River flows through northeast Mississippi and offers excellent catfish and bass fishing.

7. Tennessee River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Striped Bass, Sauger, Catfish)

Tennessee River, Tishomingo County

The Tennessee River along Mississippi's northeast border offers excellent smallmouth bass and sauger fishing.

8. Buttahatchie River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, Blue Catfish)

Buttahatchie River, Itawamba/Monroe counties

The Buttahatchie River offers good fishing.

9. Luxapalila Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Luxapalila Creek, Lowndes County

Luxapalila Creek offers good fishing near Columbus.

10. Yellow Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Yellow Creek, Tishomingo County

Yellow Creek offers good fishing near Pickwick Lake.

11. Bear Creek (Tishomingo) (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Bear Creek, Tishomingo County

Bear Creek offers good fishing.

12. Mackeys Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Mackeys Creek, Tishomingo County

Mackeys Creek offers good fishing.

13. J.P. Coleman SP Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

J.P. Coleman SP, Tishomingo County

J.P. Coleman State Park offers good fishing on Pickwick Lake.

14. Trace State Park Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish)

Trace SP, Pontotoc County

Trace State Park Lake offers good family fishing.

15. Lake Pontotoc (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Pontotoc, Pontotoc County

Lake Pontotoc offers good fishing.

16. Lake Tippo (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Tippo offers good fishing.

Lake George, Tallahatchie County

Lake George offers good fishing.

18. Okatibbee Creek (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Okatibbee Creek, Lauderdale County

Okatibbee Creek offers good fishing.

19. Chunky River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Chunky River, Lauderdale County

The Chunky River offers good fishing.

20. Okatibbee Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish)

Okatibbee Lake, Lauderdale County

Okatibbee Lake offers good fishing near Meridian.

21. Lake Claude Bennett (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Claude Bennett, Lauderdale County

Upper Pearl River, Neshoba/Leake counties

26. Lake Anita (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Ross Barnett Reservoir, Rankin/Madison counties

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at mdwfp.com.
  • Fish Pickwick Lake -- Pickwick offers excellent smallmouth bass and striped bass fishing on the Tennessee River.
  • Try the Tenn-Tom -- The Tennessee-Tombigbee Waterway offers excellent bass and crappie fishing.
  • Ross Barnett Reservoir -- The Rez near Jackson offers excellent striped bass and crappie fishing.
  • Spring and fall are best -- March-May and September-November offer the best fishing.
