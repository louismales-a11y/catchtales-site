Fishing in Northern Maine

Moosehead Lake, Chesuncook Lake, Allagash River, St. John River, and the vast wilderness of northern Maine.

1. Moosehead Lake (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike)

Moosehead Lake, Piscataquis County

Moosehead Lake is Maine's largest lake at 74,890 acres, offering world-class lake trout and landlocked salmon fishing.

2. Chesuncook Lake (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout, Northern Pike)

Chesuncook Lake, Piscataquis County

Chesuncook Lake is a 23,000-acre lake offering excellent coldwater fishing in the Maine wilderness.

3. Allagash River (Brook Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Lake Trout, Northern Pike)

Allagash River, Piscataquis/Aroostook counties

The Allagash River is a designated Wild and Scenic River offering excellent brook trout fishing in the Allagash Wilderness Waterway.

4. St. John River (Brook Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass)

St. John River, Aroostook County

The St. John River forms the Maine-New Brunswick border and offers excellent brook trout and landlocked salmon fishing.

5. Chamberlain Lake (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout)

Chamberlain Lake, Piscataquis County

Chamberlain Lake offers excellent lake trout and salmon fishing in the Allagash region.

6. Eagle Lake (ME) (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout)

Eagle Lake, Piscataquis County

Eagle Lake offers good coldwater fishing in the Moosehead region.

7. Seboomook Lake (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout)

Seboomook Lake, Somerset County

Seboomook Lake offers good fishing near the headwaters of the Kennebec River.

8. Brassua Lake (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Brassua Lake, Somerset County

Brassua Lake offers good fishing near Moosehead.

9. Long Pond (ME) (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout)

Long Pond, Piscataquis County

Long Pond offers good fishing.

10. Roach Pond (Brook Trout, Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon)

Roach Pond, Piscataquis County

Roach Pond offers good coldwater fishing.

11. Millinocket Lake (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout)

Millinocket Lake, Penobscot County

Millinocket Lake offers good fishing near Baxter State Park.

12. Ambajejus Lake (Lake Trout, Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout)

Ambajejus Lake, Penobscot County

Ambajejus Lake offers good fishing near Millinocket.

13. Penobscot River (West Branch) (Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout, Lake Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

West Branch Penobscot, Piscataquis County

The West Branch of the Penobscot River offers excellent landlocked salmon fishing.

14. East Branch Penobscot River (Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout, Lake Trout)

East Branch Penobscot, Penobscot County

The East Branch offers good fishing in a remote setting.

15. Matagamon Lake (Landlocked Salmon, Brook Trout, Lake Trout)

Matagamon Lake, Penobscot County

Matagamon Lake offers good fishing near Baxter State Park.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at maine.gov/ifw.
  • Fish Moosehead Lake -- Moosehead Lake offers world-class lake trout and landlocked salmon fishing.
  • Explore the Allagash -- The Allagash Wilderness Waterway offers remote brook trout fishing in a pristine setting.
  • Spring and fall are best -- May-June and September offer the best fishing in northern Maine.
  • Remote wilderness -- Northern Maine offers true wilderness fishing. Be prepared for remote conditions and bring supplies.
