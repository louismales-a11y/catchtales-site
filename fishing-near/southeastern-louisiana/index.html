Fishing in Southeastern Louisiana

Lake Pontchartrain, Pearl River, Honey Island Swamp, and the New Orleans area fishing.

1. Lake Pontchartrain (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Sheepshead)

Lake Pontchartrain, Orleans/Jefferson/St. Tammany parishes

Lake Pontchartrain is a massive 630,000-acre brackish lake offering world-class speckled trout and redfish fishing right at New Orleans' doorstep.

2. Pearl River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bream, Redfish, Sac-a-lait)

Pearl River, St. Tammany/Washington parishes

The Pearl River offers excellent bass and catfish fishing in a scenic river forming the Louisiana-Mississippi border.

3. Honey Island Swamp (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, Sac-a-lait)

Honey Island Swamp, St. Tammany Parish

Honey Island Swamp offers excellent fishing in a pristine swamp ecosystem near Slidell.

4. Lake Borgne (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead, Black Drum)

Lake Borgne, St. Bernard Parish

Lake Borgne offers excellent saltwater fishing east of New Orleans.

5. Bayou Sauvage (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Blue Crab, Flounder)

Bayou Sauvage NWR, Orleans Parish

Bayou Sauvage National Wildlife Refuge offers excellent fishing in New Orleans' largest urban wildlife refuge.

6. Tchefuncte River (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Tchefuncte River, St. Tammany Parish

The Tchefuncte River offers good fishing near Mandeville and Covington.

7. Tangipahoa River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Bream, Crappie)

Tangipahoa River, Tangipahoa Parish

The Tangipahoa River offers good freshwater fishing near Hammond.

8. Tickfaw River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bream)

Tickfaw River, Tangipahoa/Livingston parishes

The Tickfaw River offers good fishing in southeastern Louisiana.

9. Amite River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bream)

Amite River, East Baton Rouge/Livingston parishes

The Amite River offers good fishing near Baton Rouge.

10. Blind River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Sac-a-lait, Redfish)

Blind River, St. James/St. John parishes

Blind River offers good fishing in a scenic swamp near the Mississippi River.

11. Louisiana Cajun Bayous (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Crabs)

Cajun Bayous, Lafourche/Terrebonne parishes

The Cajun bayous offer excellent saltwater fishing in the brackish marshes.

12. Delacroix Island (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Delacroix, St. Bernard Parish

Delacroix Island is a legendary fishing destination in the marsh east of New Orleans.

13. Hopedale (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Drum)

Hopedale, St. Bernard Parish

Hopedale offers excellent marsh fishing in the Mississippi River delta.

14. Shell Beach (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Crabs)

Shell Beach, St. Bernard Parish

Shell Beach offers good fishing in the Lake Borgne area.

15. Mississippi River (New Orleans) (Blue Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Drum)

Mississippi River, Orleans/Jefferson parishes

The Mississippi River through New Orleans offers excellent blue catfish fishing from the riverfront.

16. Additional Bayou (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Additional Bayou, Louisiana

Additional Bayou offers good fishing in the region.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at wlf.louisiana.gov. Saltwater licence required.
  • Fish Lake Pontchartrain -- Lake Pontchartrain offers world-class speckled trout and redfish fishing. The Causeway area is particularly productive.
  • Try the Pearl River -- The Pearl River offers excellent bass fishing in a scenic setting.
  • Marsh fishing -- The Delacroix and Hopedale areas offer some of the best marsh fishing in Louisiana.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-November offer the best fishing.
