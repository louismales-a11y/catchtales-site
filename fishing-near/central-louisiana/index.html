Fishing in Central Louisiana

Red River, Lake Verret, Grand Lake, Catahoula Lake, Chicot Lake, and the heart of Louisiana fishing.

1. Red River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Blue Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass)

Red River, central Louisiana

The Red River through central Louisiana offers world-class blue catfish and flathead catfish fishing.

2. Lake Verret (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, Sac-a-lait)

Lake Verret, Assumption Parish

Lake Verret is a 14,300-acre lake offering excellent sac-a-lait (crappie) and bass fishing.

3. Grand Lake (Chitimacha) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, Redfish)

Grand Lake, St. Mary Parish

Grand Lake offers excellent freshwater and brackish fishing in the Atchafalaya Basin region.

4. Catahoula Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream, White Bass)

Catahoula Lake, LaSalle/Catahoula parishes

Catahoula Lake is a 30,000-acre natural lake offering excellent fishing with fluctuating water levels.

5. Lake Chicot (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Lake Chicot, Evangeline Parish

Lake Chicot is a 2,000-acre lake offering good bass and crappie fishing near Ville Platte.

6. False River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Sac-a-lait, White Bass)

False River, Pointe Coupee Parish

False River is a 13-mile long oxbow lake off the Mississippi offering excellent fishing near New Roads.

7. Palourde Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bream)

Palourde Lake, Assumption Parish

Palourde Lake offers good fishing in the Verret Basin.

8. Lake Maurepas (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Maurepas, Livingston/Tangipahoa parishes

Lake Maurepas offers both freshwater and saltwater fishing in a large lake near Lake Pontchartrain.

9. Lake Pontchartrain (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Lake Pontchartrain, Orleans/Jefferson parishes

Lake Pontchartrain is a 630,000-acre brackish lake offering world-class speckled trout and redfish fishing.

10. Atchafalaya River (Blue Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass)

Atchafalaya River, St. Martin/Iberville parishes

The Atchafalaya River offers excellent catfish fishing in the nation's largest river swamp.

11. Bayou Lafourche (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Bayou Lafourche, Lafourche Parish

Bayou Lafourche offers diverse fishing from freshwater to saltwater.

12. Bayou Teche (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Bream, Redfish)

Bayou Teche, St. Martin/Iberia parishes

Bayou Teche offers good fishing in the heart of Cajun country.

13. Bayou Courtableau (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, Sac-a-lait)

Bayou Courtableau, St. Landry Parish

Bayou Courtableau offers good fishing in the Atchafalaya Basin.

14. Bayou Cocodrie (Largemouth Bass, Sac-a-lait, Catfish, Bream)

Bayou Cocodrie, Concordia Parish

Bayou Cocodrie offers good fishing near Ferriday.

15. Old River (Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie, White Bass)

Old River, Concordia Parish

Old River offers good fishing in the Mississippi River floodplain.

16. Additional Bayou (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Additional Bayou, Louisiana

Additional Bayou offers good fishing in the region.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at wlf.louisiana.gov.
  • Fish the Red River -- The Red River offers world-class blue catfish fishing.
  • Try False River -- False River offers excellent sac-a-lait and bass fishing in a unique oxbow lake.
  • Lake Pontchartrain -- Lake Pontchartrain offers excellent speckled trout and redfish fishing.
  • Spring sac-a-lait -- March through May offers the best crappie (sac-a-lait) fishing in Louisiana.
