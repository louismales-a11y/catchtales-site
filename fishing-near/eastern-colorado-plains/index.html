Fishing in Eastern Colorado & Plains

Pueblo Reservoir, John Martin Lake, Arkansas River, Nee Noshe, and the warm-water fisheries of eastern Colorado.

1. Pueblo Reservoir (Walleye, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Pueblo Reservoir, Pueblo County

Pueblo Reservoir is a 4,500-acre reservoir offering excellent walleye, bass, and crappie fishing, one of the best warm-water fisheries in Colorado.

2. John Martin Reservoir (Walleye, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

John Martin Reservoir, Bent County

John Martin Reservoir offers excellent walleye and bass fishing in southeastern Colorado.

3. Arkansas River (Lower) (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Lower Arkansas River, Pueblo to Kansas

The lower Arkansas River offers good warm-water fishing for walleye, bass, and catfish across the eastern plains.

4. Nee Noshe Reservoir (Walleye, Crappie, Catfish)

Nee Noshe Reservoir, Kiowa County

Nee Noshe Reservoir offers good walleye and crappie fishing on the eastern plains.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at cpw.state.co.us.
  • Fish Pueblo for walleye -- Pueblo Reservoir is one of Colorado's best walleye lakes. Spring and fall offer the best action.
  • Try John Martin for bass -- John Martin Reservoir offers excellent largemouth bass fishing in sheltered coves.
  • Watch for wind -- Eastern plains lakes can get very windy. Fish early morning for the calmest conditions.
  • Summer fishing -- Eastern Colorado can exceed 100F in summer. Fish early morning or evening for best results.
