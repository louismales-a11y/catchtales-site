Fishing in Central Iowa & Des Moines Area

Saylorville Lake, Red Rock Reservoir, Big Creek Lake, Des Moines River, and the best fishing near Iowa's capital.

1. Saylorville Lake (Walleye, Crappie, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Saugeye)

Saylorville Lake, Polk County

Saylorville Lake is a 5,700-acre reservoir on the Des Moines River offering excellent walleye, crappie, and saugeye fishing near Des Moines.

2. Red Rock Reservoir (Walleye, Crappie, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Saugeye, White Bass)

Red Rock Reservoir, Marion County

Red Rock Reservoir is a 15,000-acre flood-control lake offering excellent walleye and saugeye fishing.

3. Big Creek Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Walleye)

Big Creek Lake, Polk County

Big Creek Lake is a 866-acre lake offering excellent bass and crappie fishing at Big Creek State Park.

4. Des Moines River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye)

Des Moines River, central Iowa

The Des Moines River offers excellent channel catfish and smallmouth bass fishing through central Iowa.

5. Raccoon River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Walleye)

Raccoon River, Dallas/Polk counties

The Raccoon River offers good smallmouth bass and catfish fishing near Des Moines.

6. Boone River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish)

Boone River, Hamilton/Wright counties

The Boone River offers good smallmouth bass fishing.

7. North Skunk River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

North Skunk River, Jasper/Mahaska counties

The North Skunk River offers good fishing.

8. South Skunk River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

South Skunk River, Story/Polk counties

The South Skunk River offers good fishing through Ames.

9. Lake Petocka (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

10. Lake Ahquabi (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

11. Lake Keomah (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

12. Lake Wapello (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

13. Rathbun Reservoir (Walleye, Crappie, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Saugeye)

Rathbun Reservoir, Appanoose/Wayne counties

Rathbun Reservoir is an 11,000-acre lake offering excellent walleye and crappie fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at iowadnr.gov/fishing.
  • Fish Saylorville Lake -- Saylorville offers excellent walleye and saugeye fishing near Des Moines.
  • Try Red Rock Reservoir -- Red Rock offers excellent walleye and saugeye fishing in a large reservoir.
  • Urban fishing -- Des Moines has several urban lakes and the Des Moines River for easy-access fishing.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in central Iowa.
