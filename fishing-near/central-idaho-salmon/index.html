Fishing in Central Idaho & Salmon River

Salmon River, Clearwater River, Lochsa River, Selway River, and the heart of Idaho's steelhead and salmon country.

1. Salmon River (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Sturgeon)

Salmon River, central Idaho

The Salmon River, known as the "River of No Return," is one of the premier steelhead and Chinook salmon rivers in the US, offering incredible fishing through the Frank Church River of No Return Wilderness.

2. Clearwater River (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Clearwater River, north-central Idaho

The Clearwater River offers world-class steelhead and Chinook salmon fishing in north-central Idaho.

3. Middle Fork Salmon River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Chinook Salmon)

Middle Fork Salmon River, Frank Church Wilderness

The Middle Fork Salmon is a designated National Wild and Scenic River offering spectacular trout fishing in a remote wilderness canyon.

4. Lochsa River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon)

Lochsa River, Idaho County

The Lochsa River offers excellent trout and steelhead fishing along the Lewis and Clark Highway.

5. Selway River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Steelhead)

Selway River, Idaho County

The Selway River is a designated Wild and Scenic River offering excellent trout and steelhead fishing in a remote wilderness.

6. Little Salmon River (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Rainbow Trout)

Little Salmon River, Adams County

The Little Salmon River offers good steelhead and salmon fishing south of Riggins.

7. South Fork Salmon River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Chinook Salmon)

South Fork Salmon River, Valley County

The South Fork Salmon offers good trout and salmon fishing in the Payette National Forest.

8. Payette River (Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Steelhead, Catfish)

Payette River, Valley County

The Payette River offers diverse fishing from trout in the upper reaches to bass and catfish in the lower river.

9. North Fork Clearwater River (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Rainbow Trout)

North Fork Clearwater, Clearwater County

The North Fork Clearwater offers excellent steelhead fishing.

10. Dworshak Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye)

Dworshak Reservoir, Clearwater County

Dworshak Reservoir is a 17,000-acre impoundment offering excellent trout, kokanee, and smallmouth bass fishing.

11. Lake Cascade (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Perch)

Lake Cascade, Valley County

Lake Cascade offers excellent trout, kokanee, and perch fishing in west-central Idaho.

12. Warm Lake (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Lake Trout)

Warm Lake, Valley County

Warm Lake offers good trout and kokanee fishing in the Boise National Forest.

13. McCall Area Lakes (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Lake Trout, Brook Trout)

McCall, Valley County

The McCall area has numerous excellent fishing lakes including Payette Lake, Little Payette Lake, and Ponderosa State Park waters.

14. Secesh River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Secesh River, Valley County

The Secesh River offers good trout fishing in the Payette National Forest.

15. Snake River (Hells Canyon Reach) (Smallmouth Bass, Sturgeon, Catfish, Rainbow Trout)

Snake River, Hells Canyon, Idaho/Oregon border

The Snake River through Hells Canyon offers excellent smallmouth bass and white sturgeon fishing in the deepest canyon in North America.

16. Alturas Lake (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Lake Trout)

Alturas Lake, Blaine County

Alturas Lake offers excellent trout and kokanee fishing in the Sawtooth Mountains.

17. Redfish Lake (Sockeye Salmon, Rainbow Trout, Kokanee)

Redfish Lake, Custer County

Redfish Lake is famous for its sockeye salmon recovery program and offers excellent trout fishing.

18. Stanley Lake (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee, Lake Trout)

Stanley Lake, Custer County

Stanley Lake offers good trout fishing at the base of the Sawtooth Mountains.

19. Little Redfish Lake (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Little Redfish Lake, Custer County

Little Redfish Lake offers good trout fishing in a beautiful setting.

20. Pettit Lake (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee)

Pettit Lake, Blaine County

Pettit Lake offers good trout and kokanee fishing in the Sawtooth Valley.

21. Yellow Belly Lake (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee)

Yellow Belly Lake, Blaine County

Yellow Belly Lake offers good trout fishing.

22. Fisher Creek (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Fisher Creek, Custer County

Fisher Creek offers good trout fishing in the Sawtooth area.

23. Valley Creek (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Valley Creek, Custer County

Valley Creek offers good trout fishing near Stanley.

24. Salmon River (Upper) (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Brook Trout)

Upper Salmon River, Custer County

The upper Salmon River offers excellent trout fishing in the Sawtooth Valley.

25. North Fork Payette River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

North Fork Payette River, Valley County

The North Fork Payette River offers good trout fishing.

26. South Fork Payette River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

South Fork Payette River, Boise County

The South Fork Payette offers good trout fishing in the Boise National Forest.

27. Deadwood River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Deadwood River, Valley County

The Deadwood River offers good trout fishing in remote wilderness.

28. East Fork Salmon River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Brook Trout)

East Fork Salmon, Custer County

The East Fork Salmon River offers good trout fishing in the Lemhi Range.

29. Lemhi River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Lemhi River, Lemhi County

The Lemhi River offers good trout fishing near Salmon.

30. Pahsimeroi River (Rainbow Trout, Cutthroat Trout, Brook Trout)

Big Lost River, Custer County

32. Little Lost River (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

33. Kelly Lake (Rainbow Trout, Kokanee)

34. Upper Payette Lake (Rainbow Trout, Lake Trout)

Upper Payette Lake, Valley County

35. Louie Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Louie Lake, Valley County

Louie Lake offers good trout fishing near McCall.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at idfg.idaho.gov. Steelhead and salmon permits required.
  • Fish the Salmon River -- The Salmon River offers world-class steelhead and Chinook salmon fishing. Fall and spring are peak steelhead seasons.
  • Try the Middle Fork -- The Middle Fork Salmon River offers incredible wilderness trout fishing accessible by float trips.
  • Check steelhead regulations -- Steelhead seasons vary by river section. Check current regulations before planning.
  • Summer steelhead -- Summer steelhead (B-run) on the Clearwater River can exceed 20 pounds.
