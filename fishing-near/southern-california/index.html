Fishing in Southern California

Pyramid Lake, Castaic Lake, San Diego County lakes, offshore fishing, and the diverse waters of Southern California.

1. Pyramid Lake (Striped Bass, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Pyramid Lake, Los Angeles County

Pyramid Lake offers excellent striped bass and largemouth bass fishing in the Angeles National Forest.

2. Castaic Lake (Striped Bass, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Castaic Lake, Los Angeles County

Castaic Lake is famous for striped bass and largemouth bass fishing in northern Los Angeles County.

3. Silverwood Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Striped Bass)

Silverwood Lake, San Bernardino County

Silverwood Lake offers trout, bass, and striped bass fishing in the San Bernardino Mountains.

4. Lake Perris (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Perris, Riverside County

Lake Perris offers trout and bass fishing in a state recreation area near Riverside.

5. Big Bear Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Big Bear Lake, San Bernardino County

Big Bear Lake offers excellent trout and bass fishing at 7,000 feet in the San Bernardino Mountains.

6. Lake Arrowhead (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee)

Lake Arrowhead, San Bernardino County

Lake Arrowhead offers trout and kokanee fishing in the San Bernardino Mountains.

7. Lake Skinner (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Lake Skinner, Riverside County

Lake Skinner offers good trout and bass fishing near Temecula.

8. Lake Hemet (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Lake Hemet, Riverside County

Lake Hemet offers good trout and bass fishing in the San Jacinto Mountains.

9. Lake Elsinore (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Elsinore, Riverside County

Lake Elsinore is Southern California's largest natural lake, offering good bass and catfish fishing.

10. Diamond Valley Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Striped Bass, Crappie)

Diamond Valley Lake, Riverside County

Diamond Valley Lake is a 4,500-acre reservoir offering excellent trout, bass, and striped bass fishing.

11. Lake San Antonio (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Striped Bass)

Lake San Antonio, Monterey County

Lake San Antonio offers good trout and bass fishing in southern Monterey County.

12. Nacimiento Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, White Bass)

Nacimiento Lake, Monterey County

Nacimiento Lake offers good trout and bass fishing.

13. Lake Cachuma (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Lake Cachuma, Santa Barbara County

Lake Cachuma offers good trout and bass fishing in the Santa Ynez Valley.

14. Casitas Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Lake Casitas, Ventura County

Lake Casitas offers excellent bass and trout fishing near Ojai.

15. Long Beach / San Pedro (Rockfish, Lingcod, Halibut, Mackerel)

Long Beach / San Pedro, Los Angeles County

The Long Beach area offers excellent saltwater fishing from party boats and piers.

16. San Diego Bay (Halibut, Spotted Bay Bass, Mackerel, Bonito)

San Diego Bay, San Diego County

San Diego Bay offers excellent saltwater fishing for halibut, spotted bay bass, and mackerel.

17. Oceanside Harbor (Halibut, Rockfish, Mackerel)

Oceanside, San Diego County

Oceanside offers good saltwater fishing from the harbor and pier.

18. Channel Islands (Rockfish, Lingcod, Yellowtail, White Seabass)

Channel Islands, Ventura County

The Channel Islands offer world-class saltwater fishing for rockfish, lingcod, yellowtail, and white seabass.

19. Catalina Island (Yellowtail, White Seabass, Calico Bass, Rockfish)

Catalina Island, Los Angeles County

Catalina Island offers excellent saltwater fishing in a beautiful island setting.

20. Salton Sea (Corvina, Croaker, Catfish)

Salton Sea, Imperial/Riverside counties

The Salton Sea offers unique fishing for corvina, croaker, and catfish in California's largest lake.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at wildlife.ca.gov. Ocean Enhancement Validation required for saltwater.
  • Fish the local lakes -- Southern California's reservoirs offer excellent bass and trout fishing year-round.
  • Try offshore fishing -- San Diego and Long Beach have excellent party boats targeting yellowtail, tuna, and rockfish.
  • Winter trout stocking -- Many Southern California lakes are stocked with rainbow trout from November through March.
  • Watch for heat -- Summer temperatures in Southern California can exceed 100F. Fish early morning for best results.
