Fishing in Saginaw Bay & Thumb Region

Saginaw Bay, Lake Huron, Cass River, Port Austin, and the walleye, perch, and bass fishing of Michigan's Thumb.

1. Saginaw Bay (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish, Chinook Salmon)

Saginaw Bay, Bay/Arenac counties

Saginaw Bay is one of the premier walleye fisheries in the Great Lakes, with a world-class walleye population and excellent perch fishing.

2. Lake Huron (Lake Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Rainbow Trout, Walleye, Smallmouth Bass)

Lake Huron, Thumb coast

Lake Huron offers excellent salmon, trout, and walleye fishing along Michigan's Thumb coast from Port Huron to Cheboygan.

3. Saginaw River (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Northern Pike, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Saginaw River, Saginaw/Bay counties

The Saginaw River flows through the heart of the Saginaw Valley and offers excellent walleye and catfish fishing.

4. Cass River (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish, Crappie, Bluegill)

Cass River, Tuscola County

The Cass River offers good walleye and smallmouth bass fishing in the Thumb region.

5. Flint River (Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Flint River, Genesee/Lapeer counties

The Flint River offers good smallmouth bass and walleye fishing in the lower Thumb region.

6. Shiawassee River (Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Shiawassee River, Saginaw County

The Shiawassee River offers good fishing in the Saginaw Valley.

7. Port Austin Reef (Walleye, Lake Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Chinook Salmon)

Port Austin, Huron County

Port Austin Reef at the tip of the Thumb offers excellent walleye and lake trout fishing in Lake Huron.

8. Caseville Harbor (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike)

Caseville, Huron County

Caseville Harbor on Saginaw Bay offers excellent walleye and perch fishing.

9. Sebewaing River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Sebewaing River, Huron County

The Sebewaing River offers good fishing flowing into Saginaw Bay.

10. Pinconning River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Pinconning River, Bay County

The Pinconning River offers good fishing near Saginaw Bay.

11. Tawas Point (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Lake Trout, Chinook Salmon)

Tawas Point, Iosco County

Tawas Point on Lake Huron offers excellent walleye and smallmouth bass fishing at Tawas Bay.

12. Au Gres River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Au Gres River, Arenac County

The Au Gres River offers good fishing flowing into Saginaw Bay.

13. Rifle River (Arenac) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Rifle River, Arenac County

The Rifle River offers good walleye and pike fishing near Saginaw Bay.

14. Harbor Beach (Walleye, Lake Trout, Chinook Salmon, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Harbor Beach, Huron County

Harbor Beach offers excellent fishing on Lake Huron with a protected harbor.

15. Lexington Harbor (Walleye, Lake Trout, Chinook Salmon, Smallmouth Bass)

Lexington, Sanilac County

Lexington Harbor offers good fishing on Lake Huron in the southern Thumb.

16. Port Sanilac (Walleye, Lake Trout, Chinook Salmon, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Port Sanilac, Sanilac County

Port Sanilac offers good fishing on Lake Huron.

17. Croswell (Lake Huron) (Walleye, Lake Trout, Chinook Salmon, Smallmouth Bass)

Croswell, Sanilac County

Croswell offers good fishing on Lake Huron at the mouth of the Black River.

18. Black River (Sanilac) (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye)

Black River, Sanilac County

The Black River offers good steelhead and salmon fishing in the Thumb region.

19. St. Clair River (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Lake Trout, Muskellunge, Northern Pike)

St. Clair River, St. Clair County

The St. Clair River connects Lake Huron to Lake St. Clair and offers world-class walleye and smallmouth bass fishing.

20. Lake St. Clair (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Muskellunge, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike)

Lake St. Clair, Macomb/St. Clair counties

Lake St. Clair is one of the best walleye and muskie fisheries in North America, with world-class smallmouth bass fishing.

21. Anchor Bay (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch, Muskellunge)

Anchor Bay, Macomb County

Anchor Bay on Lake St. Clair offers excellent walleye and muskie fishing.

22. Mitchell Bay (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Mitchell Bay, St. Clair County

Mitchell Bay offers good fishing on Lake St. Clair.

23. Harsens Island (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Muskellunge, Yellow Perch)

Harsens Island, St. Clair County

Harsens Island at the mouth of the St. Clair River offers excellent fishing.

24. Algonac Harbor (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Muskellunge, Yellow Perch)

Algonac, St. Clair County

Algonac Harbor on the St. Clair River offers good fishing.

25. Marine City (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Marine City, St. Clair County

Marine City offers good fishing on the St. Clair River.

26. Pearl Beach (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike)

Pearl Beach, St. Clair County

Pearl Beach offers good fishing on Lake Huron at the St. Clair River's head.

27. Port Huron (Walleye, Lake Trout, Chinook Salmon, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Port Huron, St. Clair County

Port Huron at the base of Lake Huron offers excellent salmon, walleye, and bass fishing at the Blue Water Bridge.

28. Fort Gratiot (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Chinook Salmon, Walleye, Smallmouth Bass)

Fort Gratiot, St. Clair County

Fort Gratiot offers good fishing on Lake Huron near Port Huron.

29. Lakeport (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike)

Lakeport, St. Clair County

Lakeport offers good fishing on Lake Huron.

30. Marysville (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Marysville, St. Clair County

Marysville offers good fishing on the St. Clair River.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at Michigan.gov/DNR.
  • Fish Saginaw Bay -- Saginaw Bay is one of the best walleye fisheries in the Great Lakes.
  • Try Lake St. Clair -- Lake St. Clair offers world-class muskie and smallmouth bass fishing.
  • Port Austin Reef -- The reef at Port Austin is famous for walleye and lake trout.
  • Spring and fall -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in the Thumb region.
