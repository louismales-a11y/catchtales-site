Fishing in North Shore & Merrimack Valley

Merrimack River, Plum Island, Ipswich Bay, Parker River National Wildlife Refuge, and North Shore fishing.

1. Merrimack River (Striped Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, White Perch, Catfish)

Merrimack River, Essex County

The Merrimack River is the largest river in Massachusetts, offering excellent striped bass fishing in spring and smallmouth bass, catfish, and white perch fishing year-round.

2. Plum Island (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Surf Perch)

Plum Island, Newburyport

Plum Island offers world-class surf fishing for striped bass along miles of pristine beaches.

3. Parker River NWR (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Surf Perch)

Parker River NWR, Newburyport

Parker River National Wildlife Refuge on Plum Island offers excellent surf fishing in a protected natural setting.

4. Ipswich Bay (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Tautog, Lobster)

Ipswich Bay, Essex/Ipswich

Ipswich Bay offers excellent fishing for striped bass and bluefish.

5. Crane Beach (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder)

Crane Beach, Ipswich

Crane Beach offers excellent surf fishing on the North Shore.

6. Salisbury Beach (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Surf Perch)

Salisbury Beach, Salisbury

Salisbury Beach offers good surf fishing near the New Hampshire border.

7. Nahant Beach (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder)

Nahant Beach, Nahant

Nahant Beach offers good fishing near Boston.

8. Lynn Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Tautog)

Lynn Harbor, Lynn

Lynn Harbor offers good fishing on the North Shore.

9. Swampscott Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder)

Swampscott Harbor, Swampscott

Swampscott Harbor offers good fishing on the North Shore.

10. Marblehead Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Tautog)

Marblehead Harbor, Marblehead

Marblehead Harbor offers good fishing on the North Shore.

11. Salem Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Mackerel)

Salem Harbor, Salem

Salem Harbor offers good fishing for striped bass and bluefish.

12. Beverly Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder)

Beverly Harbor, Beverly

Beverly Harbor offers good fishing on the North Shore.

13. Manchester Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Flounder, Tautog)

Manchester Harbor, Manchester-by-the-Sea

Manchester Harbor offers good fishing on the North Shore.

14. Gloucester Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Mackerel, Cod, Haddock)

Gloucester Harbor, Gloucester

Gloucester Harbor offers excellent fishing in America's oldest fishing port, with both inshore and offshore opportunities.

15. Rockport Harbor (Striped Bass, Bluefish, Mackerel, Tautog)

Rockport Harbor, Rockport

Rockport Harbor offers good fishing on Cape Ann.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Saltwater permit required. Buy at mass.gov/masswildlife.
  • Fish the Merrimack River -- The Merrimack offers excellent striped bass fishing in late spring.
  • Try Plum Island -- Plum Island is one of the best surf fishing destinations on the East Coast.
  • Book a charter in Gloucester -- Gloucester has a strong charter fleet for offshore fishing.
  • Spring through fall -- April through November offers the best fishing on the North Shore.
