Fishing in Eastern Illinois

Lake Vermilion, Kickapoo State Park, Wabash River, Embarras River, and the waters of eastern Illinois.

1. Wabash River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass)

Wabash River, eastern Illinois border

The Wabash River forms Illinois' eastern border and offers excellent catfish and bass fishing in a large, free-flowing river.

2. Lake Vermilion (IL) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Walleye)

Lake Vermilion, Vermilion County

Lake Vermilion offers good bass and crappie fishing near Danville.

3. Kickapoo State Park Lakes (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Kickapoo SP, Vermilion County

Kickapoo State Park has numerous strip-mine lakes offering excellent fishing in a unique landscape.

4. Embarras River (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnr.illinois.gov.
  • Fish the Wabash River -- The Wabash River offers excellent channel catfish and flathead catfish fishing.
  • Try Kickapoo State Park -- The strip-mine lakes at Kickapoo offer excellent bass fishing in a unique setting.
  • County lakes -- Eastern Illinois has numerous county lakes offering good family fishing.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-May and September-October offer the best fishing in eastern Illinois.
