Fishing in Northwest Missouri & Kansas City Area

Smithville Lake, Truman Lake, Missouri River, Longview Lake, and the bass, crappie, catfish, and walleye fishing of northwest Missouri.

1. Truman Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Blue Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Walleye)

Truman Lake, Benton/Hickory counties

Truman Lake is a 55,000-acre reservoir on the Osage River offering world-class crappie and catfish fishing, with excellent docks and shore fishing access.

2. Smithville Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Bluegill, Walleye)

Smithville Lake, Clay County

Smithville Lake is a 7,200-acre reservoir near Kansas City offering excellent bass, crappie, and walleye fishing with multiple fishing docks.

3. Missouri River (Blue Catfish, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, White Bass, Sauger, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass)

Missouri River, KS/MO border

The Missouri River along the Kansas City area offers world-class blue catfish and flathead catfish fishing, with channel catfish and sauger also abundant.

4. Longview Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Bluegill, Redear Sunfish)

Longview Lake, Jackson County

Longview Lake is a 980-acre lake near Lee's Summit offering excellent bass and crappie fishing with good shore access.

5. Blue Springs Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Bluegill)

Blue Springs Lake, Jackson County

Blue Springs Lake offers excellent fishing near Blue Springs with multiple fishing docks.

6. Lake Jacomo (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, Bluegill, Redear Sunfish, Crappie)

Lake Jacomo, Jackson County

Lake Jacomo at Fleming Park offers good fishing near Blue Springs.

7. James A. Reed Wildlife Area (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish)

James A. Reed WA, Jackson County

The James A. Reed Wildlife Area offers multiple fishing lakes with excellent shore access.

8. Kansas City Urban Lakes (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Various urban lakes, Kansas City

Kansas City's many urban lakes offer convenient fishing with good populations of bass, crappie, and catfish.

9. Swope Park Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Swope Park, Kansas City

Swope Park Lake offers good urban fishing in one of the largest city parks in America.

10. Loose Park Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Channel Catfish)

Loose Park, Kansas City

Loose Park Lake offers good urban fishing.

11. Wyandotte County Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Channel Catfish, White Bass, Bluegill)

Wyandotte County Lake, Wyandotte County KS

Wyandotte County Lake near Kansas City KS offers good fishing with a fishing dock.

12. Stockton Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Bluegill)

Stockton Lake, Cedar/Dade counties

Stockton Lake is a 25,000-acre reservoir offering excellent walleye and bass fishing with numerous fishing docks.

13. Pomme de Terre Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Walleye, Bluegill)

Pomme de Terre Lake, Hickory/Polk counties

Pomme de Terre Lake offers excellent bass, crappie, and walleye fishing with good shore access.

14. Lake of the Ozarks (West) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, White Bass, Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish)

Western Lake of the Ozarks, Benton County

The western portion of Lake of the Ozarks near Truman Lake offers excellent fishing.

15. Grand River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Blue Catfish)

Grand River, Daviess/Livingston counties

The Grand River offers excellent catfish fishing in northwest Missouri.

16. Platte River (MO) (Channel Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Smallmouth Bass)

Platte River, Platte County

The Platte River offers good fishing near Kansas City.

17. Crooked River (Channel Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Crooked River, Ray County

The Crooked River offers good fishing.

18. Watkins Mill Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Redear Sunfish)

Watkins Mill SP, Clay County

Watkins Mill State Park Lake offers good family fishing.

19. Lake Arrowhead (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish)

Lake Arrowhead, Clay County

Lake Arrowhead offers good fishing.

20. Little Platte River (Channel Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Little Platte River, Clay County

The Little Platte River offers good fishing.

21. Fishing River (Channel Catfish, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Fishing River, Clay County

The Fishing River offers good fishing.

Shoal Creek, Clay County

Shoal Creek offers good fishing.

The Blue River offers urban fishing in Kansas City.

Little Blue River, Jackson County

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at mdc.mo.gov. Trout stamp required for trout fishing.
  • Fish Truman Lake -- Truman Lake offers world-class crappie and catfish fishing.
  • Try Smithville Lake -- Smithville is one of the best walleye fisheries in northwest Missouri.
  • Urban fishing -- Kansas City's urban lakes offer excellent convenient fishing with fishing docks.
  • Spring and fall are best -- March-May and September-November offer the best fishing.
