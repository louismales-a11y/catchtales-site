Fishing in Northeastern Iowa & Mississippi River

Mississippi River pools, Upper Iowa River, Decorah trout streams, and the best fishing in Iowa's Driftless Region.

1. Mississippi River (Pool 9) (Walleye, Sauger, Smallmouth Bass, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Mississippi River, Clayton/Allamakee counties

Pool 9 of the Mississippi River offers world-class walleye and sauger fishing along Iowa's northeastern border.

2. Mississippi River (Pool 10) (Walleye, Sauger, Smallmouth Bass, Catfish, White Bass)

Mississippi River, Clayton County

Pool 10 offers excellent walleye and sauger fishing near Guttenberg and McGregor.

3. Upper Iowa River (Smallmouth Bass, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Walleye)

Upper Iowa River, Winneshiek/Allamakee counties

The Upper Iowa River is a premier smallmouth bass and trout stream in Iowa's Driftless Region.

4. Decorah Trout Streams (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Decorah area, Winneshiek County

The Decorah area has numerous spring-fed trout streams including the Upper Iowa, Siewers Spring, and Twin Springs.

5. Turkey River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Catfish)

Turkey River, Winneshiek/Clayton counties

The Turkey River offers good smallmouth bass and walleye fishing.

6. Yellow River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Yellow River, Allamakee County

The Yellow River offers excellent trout fishing in the Yellow River State Forest.

7. Volga River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Rainbow Trout)

Volga River, Clayton/Fayette counties

The Volga River offers good smallmouth and trout fishing.

8. Little Maquoketa River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Little Maquoketa River, Dubuque County

The Little Maquoketa River offers good smallmouth fishing.

9. Maquoketa River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Catfish)

Maquoketa River, Delaware/Dubuque counties

The Maquoketa River offers good fishing in northeast Iowa.

10. Wapsipinicon River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Catfish)

Wapsipinicon River, Fayette/Delaware counties

The Wapsipinicon River offers good smallmouth and walleye fishing.

11. Lake Delhi (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, Catfish, Bluegill)

Lake Delhi, Delaware County

Lake Delhi is a 350-acre man-made lake on the Maquoketa River offering good fishing.

12. Cascade Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Cascade Lake, Dubuque County

Cascade Lake offers good fishing near Cascade.

13. Pine Lake (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

Pine Lake, Delaware County

Pine Lake offers good fishing near Manchester.

14. Lake Macbride (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Walleye)

Lake Macbride, Johnson County

Lake Macbride is a 612-acre reservoir offering excellent bass and crappie fishing near Iowa City.

15. Coralville Lake (Walleye, Sauger, White Bass, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Coralville Lake, Johnson County

Coralville Lake is a 5,000-acre reservoir offering excellent walleye and sauger fishing near Iowa City.

16. Iowa River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Catfish, Northern Pike)

Iowa River, Johnson/Iowa counties

The Iowa River offers good fishing through eastern Iowa.

17. Cedar River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Channel Catfish, Northern Pike)

Cedar River, Black Hawk/Linn counties

The Cedar River offers excellent smallmouth bass and walleye fishing.

18. Lake George (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Northern Pike)

Lake George, Black Hawk County

Lake George offers good fishing near Waterloo.

19. Brinker Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Brinker Lake, Black Hawk County

Brinker Lake offers good fishing.

20. Hendrickson Marsh Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Hendrickson Marsh, Story County

Hendrickson Marsh offers good fishing near Ames.

21. Hickory Grove Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

Hickory Grove Lake, Story County

Hickory Grove Lake offers good fishing near Colo.

22. Banner Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Banner Lake, Johnson County

Banner Lake offers good fishing near North Liberty.

23. Otter Creek Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Otter Creek Lake, Marshall County

Otter Creek Lake offers good fishing near Marshalltown.

24. Union Grove Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Union Grove Lake, Tama County

Union Grove Lake offers good fishing near Gladbrook.

25. George Wyth Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

George Wyth Lake, Black Hawk County

George Wyth Lake offers good fishing in Waterloo.

26. Prairie Lakes (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Prairie Lakes, Black Hawk County

Prairie Lakes offer good fishing in Cedar Falls.

27. Big Woods Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Big Woods Lake, Black Hawk County

Big Woods Lake offers good fishing in Cedar Falls.

28. Cedar Lake (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

Cedar Lake, Black Hawk County

Cedar Lake offers good fishing.

29. Casey Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Casey Lake, Black Hawk County

Casey Lake offers stocked trout fishing near Waterloo.

30. Hartman Reserve Pond (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Hartman Reserve, Black Hawk County

Hartman Reserve offers good urban fishing in Cedar Falls.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at iowadnr.gov/fishing. Trout stamp required for trout.
  • Fish the Mississippi River -- Pools 9-10 offer excellent walleye and sauger fishing.
  • Try Decorah trout streams -- Northeast Iowa's spring-fed streams offer excellent trout fishing in the Driftless Region.
  • Upper Iowa River -- The Upper Iowa is one of Iowa's best smallmouth bass rivers.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing.
