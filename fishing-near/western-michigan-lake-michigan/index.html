Fishing in Western Michigan & Lake Michigan Coast

Lake Michigan, Grand River, Pere Marquette River, Muskegon River, Kalamazoo River, and the salmon, steelhead, and bass fishing of western Michigan.

1. Lake Michigan (Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Whitefish)

Lake Michigan, western MI coast

Lake Michigan offers world-class salmon and trout fishing along the entire western coast of Michigan, with major ports from Ludington to St. Joseph.

2. Grand River (Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon)

Grand River, Kent/Ottawa counties

The Grand River is Michigan's longest river at 252 miles, offering excellent smallmouth bass, walleye, and steelhead fishing from Grand Rapids to Lake Michigan.

3. Pere Marquette River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Smallmouth Bass)

Pere Marquette River, Lake/Mason counties

The Pere Marquette River is one of Michigan's premier trout and salmon streams, famous for its steelhead and salmon runs and designated a National Wild and Scenic River.

4. Muskegon River (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Brown Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye)

Muskegon River, Newaygo/Muskegon counties

The Muskegon River offers exceptional steelhead, salmon, and smallmouth bass fishing from inland reaches through Hardy Dam to Muskegon Lake.

5. Kalamazoo River (Smallmouth Bass, Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Walleye, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish)

Kalamazoo River, Allegan/Kalamazoo counties

The Kalamazoo River offers excellent smallmouth bass and steelhead fishing flowing into Lake Michigan at Saugatuck.

6. St. Joseph River (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Channel Catfish)

St. Joseph River, Berrien County

The St. Joseph River offers excellent steelhead, salmon, and smallmouth bass fishing at the Michigan-Indiana border.

7. Muskegon Lake (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass)

Muskegon Lake, Muskegon County

Muskegon Lake is a 4,100-acre lake connected to Lake Michigan, offering excellent walleye, bass, and perch fishing.

8. White Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Largemouth Bass)

White Lake, Muskegon County

White Lake offers excellent fishing near Whitehall and Montague.

9. Lake Macatawa (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish)

Lake Macatawa, Ottawa County

Lake Macatawa offers good fishing at Holland Harbor, connected to Lake Michigan.

10. Pentwater Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Pentwater Lake, Oceana County

Pentwater Lake offers good fishing near the Lake Michigan port of Pentwater.

11. Hamlin Lake (Northern Pike, Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Hamlin Lake, Mason County

Hamlin Lake is a 4,900-acre man-made lake connected to Lake Michigan, offering excellent pike and bass fishing at Ludington State Park.

12. Pere Marquette Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon)

Pere Marquette Lake, Mason County

Pere Marquette Lake at Ludington offers excellent salmon, walleye, and bass fishing at the mouth of the Pere Marquette River.

13. Manistee Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Manistee Lake, Manistee County

Manistee Lake offers excellent fishing at the Lake Michigan port of Manistee.

14. Portage Lake (Manistee) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Portage Lake, Manistee County

Portage Lake offers good fishing near Manistee.

15. Tippy Dam Pond (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Tippy Dam Pond, Manistee County

Tippy Dam Pond on the Manistee River offers excellent walleye and bass fishing.

16. Hodenpyl Dam Pond (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie)

Hodenpyl Dam Pond, Manistee County

Hodenpyl Dam Pond offers good fishing on the Manistee River.

17. Big Manistique Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Big Manistique Lake, Mackinac County

Big Manistique Lake offers excellent walleye fishing.

18. Croton Pond (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Croton Pond, Newaygo County

Croton Pond on the Muskegon River offers good walleye and bass fishing.

19. Hardy Dam Pond (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Hardy Dam Pond, Newaygo County

Hardy Dam Pond on the Muskegon River offers excellent walleye and bass fishing.

20. Kimball Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Kimball Lake, Newaygo County

Kimball Lake offers good fishing.

21. Fremont Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Fremont Lake, Newaygo County

Fremont Lake offers good warm-water fishing.

22. Hess Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Yellow Perch)

Hess Lake, Newaygo County

Hess Lake offers good panfish and bass fishing.

23. Gun Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Walleye)

Gun Lake, Allegan County

Gun Lake offers excellent bass and panfish fishing in southwestern Michigan.

24. Gull Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Gull Lake, Kalamazoo County

Gull Lake offers excellent bass and walleye fishing near Kalamazoo.

25. Crooked Lake (Kalamazoo) (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie)

Crooked Lake, Kalamazoo County

Crooked Lake offers good fishing near Kalamazoo.

26. Eagle Lake (Kalamazoo) (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Eagle Lake, Kalamazoo County

Eagle Lake offers good fishing near Kalamazoo.

27. Sugarloaf Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch)

Sugarloaf Lake, Kalamazoo County

Sugarloaf Lake offers good fishing.

28. Wintergreen Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Northern Pike)

Wintergreen Lake, Kalamazoo County

Wintergreen Lake offers good panfish fishing.

29. Paw Paw River (Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Channel Catfish, Northern Pike)

Paw Paw River, Berrien/Van Buren counties

The Paw Paw River offers good smallmouth bass and walleye fishing flowing into Lake Michigan.

30. Galien River (Steelhead, Chinook Salmon, Smallmouth Bass, Brown Trout)

Galien River, Berrien County

The Galien River offers good steelhead and salmon fishing at the Indiana border.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at Michigan.gov/DNR.
  • Fish the Grand River -- The Grand River offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing through Grand Rapids.
  • Try the Pere Marquette -- The PM is one of Michigan's best steelhead and salmon rivers.
  • Spring salmon charters -- Book a Lake Michigan charter from Ludington, Manistee, or Muskegon for spring Chinook and coho.
  • Fall steelhead -- October through November offers peak steelhead runs on all major rivers.
