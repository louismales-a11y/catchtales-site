Fishing on Oahu

Honolulu, Waikiki, North Shore, Kaneohe Bay, and the fishing capital of Hawaii.

1. Waikiki (Yellowfin Tuna, Mahi-Mahi, Skipjack Tuna, Blue Marlin)

Waikiki, Honolulu

Waikiki offers excellent offshore fishing just beyond the famous beach. Charter boats target ahi (yellowfin), mahi-mahi, and blue marlin in the waters off Waikiki.

2. Kaneohe Bay (Bonefish, Papio, Trevally, Goatfish)

Kaneohe Bay, windward Oahu

Kaneohe Bay offers excellent inshore fishing for bonefish, papio (juvenile trevally), and goatfish in a protected bay with a barrier reef.

3. North Shore (Ulua, Papio, Bonefish, Sharks)

North Shore, Oahu

The North Shore of Oahu offers excellent shore fishing from its famous beaches. Ulua (giant trevally) and papio are the primary targets from the rocky shores.

4. Ala Wai Harbor (Yellowfin Tuna, Mahi-Mahi, Ono, Marlin)

Ala Wai Harbor, Waikiki

Ala Wai Harbor is the main charter fishing departure point in Honolulu, offering access to world-class offshore fishing.

5. Pearl Harbor Area (Bonefish, Papio, Mullet, Milkfish)

Pearl Harbor, Honolulu

The waters around Pearl Harbor offer good inshore fishing for bonefish and papio in a historic setting.

6. Haleiwa (Mahi-Mahi, Ahi, Ono, Marlin)

Haleiwa, North Shore

Haleiwa Harbor on the North Shore offers charter fishing for offshore species, particularly productive in summer.

7. Makapu'u Beach (Papio, Ulua, Bonefish, Surf Perch)

Makapu'u, windward Oahu

Makapu'u Beach offers good shore fishing near the Makapu'u Lighthouse trail.

8. Sand Island (Bonefish, Papio, Mullet, Goatfish)

Sand Island, Honolulu Harbor

Sand Island State Recreation Area offers good shore fishing in Honolulu Harbor.

9. Waianae Coast (Ulua, Papio, Bonefish, Trevally)

Waianae, west Oahu

The Waianae Coast offers excellent shore fishing from the rocky coastline.

10. Kailua Beach (Bonefish, Papio, Mullet)

Kailua, windward Oahu

Kailua Beach offers good bonefish and papio fishing in the calm windward waters.

11. Lanikai Beach (Bonefish, Papio, Goatfish)

Lanikai, windward Oahu

Lanikai Beach offers good inshore fishing with views of the Mokulua Islands.

12. Hanauma Bay (Tropical Fish, Reef Fish)

Hanauma Bay, east Oahu

Hanauma Bay is a protected marine sanctuary — fishing is not permitted, but it's an excellent spot for snorkeling and viewing tropical fish.

13. Barber's Point (Ulua, Papio, Bonefish)

Barber's Point, west Oahu

Barber's Point offers good shore fishing on the leeward coast.

14. Diamond Head Area (Offshore) (Yellowfin Tuna, Mahi-Mahi, Skipjack)

Diamond Head, Waikiki

The waters off Diamond Head offer excellent offshore fishing close to Waikiki.

15. Ko Olina Lagoons (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Ko Olina, west Oahu

The Ko Olina lagoons offer good protected inshore fishing in a resort area.

16. Makapuu Tidepools (Papio, Goatfish, Reef Fish)

Makapuu, east Oahu

The tidepools at Makapuu offer good shore fishing.

17. Spitting Cave (Ulua, Papio, Trevally)

Spitting Cave, south Oahu

Spitting Cave offers good shore fishing.

18. China Walls (Ulua, Papio, Trevally)

China Walls, east Oahu

China Walls offers good shore fishing.

19. Pokai Bay (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Pokai Bay, west Oahu

Pokai Bay offers good protected inshore fishing.

20. Maili Point (Ulua, Papio, Trevally)

Maili Point, west Oahu

Maili Point offers good shore fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- No licence required for recreational saltwater fishing in Hawaii.
  • Book a Waikiki charter -- Waikiki has numerous charter boats targeting marlin, ahi, and mahi-mahi.
  • Try shore casting -- Oahu's rocky shores offer excellent ulua and papio fishing for shore-based anglers.
  • Respect the ocean -- Hawaii's surf can be dangerous. Know your limits and watch for high surf warnings.
  • Best months -- Summer (May-September) offers the best offshore fishing for marlin and tuna.
