Fishing in the Upper Peninsula

Lake Superior, Isle Royale, Tahquamenon River, and the trout, salmon, walleye, and pike waters of the UP.

1. Lake Superior (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Whitefish)

Lake Superior, UP coast

Lake Superior is the largest Great Lake, offering world-class lake trout, salmon, and whitefish fishing along Michigan's Upper Peninsula coast.

2. Tahquamenon River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Northern Pike, Walleye)

Tahquamenon River, Chippewa/Luce counties

The Tahquamenon River is a famous blackwater river in the eastern UP, offering excellent trout and pike fishing around the Tahquamenon Falls.

3. Manistique River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Rainbow Trout, Steelhead)

Manistique River, Schoolcraft County

The Manistique River offers excellent walleye, steelhead, and smallmouth bass fishing in the central UP.

4. Ontonagon River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon)

Ontonagon River, Ontonagon County

The Ontonagon River offers excellent trout and salmon fishing in the western UP.

5. Isle Royale (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Northern Pike)

Isle Royale National Park, Lake Superior

Isle Royale National Park offers pristine wilderness fishing for lake trout and brook trout in a remote island setting.

6. Portage Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Portage Lake, Houghton County

Portage Lake is a large inland lake near Houghton offering excellent walleye and bass fishing.

7. Torch Lake (UP) (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Northern Pike)

Torch Lake, Houghton County

Torch Lake in the Keweenaw Peninsula offers excellent lake trout and smallmouth bass fishing.

8. Lac Vieux Desert (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Bluegill)

Lac Vieux Desert, Gogebic County

Lac Vieux Desert is a large lake on the Michigan-Wisconsin border offering excellent walleye and bass fishing.

9. Gogebic Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Lake Gogebic, Gogebic County

Lake Gogebic is the largest natural inland lake in the UP, offering excellent walleye and pike fishing.

10. Indian Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Indian Lake, Schoolcraft County

Indian Lake offers excellent warm-water fishing in the central UP near Manistique.

11. Brevoort Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Brevoort Lake, Mackinac County

Brevoort Lake offers good fishing near the Straits of Mackinac.

12. Les Cheneaux Islands (Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Walleye, Lake Trout)

Les Cheneaux Islands, Mackinac County

The Les Cheneaux Islands area offers excellent fishing in the northern Lake Huron waters.

13. Munising Bay (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Whitefish)

Munising Bay, Alger County

Munising Bay offers excellent salmon and trout fishing near Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore.

14. Grand Island (Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Whitefish)

Grand Island, Alger County

Grand Island offers excellent fishing in Lake Superior near Munising.

15. Little Bay de Noc (Walleye, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Little Bay de Noc, Delta County

Little Bay de Noc offers excellent walleye and perch fishing on Lake Michigan in the central UP.

16. Big Bay de Noc (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Lake Trout)

Big Bay de Noc, Delta County

Big Bay de Noc offers excellent fishing on the Lake Michigan side of the UP.

17. Menominee River (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Menominee River, Menominee County

The Menominee River forms the border with Wisconsin and offers excellent walleye and smallmouth bass fishing.

18. Sturgeon River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Sturgeon River, Houghton/Baraga counties

The Sturgeon River offers excellent trout fishing in the central UP.

19. Paint River (Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye, Rainbow Trout)

Paint River, Iron County

The Paint River offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing in the western UP.

20. Brule River (MI) (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Smallmouth Bass)

Brule River, Iron County

The Brule River offers excellent trout fishing on the Wisconsin border.

21. Presque Isle River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Coho Salmon)

Presque Isle River, Gogebic County

The Presque Isle River offers excellent trout and salmon fishing in the western UP.

22. Black River (Gogebic) (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Chinook Salmon)

Black River, Gogebic County

The Black River offers excellent trout and salmon fishing near Bessemer.

23. Carp River (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Coho Salmon, Chinook Salmon)

Carp River, Mackinac County

The Carp River offers good trout and salmon fishing near St. Ignace.

24. St. Mary's River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Lake Trout)

St. Mary's River, Chippewa County

The St. Mary's River connects Lake Superior to Lake Huron and offers excellent fishing at the Soo Locks.

25. Sugar Island (Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Yellow Perch)

Sugar Island, Chippewa County

Sugar Island in the St. Mary's River offers excellent fishing near Sault Ste. Marie.

26. Drummond Island (Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Yellow Perch, Lake Trout)

Drummond Island, Chippewa County

Drummond Island offers excellent fishing in Lake Huron at the eastern tip of the UP.

27. Round Island (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Round Island, Chippewa County

Round Island offers good fishing in the St. Mary's River system.

28. Cedarville Bay (Northern Pike, Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Cedarville Bay, Mackinac County

Cedarville Bay offers good fishing in the Les Cheneaux Islands area.

29. Hessel Bay (Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Yellow Perch)

Hessel Bay, Mackinac County

Hessel Bay offers good fishing in the Les Cheneaux Islands area.

30. Whitefish Point (Lake Trout, Whitefish, Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Rainbow Trout)

Whitefish Point, Chippewa County

Whitefish Point is a famous fishing location at the eastern end of Lake Superior, offering excellent whitefish and lake trout fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at Michigan.gov/DNR. Non-resident licences available.
  • Fish Lake Superior -- The big lake offers world-class lake trout and salmon fishing.
  • Try the Tahquamenon River -- The Tahquamenon offers excellent trout fishing in a beautiful blackwater setting.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in the UP.
  • Remote wilderness -- Many UP waters require a hike or boat access. Plan ahead.
