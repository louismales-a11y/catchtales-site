Fishing in Western Iowa & Missouri River

Missouri River, Black Hawk Lake, Lake Manawa, Lewis and Clark Lake, and the rivers and lakes of western Iowa.

1. Missouri River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Walleye, Sauger, Smallmouth Bass, White Bass)

Missouri River, western Iowa border

The Missouri River forms Iowa's western border and offers world-class channel catfish and flathead catfish fishing.

2. Black Hawk Lake (Walleye, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill, Yellow Perch)

Black Hawk Lake, Sac County

Black Hawk Lake is a 968-acre natural lake offering excellent walleye and bass fishing at Lake View.

3. Lake Manawa (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Channel Catfish, Walleye)

Lake Manawa, Pottawattamie County

Lake Manawa is a 750-acre lake offering excellent fishing near Council Bluffs.

4. Lewis and Clark Lake (Walleye, Sauger, Smallmouth Bass, Catfish, Crappie)

Lewis and Clark Lake, Monona County

Lewis and Clark Lake offers good fishing on the Missouri River.

5. Blue Lake (IA West) (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Blue Lake, Monona County

Blue Lake is a Missouri River oxbow lake offering good fishing near Onawa.

6. Snyder Bend Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Snyder Bend Lake, Harrison County

Snyder Bend Lake offers good fishing near Logan.

7. Little Sioux River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike)

Little Sioux River, Woodbury/Monona counties

The Little Sioux River offers excellent catfish fishing flowing into the Missouri.

8. Soldier River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish)

Soldier River, Monona County

The Soldier River offers good fishing in western Iowa.

9. Boyer River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Boyer River, Crawford/Harrison counties

The Boyer River offers good smallmouth fishing.

10. Nishnabotna River (Channel Catfish, Flathead Catfish, Smallmouth Bass)

Nishnabotna River, Pottawattamie/Fremont counties

The Nishnabotna River offers good catfish fishing.

11. Silver Creek Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Silver Creek Lake, Mills County

Silver Creek Lake offers good fishing near Malvern.

12. Willow Lake (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Willow Lake, Fremont County

Willow Lake offers good fishing near Sidney.

13. Lake Icaria (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Walleye)

Lake Icaria, Adams County

Lake Icaria offers good fishing near Corning.

14. Viking Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

Viking Lake, Montgomery County

Viking Lake offers good fishing at Viking Lake State Park.

15. Cold Springs Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Cold Springs Lake, Cass County

Cold Springs Lake offers good fishing near Lewis.

16. Prairie Rose Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Prairie Rose Lake, Shelby County

Prairie Rose Lake offers good fishing at Prairie Rose State Park.

17. Lake Anita (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie, Catfish)

Lake Anita, Cass County

Lake Anita offers good fishing at Lake Anita State Park.

18. Arrowhead Lake (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Arrowhead Lake, Pottawattamie County

Arrowhead Lake offers good fishing near Council Bluffs.

19. Hitchcock Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Hitchcock Lake, Pottawattamie County

Hitchcock Lake offers good fishing near Council Bluffs.

20. West Okoboji (Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Yellow Perch, Northern Pike)

West Okoboji, Dickinson County

West Okoboji is part of the Iowa Great Lakes and offers excellent walleye fishing.

21. Cottonwood Lake (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Cottonwood Lake, Monona County

Cottonwood Lake offers good fishing.

22. Heisler Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Heisler Lake, Monona County

Heisler Lake offers good fishing.

23. Decatur Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Decatur Lake, Decatur County

Decatur Lake offers good fishing.

24. Lake Binder (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Lake Binder, Pottawattamie County

Lake Binder offers good fishing.

25. Middle Lake (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Middle Lake, Pottawattamie County

Middle Lake offers good fishing.

26. Deer Creek Lake (IA) (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Deer Creek Lake, Fremont County

Deer Creek Lake offers good fishing.

27. Riverton Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Riverton Lake, Fremont County

Riverton Lake offers good fishing.

28. Waubonsie Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Waubonsie Lake, Fremont County

Waubonsie Lake offers good fishing at Waubonsie State Park.

29. Wilson Park Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Wilson Park, Mills County

Wilson Park offers stocked trout fishing in Glenwood.

30. Botna Bend Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Botna Bend Lake, Pottawattamie County

Botna Bend Lake offers good fishing near Council Bluffs.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at iowadnr.gov/fishing.
  • Fish the Missouri River -- The Missouri offers world-class channel catfish and flathead catfish fishing.
  • Try Black Hawk Lake -- Black Hawk Lake offers excellent walleye and perch fishing.
  • Lake Manawa -- Lake Manawa near Council Bluffs offers good bass and crappie fishing.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in western Iowa.
