Fishing in Central Indiana & Indianapolis

Eagle Creek, Geist Reservoir, Morse Reservoir, White River, and the best fishing near Indy.

1. Eagle Creek Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Saugeye, Bluegill, Catfish)

Eagle Creek, Indianapolis

Eagle Creek Reservoir is a 1,400-acre lake in Indianapolis offering excellent bass, saugeye, and crappie fishing in an urban park setting.

2. Geist Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Saugeye)

Geist Reservoir, Hamilton/Marion counties

Geist Reservoir is a 1,900-acre reservoir offering excellent bass and crappie fishing northeast of Indianapolis.

3. Morse Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Saugeye, Bluegill, Catfish)

Morse Reservoir, Hamilton County

Morse Reservoir is a 1,500-acre reservoir offering good bass and saugeye fishing near Noblesville.

4. White River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish, Saugeye, Crappie)

White River, Marion/Hamilton counties

The White River through Indianapolis offers excellent urban fishing for smallmouth bass and catfish.

5. White River (Upper) (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Catfish)

Upper White River, Anderson/Muncie area

The upper White River offers good smallmouth bass and walleye fishing.

6. Prairie Creek Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Prairie Creek Reservoir, Delaware County

Prairie Creek Reservoir offers good fishing near Muncie.

7. Summit Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Summit Lake, Henry County

Summit Lake offers good fishing near New Castle.

8. West Fork White River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

West Fork White River, Randolph/Wayne counties

The West Fork of the White River offers good smallmouth fishing.

9. Mississinewa River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Mississinewa River, Grant/Wabash counties

The Mississinewa River offers good smallmouth bass fishing.

10. Salamonie Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Walleye, Catfish, Bluegill)

Salamonie Reservoir, Wabash County

Salamonie Reservoir is a 3,000-acre reservoir offering excellent bass and walleye fishing.

11. Mississinewa Lake (Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Mississinewa Lake, Miami/Wabash counties

Mississinewa Lake is a 3,200-acre reservoir offering excellent fishing.

12. J. Edward Roush Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish, Saugeye)

J. Edward Roush Lake, Huntington County

Roush Lake offers good fishing near Huntington.

13. Huntington Lake (Saugeye, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish)

Huntington Lake, Huntington County

Huntington Lake offers good saugeye and bass fishing.

14. Kokomo Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Catfish)

Kokomo Reservoir, Howard County

Kokomo Reservoir offers good fishing near Kokomo.

15. Wildcat Creek (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Wildcat Creek, Howard/Tippecanoe counties

Wildcat Creek offers good smallmouth bass fishing.

16. Sugar Creek (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Sugar Creek, Montgomery/Parke counties

Sugar Creek offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing in a scenic stream.

17. Big Walnut Creek (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Big Walnut Creek, Putnam County

Big Walnut Creek offers good smallmouth fishing.

18. Mill Creek (IN) (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Mill Creek, Montgomery County

Mill Creek offers good fishing.

19. Deer Creek (IN) (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass)

Deer Creek, Carroll County

Deer Creek offers good smallmouth fishing.

20. Eel River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Eel River, Cass/Miami counties

The Eel River offers good smallmouth bass fishing.

21. Wabash River (Central) (Catfish, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Saugeye)

Wabash River, Tippecanoe/Carroll counties

The Wabash River through central Indiana offers excellent catfish and smallmouth fishing.

22. Tippecanoe Lake (Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Bluegill)

Tippecanoe Lake, Marshall County

Tippecanoe Lake offers good bass and pike fishing.

23. Lake Cicott (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Cicott, Cass County

Lake Cicott offers good fishing near Logansport.

24. Lake Freeman (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Catfish, Bluegill)

Lake Freeman, Carroll/White counties

Lake Freeman offers good fishing on the Tippecanoe River.

25. Adams Mill Pond (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Adams Mill Pond, Carroll County

Adams Mill Pond offers good fishing.

26. Burlington Lake (Largemouth Bass, Bluegill, Crappie)

Burlington Lake, Carroll County

Burlington Lake offers good fishing.

27. Indy Parks Ponds (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Bluegill)

Indianapolis parks, Marion County

Indianapolis park ponds are stocked with rainbow trout in winter and offer good warm-water fishing in summer.

28. Eagle Creek Park (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Eagle Creek Park, Indianapolis

Eagle Creek Park's smaller ponds offer stocked trout fishing in spring.

29. Fort Harrison SP Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Bluegill)

Fort Harrison SP, Indianapolis

Fort Harrison State Park offers good family fishing near Indianapolis.

30. White River Canal (Catfish, Carp, Largemouth Bass)

White River Canal, Indianapolis

The Indianapolis canal offers urban fishing in the heart of downtown Indy.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at in.gov/dnr/fish.
  • Fish Eagle Creek -- Eagle Creek Reservoir offers excellent fishing within Indianapolis city limits.
  • Try the White River -- The White River through Indy offers excellent urban smallmouth fishing.
  • Saugeye fishing -- Several central Indiana reservoirs have excellent saugeye (walleye-sauger hybrid) populations.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best central Indiana fishing.
