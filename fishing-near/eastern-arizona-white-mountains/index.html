Fishing in Eastern Arizona & White Mountains

Big Lake, Sunrise Lake, Crescent Lake, Luna Lake, Becker Lake, and the premier trout fishing of Arizona's White Mountains.

1. Big Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout, Apache Trout)

Big Lake, White Mountains, Apache-Sitgreaves NF

Big Lake is the premier fishing lake in the White Mountains, offering excellent rainbow, brook, and brown trout fishing in a large mountain lake at 9,000 feet elevation.

2. Sunrise Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout, Arctic Char)

Sunrise Lake, White Mountains

Sunrise Lake offers excellent rainbow trout and the only Arctic char fishery in Arizona. The lake is part of the Sunrise Park Resort area.

3. Crescent Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Apache Trout)

Crescent Lake, White Mountains

Crescent Lake offers excellent rainbow, brook, and Apache trout fishing in a scenic White Mountains lake.

4. Luna Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout)

Luna Lake, Alpine area

Luna Lake offers excellent rainbow, brook, and brown trout fishing in a scenic lake near the New Mexico border.

5. Becker Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout, Brown Trout)

Becker Lake, Springerville area

Becker Lake is a catch-and-release, artificial-fly-only lake known for producing trophy rainbow trout and Apache trout exceeding 20 inches.

6. Rainbow Lake (Greer) (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout, Brown Trout)

Rainbow Lake, Greer area

Rainbow Lake offers rainbow, brook, and brown trout fishing in the Greer area of the White Mountains.

7. River Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

River Reservoir, Alpine area

River Reservoir offers rainbow and brown trout fishing near Alpine.

8. Lee Valley Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Lee Valley Lake, White Mountains

Lee Valley Lake offers rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing in the White Mountains accessible by high-clearance vehicle.

9. Reservation Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout, Brook Trout)

Reservation Lake, Fort Apache Reservation

Reservation Lake offers rainbow, Apache, and brook trout fishing on the Fort Apache Reservation. A tribal permit is required.

10. Hawley Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout, Brook Trout)

Hawley Lake, Fort Apache Reservation

Hawley Lake offers excellent rainbow and Apache trout fishing on the Fort Apache Reservation at 8,500 feet elevation.

11. Christmas Tree Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Christmas Tree Lake, White Mountains

Christmas Tree Lake offers rainbow and Apache trout fishing in the White Mountains.

12. Horseshoe Cienega Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Horseshoe Cienega Lake, Fort Apache Reservation

Horseshoe Cienega Lake offers rainbow and Apache trout fishing on the Fort Apache Reservation.

13. Squaw Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Squaw Lake, White Mountains

Squaw Lake offers rainbow and brook trout fishing near Big Lake in the White Mountains.

14. Bunch Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Bunch Reservoir, Alpine area

Bunch Reservoir offers rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing near Alpine.

15. Carnero Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Carnero Lake, Alpine area

Carnero Lake offers rainbow trout and Apache trout fishing in the Alpine area.

16. Nelson Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Walleye, Catfish)

Nelson Reservoir, Springerville area

Nelson Reservoir offers rainbow trout, walleye, and catfish fishing near Springerville.

17. Lyman Lake (Largemouth Bass, Walleye, Crappie, Catfish)

Lyman Lake, St. Johns area

Lyman Lake offers largemouth bass, walleye, and crappie fishing on the Little Colorado River.

18. Fool Hollow Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Fool Hollow Lake, Show Low

Fool Hollow Lake offers rainbow trout, largemouth bass, and catfish fishing in a state park near Show Low.

19. Show Low Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Catfish)

Show Low Lake, Show Low

Show Low Lake offers rainbow trout and largemouth bass fishing in the Show Low area of the White Mountains.

20. Woodland Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Woodland Lake, Pinetop-Lakeside

Woodland Lake offers rainbow trout and bass fishing in the Pinetop-Lakeside area.

21. Scott Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Scott Reservoir, Lakeside area

Scott Reservoir offers rainbow trout and bass fishing near Pinetop-Lakeside.

22. White Mountain Lake (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

White Mountain Lake, Show Low area

White Mountain Lake offers rainbow trout, largemouth bass, and crappie fishing near Show Low.

23. Little Bear Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brook Trout)

Little Bear Lake, White Mountains

Little Bear Lake offers rainbow and brook trout fishing in the White Mountains near Sunrise Lake.

24. Mexican Hay Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Mexican Hay Lake, Alpine area

Mexican Hay Lake offers rainbow and Apache trout fishing in a remote White Mountains lake.

25. Gobbler Lake (Rainbow Trout, Apache Trout)

Gobbler Lake, Alpine area

Gobbler Lake offers rainbow and Apache trout fishing in the Alpine area.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at azgfd.gov. Fort Apache Reservation lakes require a separate tribal permit.
  • Fish Big Lake in June -- June offers the best trout fishing on Big Lake after ice-out and before summer crowds.
  • Try Becker Lake for trophies -- Becker Lake is strictly catch-and-release, artificial flies only, and produces trophy trout.
  • Summer escape -- The White Mountains offer excellent trout fishing and cool temperatures (70s) when Phoenix is over 110F.
  • Bring warm clothes -- Even in July, White Mountain temperatures can drop into the 40s at night. Pack layers.
