Fishing in North Central Minnesota

Leech Lake, Lake Winnibigoshish, Brainerd lakes area, Bemidji, and the walleye, muskie, pike, and bass fishing of north central Minnesota.

1. Leech Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Muskellunge, Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Leech Lake, Cass County

Leech Lake is Minnesota's third-largest lake at 112,000 acres, offering world-class walleye and muskellunge fishing.

2. Lake Winnibigoshish (Walleye, Northern Pike, Yellow Perch, Crappie, Muskellunge)

Lake Winnibigoshish, Itasca/Cass counties

Lake Winnibigoshish is a 58,000-acre lake offering excellent walleye and perch fishing with some of the best walleye habitat in Minnesota.

3. Cass Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Muskellunge, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Cass Lake, Cass County

Cass Lake is a 15,000-acre lake offering excellent walleye and muskie fishing in the Chippewa National Forest.

4. Upper Red Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Upper Red Lake, Beltrami County

Upper Red Lake is famous for its revived walleye fishery, one of the best in Minnesota with excellent year-round fishing.

5. Lower Red Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Crappie, Yellow Perch, Walleye)

Lower Red Lake, Beltrami County

Lower Red Lake is the largest lake entirely within Minnesota at 150,000 acres, offering excellent walleye and perch fishing.

6. Lake Bemidji (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Bemidji, Beltrami County

Lake Bemidji offers excellent walleye and bass fishing in the heart of Bemidji.

7. Big Lake (Beltrami) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Big Lake, Beltrami County

Big Lake offers good fishing near Bemidji.

8. Gull Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Gull Lake, Cass County

Gull Lake is a 9,400-acre lake in the Brainerd lakes area offering excellent walleye and bass fishing.

9. Pelican Lake (Crow Wing) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Pelican Lake, Crow Wing County

Pelican Lake offers excellent fishing in the Brainerd area.

10. North Long Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

North Long Lake, Crow Wing County

North Long Lake offers good fishing near Brainerd.

11. Cross Lake (Crow Wing) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Cross Lake, Crow Wing County

Cross Lake offers good fishing in the Brainerd area.

12. Round Lake (Crow Wing) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Bluegill)

Round Lake, Crow Wing County

Round Lake offers good fishing.

13. Lower Hay Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lower Hay Lake, Crow Wing County

Lower Hay Lake offers good fishing.

14. Upper Hay Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie)

Upper Hay Lake, Crow Wing County

Upper Hay Lake offers good fishing.

15. Whitefish Lake (Crow Wing) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Whitefish Lake, Crow Wing County

Whitefish Lake offers good fishing in the Brainerd area.

16. Bay Lake (Crow Wing) (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Bay Lake, Crow Wing County

Bay Lake offers good fishing near Deerwood.

17. Big Trout Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Big Trout Lake, Crow Wing County

Big Trout Lake offers good fishing.

18. Mille Lacs Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Muskellunge, Crappie, Yellow Perch)

Mille Lacs Lake, Aitkin/Mille Lacs counties

Mille Lacs Lake is a 132,000-acre lake famous for its world-class walleye fishery and excellent smallmouth bass fishing.

19. Big Sandy Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Big Sandy Lake, Aitkin County

Big Sandy Lake offers excellent walleye and bass fishing in east-central Minnesota.

20. Lake Minnewawa (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Lake Minnewawa, Aitkin County

Lake Minnewawa offers good fishing near McGregor.

21. Farm Island Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Farm Island Lake, Aitkin County

Farm Island Lake offers good fishing.

22. Lake Mille Lacs (Northern) (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Muskellunge)

Northern Mille Lacs, Aitkin County

The northern shore of Mille Lacs offers excellent shore and boat fishing access.

23. Bowstring Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Bowstring Lake, Itasca County

Bowstring Lake offers good fishing near Grand Rapids.

24. Big Fork River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie)

Big Fork River, Itasca County

The Big Fork River offers good fishing in north central Minnesota.

25. Little Fork River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass)

Little Fork River, Itasca County

The Little Fork River offers good fishing.

26. Rainy River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Sturgeon)

Rainy River, Koochiching County

The Rainy River forms the Canadian border and offers excellent walleye and sturgeon fishing, especially during spring runs.

27. Lake of the Woods (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Yellow Perch, Lake Trout)

Lake of the Woods, Lake of the Woods County

Lake of the Woods is a massive 950,000-acre lake on the Canadian border, famous as the Walleye Capital of the World.

28. Zipple Bay (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Zipple Bay, Lake of the Woods County

Zipple Bay on Lake of the Woods offers excellent walleye fishing at Zipple Bay State Park.

29. Baudette River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Sturgeon)

Baudette River, Lake of the Woods County

The Baudette River offers good fishing near Lake of the Woods.

30. Winter Road River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Crappie)

Winter Road River, Lake of the Woods County

The Winter Road River offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at dnr.state.mn.us. Non-resident licences available.
  • Fish Leech Lake -- Leech Lake is one of the best walleye and muskie lakes in Minnesota.
  • Try the Brainerd area -- The Brainerd lakes area has hundreds of lakes with excellent multi-species fishing.
  • Mille Lacs walleye -- Mille Lacs Lake is famous for its walleye fishing, though regulations vary.
  • Summer is peak -- June through September offers the best fishing in north central Minnesota.
