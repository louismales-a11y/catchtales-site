Fishing in Gulf Coast & Copper River

Copper River, Cordova, Valdez, Prince William Sound, and the Gulf of Alaska coast.

1. Copper River (Chinook Salmon, Sockeye Salmon, Coho Salmon, Steelhead)

Copper River, Cordova area

The Copper River is famous for its prized Copper River salmon — king, sockeye, and coho that are among the most sought-after salmon in the world. The river also offers excellent steelhead fishing in spring and fall.

2. Cordova Area (Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Halibut, Steelhead)

Cordova, Prince William Sound

Cordova offers excellent fishing in the Copper River delta and Prince William Sound. The area is famous for its Copper River salmon and excellent halibut fishing.

3. Valdez / Prince William Sound (Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Halibut, Rockfish)

Valdez, Prince William Sound

Valdez offers exceptional saltwater fishing in Prince William Sound. Charter boats target halibut, king salmon, silver salmon, and rockfish in the shadow of towering mountains and glaciers.

4. Whittier (Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Halibut, Rockfish)

Whittier, Prince William Sound

Whittier offers access to Prince William Sound's excellent saltwater fishing. The community is the gateway to the sound's numerous productive fishing grounds.

5. Kenai Fjords (Halibut, Chinook Salmon, Rockfish, Lingcod)

Kenai Fjords National Park

The Kenai Fjords offer spectacular saltwater fishing in a dramatic fjord setting with tidewater glaciers. Halibut and salmon fishing is excellent.

6. Prince William Sound (Multiple Species)

Prince William Sound, entire region

Prince William Sound offers world-class saltwater fishing for halibut, salmon, rockfish, and lingcod. The sound's protected waters and stunning scenery make it a premier destination.

7. Bering River (Sockeye Salmon, Coho Salmon, Steelhead)

Bering River, Copper River delta

The Bering River offers good salmon and steelhead fishing in the Copper River delta region near Cordova.

8. Martin River (Sockeye Salmon, Coho Salmon)

Martin River, Copper River delta

The Martin River offers good sockeye and silver salmon fishing in the extensive Copper River delta wetland system.

9. Eyak River (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Eyak River, Cordova

The Eyak River flows through Cordova and offers excellent silver salmon and Dolly Varden fishing within walking distance of town.

10. Katalmute River (Sockeye Salmon, Coho Salmon)

Katalmute River, Copper River delta

The Katalmute River offers good salmon fishing in the Copper River delta region.

11. Port of Valdez (Halibut, Chinook Salmon, Rockfish)

Port of Valdez, Valdez

The Port of Valdez offers excellent saltwater fishing from the docks and charter boats in one of the deepest ports in North America.

12. Shoup Bay (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Halibut)

Shoup Bay, near Valdez

Shoup Bay offers good salmon and halibut fishing near Valdez with stunning glacier views.

13. Columbia Glacier Area (Halibut, Rockfish)

Columbia Glacier, Prince William Sound

The waters near Columbia Glacier offer unique halibut and rockfish fishing in the shadow of the massive tidewater glacier.

14. Montague Island (Halibut, Coho Salmon, Rockfish)

Montague Island, Prince William Sound

Montague Island offers excellent saltwater fishing in the southern Prince William Sound, accessible by boat from Whittier or Valdez.

15. Kayak Island (Halibut, Salmon, Rockfish)

Kayak Island, Gulf of Alaska

Kayak Island offers remote saltwater fishing in the Gulf of Alaska, accessible by boat from Cordova.

16. Copper River Highway Bridges (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Copper River Highway, Cordova

The numerous bridges along the Copper River Highway provide excellent roadside access to salmon and Dolly Varden fishing in the Copper River delta.

17. Alaganik Slough (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Alaganik Slough, Cordova area

Alaganik Slough offers good silver salmon and Dolly Varden fishing in the Copper River delta.

18. McKinley Lake (Rainbow Trout, Dolly Varden, Arctic Char)

McKinley Lake, Cordova area

McKinley Lake offers good trout and char fishing in the Copper River delta area near Cordova.

19. Flemer Lake (Rainbow Trout, Arctic Char)

Flemer Lake, Cordova area

Flemer Lake offers good stocked rainbow trout and Arctic char fishing near Cordova.

20. Power Creek (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Power Creek, Cordova area

Power Creek offers good salmon and Dolly Varden fishing near the Eyak River in Cordova.

21. Hartney Bay (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Hartney Bay, Cordova area

Hartney Bay offers good saltwater and anadromous fishing in the Cordova area of Prince William Sound.

22. Orca Inlet (Halibut, Coho Salmon, Rockfish)

Orca Inlet, Cordova

Orca Inlet offers excellent saltwater fishing near Cordova for halibut, silver salmon, and rockfish.

23. Hawkins Island Cut (Halibut, Coho Salmon, Rockfish)

Hawkins Island, Prince William Sound

Hawkins Island Cut offers good saltwater fishing in eastern Prince William Sound near Cordova.

24. Sheep Bay (Halibut, Coho Salmon, Rockfish)

Sheep Bay, Prince William Sound

Sheep Bay offers good halibut and salmon fishing in the eastern Prince William Sound.

25. Jack Bay (Halibut, Coho Salmon, Rockfish)

Jack Bay, Prince William Sound

Jack Bay offers good saltwater fishing in Prince William Sound near Valdez.

26. Sawmill Bay (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Sawmill Bay, Valdez area

Sawmill Bay offers good salmon and Dolly Varden fishing near Valdez.

27. Dayville Road Area (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Dayville Road, Valdez

The streams along Dayville Road near Valdez offer good salmon and Dolly Varden fishing with road access.

28. Mineral Creek (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Mineral Creek, Valdez

Mineral Creek offers good salmon and Dolly Varden fishing within walking distance of downtown Valdez.

29. Lowe River (Coho Salmon, Pink Salmon, Dolly Varden)

Lowe River, Valdez area

The Lowe River offers good salmon and Dolly Varden fishing near Valdez along the Richardson Highway.

30. Tazlina River (Sockeye Salmon, Coho Salmon, Rainbow Trout)

Tazlina River, Glennallen area

The Tazlina River offers good sockeye and silver salmon fishing in the Copper River basin near Glennallen.

31. Gulkana River (Sockeye Salmon, Rainbow Trout, Arctic Grayling)

Gulkana River, Copper Basin

The Gulkana River is a designated National Wild and Scenic River offering excellent sockeye salmon and rainbow trout fishing.

32. Klutina River (Sockeye Salmon, Coho Salmon, Rainbow Trout)

Klutina River, Copper Basin

The Klutina River offers good sockeye salmon and rainbow trout fishing near Copper Center.

33. Tonsina River (Sockeye Salmon, Coho Salmon, Rainbow Trout)

Tonsina River, Copper Basin

The Tonsina River offers good sockeye salmon and rainbow trout fishing in the Copper River basin.

34. Chitina River (Sockeye Salmon, Chinook Salmon, Arctic Grayling)

Chitina River, Copper Basin

The Chitina River offers good sockeye and king salmon fishing in the upper Copper River basin.

35. Tebay Lake (Arctic Grayling, Lake Trout, Rainbow Trout)

Tebay Lake, Wrangell-St. Elias

Tebay Lake offers Arctic grayling, lake trout, and rainbow trout fishing in the Wrangell-St. Elias National Park area.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at adfg.alaska.gov.
  • Fish the Copper River in May -- The first Copper River salmon of the season arrive in May and command premium prices worldwide.
  • Book a Valdez charter -- Valdez has numerous charter operators offering halibut and salmon fishing in Prince William Sound.
  • Watch for glaciers -- Prince William Sound has numerous tidewater glaciers. Keep a safe distance and watch for calving ice.
  • Bring binoculars -- The Gulf Coast region is excellent for wildlife viewing including sea otters, seals, whales, and puffins.
