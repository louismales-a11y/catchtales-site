Fishing in Southwest Florida & Gulf Coast

Tampa Bay, Sarasota, Fort Myers, Naples, 10,000 Islands, and the incredible fishing of Florida's Gulf Coast.

1. Tampa Bay (Snook, Redfish, Speckled Trout, Tarpon, Grouper)

Tampa Bay, Hillsborough/Pinellas/Manatee counties

Tampa Bay is a world-class estuary offering excellent fishing for snook, redfish, trout, and tarpon.

2. Sarasota Bay (Snook, Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder)

Sarasota Bay, Sarasota County

Sarasota Bay offers excellent inshore fishing in clear, shallow waters.

3. Charlotte Harbor (Snook, Redfish, Tarpon, Speckled Trout, Snapper)

Charlotte Harbor, Charlotte/Lee counties

Charlotte Harbor is one of Florida's most productive estuaries, offering world-class inshore fishing.

4. Fort Myers Beach (Snook, Redfish, Speckled Trout, Spanish Mackerel)

Fort Myers Beach, Lee County

Fort Myers Beach offers excellent surf and pier fishing.

5. Sanibel Island (Snook, Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Shelling)

Sanibel Island, Lee County

Sanibel Island offers excellent fishing on its world-famous shelling beaches.

6. Naples Pier (Snook, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel, Pompano)

Naples, Collier County

The Naples Pier offers excellent fishing in the Gulf of Mexico.

7. 10,000 Islands (Redfish, Snook, Tarpon, Speckled Trout, Mangrove Snapper)

10,000 Islands, Collier/Monroe counties

The 10,000 Islands region offers incredible backcountry fishing in a vast mangrove wilderness.

8. Everglades City (Snook, Redfish, Tarpon, Trout)

Everglades City, Collier County

Everglades City is the gateway to the 10,000 Islands fishing.

9. Chokoloskee Island (Snook, Redfish, Tarpon, Trout)

Chokoloskee, Collier County

Chokoloskee offers excellent backcountry fishing access.

10. Crystal River (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Sheepshead, Manatee Viewing)

Crystal River, Citrus County

Crystal River offers good fishing and is famous for manatee viewing in winter.

11. Homosassa River (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Snook, Tarpon)

Homosassa, Citrus County

The Homosassa River offers excellent inshore fishing with numerous springs.

12. Cedar Key (Redfish, Speckled Trout, Flounder, Sheepshead)

Cedar Key, Levy County

Cedar Key offers excellent fishing in the Big Bend region of Florida's Gulf Coast.

13. Steinhatchee (Speckled Trout, Redfish, Flounder, Scallops)

Steinhatchee, Taylor County

Steinhatchee offers excellent fishing and scalloping in the Big Bend.

14. Withlacoochee River (Largemouth Bass, Bream, Catfish, Crappie)

Withlacoochee River, Citrus/Sumter counties

The Withlacoochee River offers good freshwater fishing.

15. Peace River (Largemouth Bass, Snook, Redfish, Catfish)

Peace River, Charlotte/DeSoto counties

The Peace River offers both freshwater and tidal fishing.

16. Fort Myers Pier (Spanish Mackerel, Snook, Redfish)

Fort Myers, Lee County

Fort Myers Pier offers good fishing.

17. Venice Pier (Snook, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel)

Venice, Sarasota County

Venice Pier offers good fishing.

18. Sarasota Pier (Snook, Redfish, Spanish Mackerel)

Sarasota, Sarasota County

Sarasota Pier offers good fishing.

19. St. Petersburg Pier (Snook, Redfish, Trout)

St. Petersburg, Pinellas County

St. Petersburg Pier offers good fishing.

20. Clearwater Pier (Spanish Mackerel, Snook, Redfish)

Clearwater, Pinellas County

Clearwater Pier offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at myfwc.com.
  • Fish Tampa Bay -- Tampa Bay offers world-class snook and redfish fishing year-round.
  • Try the 10,000 Islands -- The 10,000 Islands offer incredible backcountry fishing in a pristine mangrove wilderness.
  • Summer tarpon -- The Gulf Coast offers excellent tarpon fishing from May through August.
  • Hire a guide -- The Charlotte Harbor and 10,000 Islands areas have excellent fishing guides.
