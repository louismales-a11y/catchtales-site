Fishing in Central Michigan

Houghton Lake, Higgins Lake, Lake St. Helen, Chippewa River, and the walleye, pike, and bass waters of mid-Michigan.

1. Houghton Lake (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Bluegill, Crappie)

Houghton Lake, Roscommon County

Houghton Lake is Michigan's largest inland lake at 22,000 acres, offering exceptional walleye, pike, and panfish fishing with famous ice fishing in winter.

2. Lake St. Helen (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Lake St. Helen, Roscommon County

Lake St. Helen offers excellent walleye and bass fishing in central Michigan.

3. Chippewa River (Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish, Rainbow Trout)

Chippewa River, Isabella/Midland counties

The Chippewa River offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing through central Michigan.

4. Tittabawassee River (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Channel Catfish, Crappie)

Tittabawassee River, Midland County

The Tittabawassee River offers good walleye and smallmouth bass fishing in mid-Michigan.

5. Rifle River (Walleye, Northern Pike, Smallmouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill)

Rifle River, Arenac County

The Rifle River offers good fishing at the Rifle River Recreation Area.

6. Lake Missaukee (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Lake Missaukee, Missaukee County

Lake Missaukee offers good walleye and bass fishing near Lake City.

7. Lake Mitchell (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Yellow Perch)

Lake Mitchell, Wexford County

Lake Mitchell is a 2,500-acre lake offering excellent walleye and bass fishing near Cadillac.

8. Lake Cadillac (Walleye, Northern Pike, Largemouth Bass, Yellow Perch, Crappie, Bluegill)

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at Michigan.gov/DNR.
  • Fish Houghton Lake -- Houghton Lake is Michigan's largest inland lake with outstanding walleye fishing.
  • Try the Chippewa River -- The Chippewa River offers excellent smallmouth bass fishing near Mt. Pleasant.
  • Ice fishing -- Central Michigan lakes are famous for ice fishing, especially Houghton Lake's Tip-Up Town USA in January.
  • Spring and fall -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing in central Michigan.
