Fishing in Central Massachusetts

Webster Lake, Lake Quinsigamond, Comet Pond, and the warm-water and trout fishing of central Mass.

1. Lake Quinsigamond (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Crappie, Yellow Perch, Chain Pickerel)

Lake Quinsigamond, Worcester County

Lake Quinsigamond is a 540-acre lake offering excellent bass and northern pike fishing near Worcester.

2. Webster Lake (Largemouth Bass, Smallmouth Bass, Northern Pike, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch)

Webster Lake, Worcester County

Webster Lake (Lake Chargoggagoggmanchauggagoggchaubunagungamaugg) offers excellent bass and pike fishing.

3. Comet Pond (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Comet Pond, Worcester County

Comet Pond offers good warm-water fishing near Hubbardston.

4. South Pond (Brook Trout, Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

South Pond, Worcester County

South Pond offers good trout fishing.

5. Bush Meadow Pond (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Crappie, Bluegill)

Bush Meadow Pond, Worcester County

Bush Meadow Pond offers good warm-water fishing.

6. Lake Ripple (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch)

Lake Ripple, Worcester County

Lake Ripple offers good fishing near Grafton.

7. Lake Singletary (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Lake Singletary, Worcester County

Lake Singletary offers good fishing near Millbury.

8. Manchaug Pond (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch, White Perch)

Manchaug Pond, Worcester County

Manchaug Pond offers good fishing near Sutton.

9. Whitin Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch, Rainbow Trout)

Whitin Reservoir, Worcester County

Whitin Reservoir offers good fishing near Northbridge.

10. Fisherville Pond (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Crappie)

Fisherville Pond, Worcester County

Fisherville Pond offers good fishing.

11. Lake Lashaway (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch)

Lake Lashaway, Worcester County

Lake Lashaway offers good fishing near East Brookfield.

12. Quaboag Pond (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch, White Perch)

Quaboag Pond, Worcester County

Quaboag Pond offers good fishing near Brookfield.

13. Dean Pond (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Rainbow Trout)

Dean Pond, Worcester County

Dean Pond offers good fishing at Rutland State Park.

14. Whitehall Reservoir (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Whitehall Reservoir, Worcester County

Whitehall Reservoir offers good fishing near Hopkinton.

15. Indian Lake (Worcester) (Largemouth Bass, Chain Pickerel, Yellow Perch, Crappie)

Indian Lake, Worcester

Indian Lake offers good urban fishing in Worcester.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at mass.gov/masswildlife.
  • Fish Webster Lake -- Webster Lake offers excellent bass and pike fishing in the longest-named lake in the US.
  • Try Lake Quinsigamond -- Quinsigamond offers good northern pike and bass fishing near Worcester.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best fishing.
  • Ice fishing -- Central Massachusetts lakes offer good ice fishing for pickerel and perch in winter.
