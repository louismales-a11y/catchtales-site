Fishing in Northern Colorado & Front Range

Horsetooth Reservoir, Carter Lake, Poudre River, Big Thompson, and the major waters of Colorado's Front Range.

1. Horsetooth Reservoir (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Rainbow Trout, Kokanee Salmon, Crappie)

Horsetooth Reservoir, Larimer County

Horsetooth Reservoir is a 1,900-acre reservoir west of Fort Collins offering excellent walleye, smallmouth bass, trout, and kokanee fishing.

2. Carter Lake (Walleye, Rainbow Trout, Kokanee Salmon, Smallmouth Bass)

Carter Lake, Larimer County

Carter Lake offers excellent walleye and trout fishing near Loveland.

3. Cache la Poudre River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Cutthroat Trout)

Cache la Poudre River, Larimer County

The Poudre River is a designated Wild and Scenic River offering excellent rainbow and brown trout fishing through Poudre Canyon.

4. Big Thompson River (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Big Thompson River, Larimer County

The Big Thompson River offers excellent trout fishing from Estes Park through Big Thompson Canyon to Loveland.

5. Estes Park Area Lakes (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout)

Estes Park, Larimer County

Lake Estes and Marys Lake offer excellent trout fishing near the entrance to Rocky Mountain National Park.

6. Rocky Mountain National Park (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Brook Trout, Greenback Cutthroat)

Rocky Mountain National Park, Larimer/Grand counties

The park offers exceptional trout fishing in its streams and alpine lakes. A park fishing permit is required.

7. Boyd Lake (Walleye, Crappie, Largemouth Bass, Channel Catfish)

Boyd Lake, Larimer County

Boyd Lake offers good warm-water fishing for walleye, crappie, and bass near Loveland.

8. Lonetree Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Lonetree Reservoir, Larimer County

Lonetree Reservoir offers good trout fishing in the Horsetooth area.

9. Pinewood Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Pinewood Lake, Larimer County

Pinewood Lake offers good trout fishing near Loveland.

10. Flatiron Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Flatiron Reservoir, Larimer County

Flatiron Reservoir offers good trout fishing.

11. Saint Vrain Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Saint Vrain Creek, Boulder County

Saint Vrain Creek offers good trout fishing in Boulder County.

12. Boulder Creek (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Boulder Creek, Boulder County

Boulder Creek offers excellent trout fishing through Boulder Canyon and within the city of Boulder.

13. Gross Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout, Kokanee)

Gross Reservoir, Boulder County

Gross Reservoir offers good trout and kokanee fishing in the Boulder foothills.

14. Barker Meadow Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Barker Meadow Reservoir, Boulder County

Barker Meadow offers good trout fishing near Nederland.

15. Georgetown Lake (Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Georgetown Lake, Clear Creek County

Georgetown Lake offers good trout fishing at 10,000 feet near the historic town of Georgetown.

16. Carter Lake (Walleye, Rainbow Trout, Kokanee)

Carter Lake, Larimer County

Carter Lake offers excellent walleye and trout fishing.

17. Boyd Lake (Walleye, Crappie, Largemouth Bass)

Boyd Lake, Larimer County

Boyd Lake offers good warm-water fishing.

18. Horsetooth Reservoir (Walleye, Smallmouth Bass, Kokanee)

Horsetooth Reservoir, Larimer County

Horsetooth offers excellent fishing near Fort Collins.

19. Lon Hagler Reservoir (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Lon Hagler Reservoir, Larimer County

Lon Hagler offers good fishing.

20. Lake Loveland (Rainbow Trout, Largemouth Bass)

Lake Loveland, Larimer County

Lake Loveland offers good urban fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at cpw.state.co.us. Non-resident options available.
  • Fish Horsetooth for walleye -- Horsetooth Reservoir is one of the best walleye fisheries in Colorado. Spring and fall are peak.
  • Try the Poudre River -- The Cache la Poudre River offers excellent fly fishing through Poudre Canyon.
  • Rocky Mountain National Park -- The park offers incredible alpine lake fishing but requires a park permit.
  • Spring and fall are best -- April-June and September-October offer the best Front Range fishing.
