Fishing on Maui & Lanai

Lahaina, Kihei, Hana, Molokini, Lanai, and the best fishing in the Valley Isle.

1. Lahaina Harbor (Yellowfin Tuna, Mahi-Mahi, Ono, Blue Marlin)

Lahaina, west Maui

Lahaina Harbor is the center of Maui's charter fishing fleet, offering world-class offshore fishing for ahi, mahi-mahi, ono, and blue marlin.

2. Ma'alaea Harbor (Ahi, Mahi-Mahi, Ono, Marlin)

Ma'alaea, central Maui

Ma'alaea Harbor offers charter fishing access to the waters between Maui and Lanai.

3. Molokini Crater (Reef Fish, Tropical Fish, Ono)

Molokini Crater, off Maui

Molokini is a marine sanctuary offering excellent snorkeling and some of the best diving in Hawaii. Fishing is restricted in the crater but productive in surrounding waters.

4. Kihei Coast (Papio, Bonefish, Trevally, Goatfish)

Kihei, south Maui

The Kihei coastline offers good shore fishing for papio and bonefish along the sunny south Maui coast.

5. Hana Coast (Ulua, Papio, Bonefish, Trevally)

Hana, east Maui

The remote Hana coastline offers excellent shore fishing for ulua and papio in a stunning tropical setting.

6. Makena Coast (Papio, Bonefish, Trevally)

Makena, south Maui

Makena offers good shore fishing from the rocky coastline near Wailea.

7. Ka'anapali Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Ka'anapali, west Maui

Ka'anapali Beach offers good shore fishing from the famous resort beach.

8. Lanai (Bonefish, Papio, Ulua, Trevally, Mahi-Mahi)

Lanai, across the Auau Channel

Lanai offers excellent fishing with less pressure. The waters around Lanai are productive for bonefish, papio, and offshore species.

9. Manele Bay (Bonefish, Papio, Trevally, Reef Fish)

Manele Bay, Lanai

Manele Bay offers good inshore fishing near the Four Seasons resort on Lanai.

10. Hulopo'e Bay (Bonefish, Papio, Goatfish)

Hulopo'e Bay, Lanai

Hulopo'e Bay offers excellent snorkeling and good shore fishing in a protected marine sanctuary.

11. Auau Channel (Marlin, Ahi, Mahi-Mahi, Ono)

Auau Channel, between Maui and Lanai

The Auau Channel is a productive offshore fishing area between Maui and Lanai, known for marlin and tuna.

12. Palaoa Point (Papio, Ulua, Trevally)

Palaoa Point, Lanai

Palaoa Point offers good shore fishing on the west coast of Lanai.

13. Kaunolū Village (Ulua, Papio, Trevally)

Kaunolū, Lanai

Kaunolū offers remote shore fishing at an ancient Hawaiian fishing village site.

14. McGregor Point (Ahi, Mahi-Mahi, Ono)

McGregor Point, Ma'alaea

McGregor Point offers good shore fishing near Ma'alaea Harbor.

15. Keawakapu Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Keawakapu Beach, Kihei

Keawakapu Beach offers good inshore fishing between Kihei and Wailea.

16. McGregor Point (Ahi, Mahi-Mahi, Ono)

McGregor Point, Maui

McGregor Point offers good shore fishing.

17. Ukumehame Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Trevally)

Ukumehame, west Maui

Ukumehame Beach offers good fishing.

18. Olowalu Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Olowalu, west Maui

Olowalu offers good shore fishing.

19. Polo Beach (Papio, Bonefish)

Polo Beach, Wailea

Polo Beach offers good shore fishing.

20. Kamaole Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Kamaole, Kihei

Kamaole Beach offers good fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- No recreational saltwater licence required.
  • Book a Lahaina charter -- Lahaina Harbor has a large charter fleet targeting marlin, ahi, and mahi-mahi.
  • Try Lanai -- Lanai offers excellent fishing with far fewer anglers than Maui.
  • Best months -- Summer offers the best offshore fishing, but Maui has year-round fishing.
  • Shore fishing -- Maui's rocky shores and beaches offer good papio and bonefish fishing without a boat.
