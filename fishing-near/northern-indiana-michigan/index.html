Fishing in Northern Indiana & Lake Michigan

Lake Michigan, Indiana Dunes, Michigan City, Portage, and the salmon and trout fishing of Indiana's Lake Michigan shoreline.

1. Lake Michigan (Indiana Shore) (Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Rainbow Trout, Lake Trout, Yellow Perch)

Lake Michigan, Lake/LaPorte counties

Lake Michigan along Indiana's 45-mile shoreline offers world-class salmon and trout fishing, with charter boats operating from Michigan City and Portage.

2. Michigan City Harbor (Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Steelhead, Brown Trout, Perch)

Michigan City, LaPorte County

Michigan City Harbor is the primary Lake Michigan charter fishing port in Indiana, offering excellent salmon and trout fishing.

3. Portage Lakefront (Coho Salmon, Chinook Salmon, Steelhead, Perch, Smallmouth Bass)

Portage, Porter County

The Portage Lakefront and Indiana Dunes area offers excellent shore and boat fishing on Lake Michigan.

4. Indiana Dunes State Park (Chinook Salmon, Coho Salmon, Steelhead, Yellow Perch)

Indiana Dunes, Porter County

Indiana Dunes State Park offers good Lake Michigan shore fishing from its beaches and the pier.

5. Trail Creek (Steelhead, Coho Salmon, Rainbow Trout, Brown Trout)

Trail Creek, LaPorte County

Trail Creek offers excellent steelhead and salmon fishing flowing into Lake Michigan at Michigan City.

6. Little Calumet River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Catfish)

Little Calumet River, Porter/LaPorte counties

The Little Calumet River offers good warm-water fishing in northwest Indiana.

7. Kankakee River (Smallmouth Bass, Largemouth Bass, Northern Pike, Walleye, Catfish)

Kankakee River, LaPorte/Starke counties

The Kankakee River is one of Indiana's premier fishing rivers, known for excellent smallmouth bass and northern pike fishing.

8. Bass Lake (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Northern Pike, Walleye)

Bass Lake, Starke County

Bass Lake is a 1,260-acre natural lake offering excellent bass, crappie, and pike fishing.

9. Lake of the Woods (Largemouth Bass, Crappie, Bluegill, Northern Pike)

Lake of the Woods offers good fishing near LaPorte.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Buy online at in.gov/dnr/fish. Non-resident options available.
  • Fish Lake Michigan in spring -- April through June offers the best salmon and trout fishing along Indiana's shoreline.
  • Try the Kankakee River -- The Kankakee is one of Indiana's best smallmouth bass rivers.
  • Pier fishing -- Michigan City and Indiana Dunes piers offer good fishing without a boat.
  • Winter perch -- Yellow perch fishing from Lake Michigan piers is excellent in fall and winter.
