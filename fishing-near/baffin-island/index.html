Fishing on Baffin Island & Qikiqtaaluk

Iqaluit area, Pond Inlet, Soper River, Nettilling Lake, Amadjuak Lake, and the vast Arctic char waters of eastern Nunavut.

1. Soper River (Arctic Char)

Katannilik Park, Baffin Island

The Soper River in Katannilik Territorial Park offers spectacular Arctic char fishing in a unique Arctic oasis. The sheltered river valley supports lush vegetation. Char average 4-8 pounds. Accessible from Kimmirut or Iqaluit for multi-day canoe trips.

2. Koukdjuak River (Arctic Char)

Great Plain of the Koukdjuak, Baffin Island

The Koukdjuak River offers excellent Arctic char fishing in a remote Arctic setting near Qikiqtarjuaq. Large, powerful char in the 5-12 pound range. Fly-in lodges provide access. Strictly catch-and-release.

3. Nettilling Lake (Arctic Char, Lake Trout)

Baffin Island, south of Koukdjuak

Nettilling Lake is the largest lake on Baffin Island and one of the largest in Canada. It offers Arctic char and lake trout fishing in a vast, remote lake system. The lake connects to the sea via the Koukdjuak River.

4. Amadjuak Lake (Arctic Char, Lake Trout)

Baffin Island, north of Nettilling

Amadjuak Lake is the second-largest lake on Baffin Island, offering Arctic char and lake trout fishing in a pristine Arctic setting. Accessible by float plane from Iqaluit.

5. Iqaluit Area Lakes (Arctic Char, Lake Trout)

Iqaluit area

The lakes near Iqaluit offer Arctic char and lake trout fishing with easy access from Nunavut's capital. The Sylvia Grinnell River area provides excellent char fishing near the city.

6. Sylvia Grinnell River (Arctic Char)

Iqaluit

The Sylvia Grinnell River flows through Iqaluit and offers Arctic char fishing within the capital city. Char run up this river from Frobisher Bay in July and August, providing urban Arctic fishing.

7. Pond Inlet Area (Arctic Char, Arctic Grayling)

Pond Inlet, northern Baffin

The Pond Inlet area offers Arctic char and Arctic grayling fishing in the rivers and lakes of northern Baffin Island. The community provides access to excellent fishing with spectacular views of the Bylot Island ice cap.

8. Eclipse Sound (Arctic Char)

Eclipse Sound, near Pond Inlet

Eclipse Sound offers Arctic char fishing from the tidal waters near Pond Inlet. The sound's nutrient-rich waters support healthy char populations and provide stunning Arctic scenery.

9. Pangnirtung Area (Arctic Char, Arctic Grayling)

Pangnirtung, Cumberland Sound

The Pangnirtung area offers Arctic char and Arctic grayling fishing in the rivers and lakes around Cumberland Sound. The community is a gateway to Auyuittuq National Park.

10. Duval River (Arctic Char)

Duval River area, Baffin Island

The Duval River offers Arctic char fishing near Pangnirtung on Baffin Island. A productive river with good char runs in the Cumberland Sound region.

11. Kimmirut Area (Arctic Char)

Kimmirut (Lake Harbour), southern Baffin

The Kimmirut area offers Arctic char fishing in the rivers and lakes of southern Baffin Island. The community is the gateway to the Soper River and Katannilik Park.

12. Iqalugaarjuk River (Arctic Char)

Iqalugaarjuk area, near Kimmirut

The Iqalugaarjuk River offers Arctic char fishing near Kimmirut on southern Baffin Island. A productive tundra river with good char runs.

13. Hall Beach Area (Arctic Char)

Hall Beach, Foxe Basin

The Hall Beach area offers Arctic char fishing in the rivers flowing into Foxe Basin on the west side of Baffin Island.

14. Igloolik Area (Arctic Char)

Igloolik, Foxe Basin

The Igloolik area offers Arctic char fishing in the waters around this historic Inuit community on an island in Foxe Basin.

15. Clyde River Area (Arctic Char, Arctic Grayling)

Clyde River, eastern Baffin

The Clyde River area offers Arctic char and Arctic grayling fishing on the east coast of Baffin Island. The community sits at the base of the spectacular Patoot Mountains.

16. Qikiqtarjuaq Area (Arctic Char)

Qikiqtarjuaq, Baffin coast

The Qikiqtarjuaq area offers Arctic char fishing near the Koukdjuak River system. The community provides access to some of Baffin Island's best char fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- Contact the Department of Environment in Iqaluit. Non-resident licences are available online.
  • Fish the Soper River -- The Soper River in Katannilik Park offers excellent Arctic char fishing combined with spectacular Arctic scenery.
  • Try urban Iqaluit -- The Sylvia Grinnell River in Iqaluit offers accessible Arctic char fishing within Nunavut's capital city.
  • Hire a Baffin guide -- Local Inuit guides are essential for navigating Baffin Island's remote waters and understanding local conditions.
  • Prepare for polar bears -- Baffin Island has a healthy polar bear population. Carry appropriate safety equipment and travel with experienced guides.
