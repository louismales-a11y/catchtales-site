Fishing on Kauai

Na Pali Coast, Hanalei, Poipu, Lihue, and the Garden Isle's world-class fishing.

1. Nawiliwili Harbor (Yellowfin Tuna, Mahi-Mahi, Ono, Blue Marlin)

Nawiliwili, Lihue

Nawiliwili Harbor is Kauai's main charter fishing port, offering excellent offshore fishing for marlin, ahi, and mahi-mahi.

2. Na Pali Coast (Ulua, Papio, Trevally, Mahi-Mahi)

Na Pali Coast, north Kauai

The Na Pali Coast offers spectacular fishing from the rugged coastline and offshore waters, accessible by boat only.

3. Hanalei Bay (Bonefish, Papio, Mullet, Trevally)

Hanalei, north Kauai

Hanalei Bay offers good inshore fishing for bonefish and papio in a stunning bay with taro fields.

4. Poipu Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish, Trevally)

Poipu, south Kauai

Poipu offers good shore fishing from the sunny south shore beaches.

5. Port Allen (Ahi, Mahi-Mahi, Ono, Marlin)

Port Allen, south Kauai

Port Allen offers charter fishing access to the south and west coasts of Kauai.

6. Kilauea Point (Ulua, Papio, Trevally)

Kilauea, north Kauai

The Kilauea Point area offers good shore fishing near the historic lighthouse and wildlife refuge.

7. Anahola Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Mullet)

Anahola, east Kauai

Anahola Beach offers good shore fishing on the east side of Kauai.

8. Kapaa Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Kapaa, east Kauai

Kapaa offers good shore fishing from the east coast beaches and the Ke Ala Hele Makalae path.

9. Waimea Bay (Papio, Ulua, Trevally)

Waimea, west Kauai

Waimea Bay offers good shore fishing on the west side of Kauai.

10. Polihale Beach (Ulua, Papio, Trevally, Giant Trevally)

Polihale, west Kauai

Polihale State Park offers excellent shore fishing for ulua on a remote western beach with views of Niihau.

11. Kekaha Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Mullet)

Kekaha, west Kauai

Kekaha Beach offers good shore fishing on the dry west side.

12. Maha'ulepu Coast (Ulua, Papio, Trevally)

Maha'ulepu, south Kauai

The Maha'ulepu Coast offers good shore fishing along a scenic rocky coastline near Poipu.

13. Kalalau Valley Coast (Ulua, Trevally, Papio)

Kalalau, Na Pali Coast

The remote Kalalau coastline offers spectacular fishing accessible only by hiking the Na Pali Trail.

14. Niihau (Offshore) (Blue Marlin, Ahi, Mahi-Mahi, Ono)

Niihau, off Kauai's west coast

The waters around Niihau, the "Forbidden Island," offer world-class offshore fishing with very little fishing pressure.

15. Kauai Channel (Marlin, Ahi, Mahi-Mahi, Ono)

Kauai Channel, between Kauai and Oahu

The Kauai Channel is a productive offshore fishing area known for marlin and tuna.

16. Lydgate Park Pond (Papio, Mullet, Goatfish)

Lydgate, east Kauai

Lydgate Park offers protected pond fishing.

17. Salt Pond Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Goatfish)

Salt Pond, west Kauai

Salt Pond Beach offers good fishing.

18. Kalapaki Beach (Papio, Bonefish)

Kalapaki, east Kauai

Kalapaki Beach offers good shore fishing.

19. Anini Beach (Papio, Bonefish, Trevally)

Anini, north Kauai

Anini Beach offers good protected fishing.

20. Kilauea Lighthouse (Ulua, Papio, Trevally)

Kilauea, north Kauai

The Kilauea area offers good shore fishing.

Fishing Tips

  • Get a licence -- No recreational saltwater licence required.
  • Book a Nawiliwili charter -- Kauai's charter fleet operates from Nawiliwili and Port Allen.
  • Try shore fishing -- Kauai's beaches and rocky shores offer excellent ulua and papio fishing.
  • Best months -- Summer offers the best offshore conditions, but fishing is productive year-round.
  • Respect the Na Pali -- The Na Pali Coast is beautiful but dangerous. Fish with experienced guides.
