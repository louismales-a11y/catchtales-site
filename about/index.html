About CatchTales

Your hands-free fishing companion for North American waters.

The App

CatchTales is a fishing companion app designed for anglers who want to focus on fishing, not typing. Track catches by voice, snap selfies with your fish, and never lose a fishing memory.

With AI-powered fish identification, smart tackle recommendations, and cloud sync, CatchTales helps you fish smarter and brag bigger.

AI Features

Powered by Google Gemini AI, CatchTales can identify fish from photos, answer questions about your catch data, recommend the best lures for your conditions, and understand natural language voice commands.

Coverage

Canada & United States — freshwater and saltwater. 300+ fish species in the built-in identification guide, with nautical charts and depth data for thousands of lakes and waterways.

Built With

Flutter & Dart • SQLite • Firebase • OpenStreetMap + OpenSeaMap • OpenWeatherMap • Google Gemini AI • Google Places

Designed By

Maison Louis Design — a small design studio passionate about building tools that make outdoor adventures better.
