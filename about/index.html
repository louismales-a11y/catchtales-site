About Best Fish Buddy

The story behind the app — built by anglers, for anglers.

Our Mission

Best Fish Buddy was created to give every angler — from weekend casters to tournament veterans — a powerful, easy-to-use tool to track their fishing adventures, learn from every trip, and connect with a community that shares their passion.

Fishing isn't just about the catch. It's about the early mornings, the quiet water, the stories shared, and the thrill of the unknown. Best Fish Buddy helps you capture all of it.

What Makes Us Different

Most fishing apps are either too simple (just a notebook) or too complex (designed for biologists). Best Fish Buddy hits the sweet spot — powerful enough for serious anglers but intuitive enough that you'll start logging your first catch in under a minute.

We combine catch logging, fish identification (305 species), interactive mapping with nautical charts and weather radar, solunar predictions, tackle tracking, voice controls, a Brag Board for sharing catches, Fishing Trips, and a unique dual skin system (Dream underwater mode or Classic themes) — all in one beautifully designed app.

Built for Canada & the United States

From the Great Lakes to the Gulf Coast, from BC's salmon rivers to Florida's bass lakes — Best Fish Buddy is built for North American waters. Our 305-species field guide covers freshwater fish across both countries, and our map data includes Canadian and US waters with detailed nautical charts.

Free vs Pro

We believe everyone should have access to great fishing tools. The Free version gives you catch logging, fish ID, weather, solunar times, tackle box, voice controls, and more — with reasonable limits. The Pro version unlocks the full experience: unlimited catches, interactive maps with all overlays, offline regions, community stats, cloud sync, achievements, and everything else we've built.

Pro is a one-time purchase — no subscriptions, no recurring fees. Unlock everything forever.
