The all-in-one fishing companion for Canadian and US waters. Log your catches, explore interactive maps with nautical charts & weather radar, identify 305 species, and get solunar predictions — all in one beautifully designed app.

Your Ultimate Fishing Companion

Everything you need to log, explore, and improve every trip — from your first cast to your personal best.

🎣

Log & Track

Record every catch with species, weight, length, GPS location, weather, and photos. Voice logging lets you go hands-free.

🌊

Two App Skins

Choose between Dream — an underwater background with animated fish silhouettes in dark mode — or Classic — a clean interface with 5 color themes and light/dark toggle.

🗺️

Interactive Maps

Nautical charts, depth contours, weather radar, and catch pins — all on one map. Explore new waters with confidence.

Fish ID Guide

Built for Anglers, by Anglers

Every feature is designed to make your time on the water more productive and more enjoyable.

🎣

Catch Logging & Trip Management

Record every detail — species, weight, length, GPS, weather, photos. Group catches into Fishing Trips with per-trip stats — total catches, species breakdown, biggest fish, and date range. Voice controls mean you never have to put down your rod.

🐟

Fish ID Field Guide

305 freshwater species at your fingertips. Each entry includes photos, descriptions, size ranges, habitat, diet, and expert fishing tips. Filter by water type or region.

🗺️

Interactive Map with Tools

See your catches plotted on the map with species pins. Toggle nautical charts, depth contours, and weather radar — all in a collapsible side panel.

🌤️

Weather & Solunar Predictions

Real-time conditions, 5-day forecast, and solunar-based predictions. Know the best fishing periods, moon phases, wind, and marine conditions before you go. Get push notifications for severe weather alerts and daily solunar best times.

🎤

Hands-Free Voice Controls

"Fish buddy jason caught a pike" — voice fills in your catch forms. Say "photo", "weighs 5 lb", "length 20 inches", "save". No typing required. Also includes a Device Setup guide to get your phone mounted and ready.

🏆

Brag Board Pro

Share your catch photos with the community! Like and comment on other anglers' catches. Turn every fishing story into a friendly competition.

☁️

Cloud Sync Pro

Back up your data and sync across devices. Fish Together mode lets you share catches and chat with friends in real time during a session.
