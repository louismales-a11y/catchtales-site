Everything You Need for a Great Day on the Water

From logging your first catch to exploring community lake stats — Best Fish Buddy grows with you.

🎣

Log Your Catches

Record every catch with species, weight, length, GPS location, weather, and photos. Voice logging lets you go hands-free — just say "record"!

🎣

Trip Management

Group catches into fishing trips with stats per trip — total catches, species breakdown, biggest fish, and date range. Auto-tag new catches to the active trip.

🌊

Water Conditions

Log water clarity and flow rate with every catch. Track what conditions produce the best results over time to refine your strategy.

🐟

Fish ID Field Guide

Identify 305 freshwater species with photos, descriptions, size ranges, habitat info, diet, and fishing tips. Filter by water type or browse by region.

🗺️

Interactive Map Tools

See catches plotted on a map with species pins. Access nautical charts, depth contours, weather radar, GPS locate, and saved spots from a collapsible side panel.

🚢

Nautical Charts & Depth

OpenSeaMap overlays show water depths, shipwrecks, buoys, and navigation aids. Toggle depth contour lines to find drop-offs and underwater structure.

☁️

Weather Radar Overlay

See live weather on the map — cloud cover, precipitation radar, and temperature overlays. Plan around incoming storms or find the clearest skies.

📥

Offline Region Selector

Select any area on the map to cache for offline use. Base maps plus active overlays (nautical, depth, weather) are downloaded for your selected region.

⚖️

Crowdsourced Depth Logging

Long-press on the map to log a depth reading from your fish finder. Saved with exact GPS coordinates — every submission helps build a free community depth database.

🌤️

Weather & Best Times

Real-time weather, 5-day forecast, and solunar-based predictions showing best fishing periods, moon phases, wind, and marine conditions. Severe weather alerts via push notification.

🎤

Voice Controls

Hands-free operation — "fish buddy jason caught a pike" tallies fish per angler. Voice fills in catch forms: "photo", "weighs 5 lb", "length 20 inches", "save".

🧰

Tackle Box

Track lures, hooks, lines, and baits. Browse 30+ common lure types with photos and target species. Get weather-aware tackle recommendations for your target fish.

📊

Stats & Achievements

Detailed charts of your fishing activity, personal records, and species breakdown. Earn badges: First Catch, Master Angler, Species Collector, Big Catch, and more.

📅

Calendar & Gallery

View your catch history on a calendar heatmap. Browse all catch photos in a gallery view to relive your best days on the water.

👥

Community Stats Pro

Search any lake in Canada and the US to see what fellow anglers are catching. See top lures, best weather conditions, and peak months — powered by Google Places.

☁️

Cloud Sync Pro

Back up your catches to the cloud and sync across devices. Fish Together mode lets you share catches and chat with friends in real time during a session.
