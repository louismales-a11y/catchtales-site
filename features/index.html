Features

Everything Best Fish Buddy offers, from catch logging to community insights — designed for anglers who love the water.

🎣 Catch Logging

Record every catch with species, weight, length, GPS location, weather conditions, and photos. Our voice logging lets you go hands-free — just say "record" and dictate the details. Each entry is timestamped and plotted on your personal catch map.

🎣 Trip Management

Group your catches into fishing trips with custom names. View detailed stats per trip — total catches, species breakdown, biggest fish, date range, and more. New catches are auto-tagged with the active trip so you never lose track. Rename or remove trips anytime.

🌊 Water Conditions

Log water clarity (clear, stained, muddy) and flow rate (slow, moderate, fast) with every catch. Over time, you'll be able to analyze which conditions produce the best results for each species — invaluable pattern recognition for your fishing log.

🐟 Fish ID Field Guide

Our comprehensive field guide covers 305 freshwater species found across Canada and the United States. Each species entry includes high-quality photos, detailed descriptions, typical size ranges, habitat preferences, diet, and expert fishing tips. Filter by water type or browse by region to quickly identify your catch.

🗺️ Interactive Map

See all your catches plotted on a full-featured interactive map with color-coded species pins. A collapsible side panel gives you quick access to nautical charts, depth contours, weather radar, GPS locate, and your saved spots. Long-press anywhere on the map to save a spot or log a depth reading.

🚢 Nautical Charts & Depth Overlays

OpenSeaMap overlays show water depths, shipwrecks, buoys, and navigation aids directly on the map. Toggle depth contour lines to find drop-offs, deep holes, and underwater structure where fish hold. Essential for serious anglers exploring new waters.

☁️ Weather Radar Overlay

See live weather conditions right on the map. Toggle between cloud cover, precipitation radar, and temperature overlays. Plan around incoming storms or find the clearest skies for your trip — all without leaving the app.

📥 Offline Region Selector

Tap two corners on the map to select exactly which area to cache for offline use. Name your region and the app downloads base maps plus active overlays (nautical, depth, weather) for offline access. Manage your downloaded regions individually.

⚖️ Crowdsourced Depth Logging

Long-press on the map to log a depth reading from your fish finder (or an estimate). Each submission is saved with exact GPS coordinates. Toggle depth markers on the map to see all logged depths from the community. Every contribution helps build a free, shared depth database.

🌤️ Weather & Best Fishing Times

Get real-time weather conditions and a 5-day forecast right in the app. Solunar-based predictions show you the best fishing periods, moon phases, wind conditions, and marine data for your location. Enable push notifications for severe weather alerts and daily solunar best fishing times.

🎤 Voice Controls

Completely hands-free operation. Say "fish buddy jason caught a pike" to tally fish per angler. Voice fills in catch forms: say "photo" to snap a picture, "weighs 5 lb", "length 20 inches", and "save" to log it all. Perfect when your hands are busy with a rod.

🧰 Tackle Box

Track your lures, hooks, lines, and baits in a digital tackle box. Browse 30+ common lure types with photos and target species information. Get weather-aware tackle recommendations based on your target fish and current conditions.

📊 Stats & Achievements

Detailed charts of your fishing activity over time, personal records, and species breakdown. Earn achievement badges — First Catch, Master Angler, Species Collector, Big Catch, and more — as you progress. Compare your stats across seasons and locations.

📅 Calendar & Gallery

View your catch history on a calendar heatmap where days with catches are highlighted. Browse all catch photos in a gallery view to relive your best moments on the water. Tap any entry for full details.

👥 Community Stats Pro

Search any lake in Canada and the United States to see what fellow anglers are catching. Discover top-performing lures, best weather conditions, and peak months for each location — powered by Google Places integration. Plan your next trip with data from the community.

☁️ Cloud Sync Pro

Back up your catches securely to the cloud and sync across all your devices. Fish Together mode lets you share catches and chat with friends in real time during a shared fishing session. Never lose a record again.

🔔 Push Notifications

Get notified for severe weather alerts (storms, high winds), daily solunar best fishing times, and Fish Together session activity. Notifications are permission-based and requested contextually — not on first launch — so you're never bothered unnecessarily.

🌍 Multi-Language Support

Available in English, French, Spanish, German, and Ukrainian. Full translation of all features, help content, and the introductory walkthrough. Switch languages anytime from the settings.
