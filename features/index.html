All Features

Everything you need to fish smarter and brag bigger.

Your Catch, Your Story

From AI-powered fish identification to interactive nautical charts — CatchTales packs every tool you need into one beautiful app. Log catches by voice, explore maps with weather overlays, and share your brags with the community.

AI-Powered

AI Fish ID AI

Snap a photo and AI identifies the species instantly. No more guessing.

AI Lake Insights AI

Chat with your personal fishing data. Ask questions like "What's my most productive lure for walleye?" or "Which lake has the best bass fishing in June?" — AI instantly analyzes your catch history, weather patterns, and seasonal trends to give you actionable answers. The more you log, the smarter it gets.

AI Tackle Picks AI

Smart lure suggestions based on species, weather, season, and water type.

Catch Logging

Voice Recording

Log catches hands-free. Say "caught a 5lb bass" and the form fills itself.

Selfie Camera

Built-in camera with countdown timer. Perfect catch photos every time.

Calendar & History

Calendar heatmap, gallery view, search, and export to CSV / JSON / KML.

Maps & Weather

Interactive Map

Catch pins, nautical charts, depth contours, and weather overlays — all in one map.

Weather & Forecast

Real-time conditions, 5-day forecast, auto-fetched from your GPS location.

Solunar Times

Daily major/minor fishing periods, moon phase, and a 0-10 rating. Know when they bite.

Community & Cloud

Brag Board

Share catch photos, like, comment, and show off your biggest fish. Friendly community.

Cloud Sync

Back up, sync across devices, and fish together with friends in real-time sessions with photo sharing, permanent fishing rooms, and notification sounds.

Stats & Badges

Track your progress with detailed charts showing your catch activity over time, species breakdown by percentage, personal bests by weight and length, and seasonal patterns. Earn achievement badges as you hit milestones — First Catch, Master Angler, Species Collector, Big Catch, and more. Share your stats card as an image to show off on social media.

Tools

Fish Together

Real-time chat with friends while fishing. Share photos, GPS location, and emergency alerts. Open as a separate window to switch between apps freely.
