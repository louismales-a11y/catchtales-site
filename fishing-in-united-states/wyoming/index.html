Fishing in Wyoming

Click a region to explore top fishing spots, species, and local tips.

Wyoming offers world-class trout fishing in spectacular Rocky Mountain settings. Yellowstone and Grand Teton National Parks feature famous waters like the Madison, Snake, and Yellowstone Rivers — home to native cutthroat trout. The North Platte River below Grey Reef is a blue-ribbon trout stream, and the Bighorn Mountains offer hundreds of alpine lakes. Flaming Gorge Reservoir provides trophy lake trout and smallmouth bass.
