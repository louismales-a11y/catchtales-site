Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Best Fish Buddy.

Best Fish Buddy is a fishing companion app for Android that helps you log catches, identify fish species, explore interactive maps with nautical charts and weather radar, track your tackle, and much more. It features a dual skin system — Dream (immersive underwater background with animated fish) and Classic (clean interface with 5 color themes) — so you can customize the look and feel. Designed for anglers across Canada and the United States.

There's a fully functional Free version that includes catch logging, fish ID, weather, solunar predictions, voice controls, both Dream and Classic skins, and more — with some limits (10 catches, first 10 species in the field guide, 10 tackle items). The Pro version is a one-time purchase that unlocks everything with no limits.

You can download the Free version directly from bestfishbuddy.com/free on your Android phone. The APK file will install the app. Make sure you've enabled "Install from unknown sources" in your phone settings. The Pro version can be purchased by emailing us at BestfishBuddy@gmail.com.

Dream Skin creates an immersive fishing atmosphere with an underwater background and animated fish silhouettes (Pike, Perch, Walleye, Bass, Sunfish) swimming across your screen. Dark mode is locked for the full effect. Classic Skin provides a clean, professional interface with 5 color themes (Ocean Blue, Forest Green, Sunset Orange, Midnight, Lakeside) and a light/dark toggle. You can switch between them anytime from the 3-dot menu — no restart needed.

The field guide includes 305 freshwater species found across Canada and the United States. Each species has photos, descriptions, size ranges, habitat information, diet, and fishing tips. Free users can access the first 10 species; Pro users get full access.
