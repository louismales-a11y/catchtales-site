Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CatchTales.

CatchTales works across all of Canada and the United States — not just one region. Our 305-species fish database covers freshwater species found in both countries, and our interactive maps include Canadian and US waters with nautical charts and depth data. Whether you fish in Ontario, Florida, British Columbia, Texas, Quebec, or anywhere in between, CatchTales has you covered.

The app uses your GPS location to show local weather, nearby species, and community stats for your area — but the app itself is built for all of North America. You can use it anywhere in Canada or the USA.

CatchTales is a fishing companion app for Android that helps you log catches, identify 305 freshwater species from across Canada and the United States, explore interactive maps with nautical charts and weather radar, track your tackle, and much more. It features a dual skin system — Dream (immersive underwater background with animated fish) and Classic (clean interface with 5 color themes) — so you can customize the look and feel. Designed for anglers across all of Canada and the United States.

Classic Skin provides a clean, professional interface with 5 color themes (Ocean Blue, Forest Green, Sunset Orange, Midnight, Lakeside) and a light/dark toggle.
